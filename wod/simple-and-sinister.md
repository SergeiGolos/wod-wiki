# Simple and Sinister

**Category**: StrongFirst  
**Type**: Strength Conditioning  
**Difficulty**: Intermediate  

## Description
The classic StrongFirst program focusing on kettlebell skill development and conditioning.

```wod
5:00 100 KB Swings 70lb
1:00 Rest
10:00 10 Turkish Getups 70lb
```

## Breakdown
- **Part 1**: 100 kettlebell swings in 5 minutes (70lb/32kg)
- **Rest**: 1 minute
- **Part 2**: 10 Turkish getups in 10 minutes (70lb/32kg)
- **Goal**: Complete with perfect form

## Tips
- Focus on perfect swing technique (hip hinge, not squat)
- Rest as needed during getups - 10 minutes is generous
- Quality over quantity - form is paramount

## Scaling Options
- **Beginner**: 16kg swings, 12kg getups
- **Intermediate**: 24kg swings, 16kg getups
- **Advanced**: 32kg swings, 24kg getups

## StrongFirst Principles
- Practice before training
- Tension and relaxation techniques
- Power breathing
- Grind the getups, ballistically swing

## Technical Notes
- Hardstyle swings: lock out knees and hips at top
- Getups: slow and controlled, perfect movement pattern
- Rest as needed during getup portion
