# KB Axe Lite

**Category**: StrongFirst  
**Type**: Interval Training  
**Difficulty**: Intermediate  

## Description
A kettlebell interval workout focusing on technique and conditioning with moderate weight.

## WOD Wiki Syntax
```
(20) 1:00 
  6 KB Swings 70lb
```

## Breakdown
- **Duration**: 20 minutes
- **Format**: EMOM (Every Minute On the Minute)
- **Exercise**: 6 kettlebell swings per minute (70lb/32kg)
- **Rest**: Remaining time in each minute after completing swings

## Tips
- Focus on perfect swing technique
- Use the rest time to reset form
- Maintain consistent power output

## Scaling Options
- **Beginner**: 12kg-16kg, 5-6 swings
- **Intermediate**: 20kg-24kg, 6 swings
- **Advanced**: 28kg-32kg, 6-8 swings

## StrongFirst Principles
- Practice perfect movement patterns
- Use tension and relaxation appropriately
- Focus on power breathing

## Technical Notes
- Maintain hardstyle lockout
- Use aggressive hip drive
- Keep arms relaxed, let hips do the work
- Good preparation for heavier KB Axe sessions
