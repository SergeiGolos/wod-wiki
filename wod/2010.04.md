# 2010 CrossFit Games - Event 4

**Category:** Competition
**Type:** For Time
**Location:** Aromas, California
**Date:** July 17, 2010 (Day 2 - Saturday)

## Description

A 10-round triplet of burpees, push presses, and box jumps with a 15-minute time cap.

```wod
(10)
  5 Burpees
  10 Push Press 75lb
  15 Box Jumps 24in
```

## Scaling

| Level | Push Press | Box Height |
|-------|------------|------------|
| Men | 75 lb | 24" |
| Women | 55 lb | 20" |

## Breakdown

- **Modality:** Mixed triplet
- **Time Domain:** Moderate with cap (15 minutes max)
- **Primary Movements:** Burpee, push press, box jump
- **Intensity:** High

## Collectible Metrics

- **Time:** Total time to complete (or reps at time cap)
