# Barbara

**Category**: CrossFit Benchmark  
**Type**: For Time  
**Difficulty**: Intermediate  

## Description
Barbara is a challenging benchmark that combines gymnastics movements with structured rest periods.

## WOD Wiki Syntax
```wod
(5)
  + 20 Pullups
  + 30 Pushups
  + 40 Situps
  + 50 Air Squats
  3:00 Rest
```

## Breakdown
- **Rounds**: 5 rounds
- **Rest**: 3 minutes between each round
- **Exercises**: 
  - Pullups: 20 reps
  - Pushups: 30 reps
  - Situps: 40 reps
  - Air Squats: 50 reps
- **Goal**: Complete all exercises in each round, then rest

## Target Times
- **Elite**: Under 7 minutes (excluding rest)
- **Advanced**: 7-9 minutes  
- **Intermediate**: 9-12 minutes
- **Beginner**: 12+ minutes

## Tips
- Use the rest periods to recover fully
- Break up sets early to avoid failure
- Focus on proper form over speed

## Scaling Options
- **Beginner**: Band-assisted pullups, knee pushups, reduce reps
- **Intermediate**: Jumping pullups, regular pushups
- **Advanced**: Strict pullups, hand-release pushups
