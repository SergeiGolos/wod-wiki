# 2013 CrossFit Games - Event 1 - "The Pool"

**Location: StubHub Center, Carson, California  
Date: July 24-28, 2013**

- **Format:** For time
- **Workout:**
- Swim 10 lengths of the pool (approx. 250 meters)
- Then for time:
- 21 Thrusters (95 lbs men / 65 lbs women)
- 21 Lateral burpees over bar
- 15 Thrusters
- 15 Lateral burpees over bar
- 9 Thrusters
- 9 Lateral burpees over bar
