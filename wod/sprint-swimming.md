# Sprint Swimming

**Category**: Swimming  
**Type**: Speed & Power Training  
**Difficulty**: Intermediate  

## Description
A sprint-focused swimming workout developing speed and explosive power.

## WOD Wiki Syntax
```
(8) Warmup
  25m Swim
  :30 Rest

(4)
  25m Kick
  :30 Rest

(4)
  25m Pull
  :30 Rest

(8)
  25m Sprint
  :30 Rest

100m Cooldown
```

## Breakdown
- **Warmup**: 8 sets of 25m swimming
- **Kick Set**: 4 sets of 25m kicking
- **Pull Set**: 4 sets of 25m pulling
- **Sprint Set**: 8 sets of 25m maximum effort sprints
- **Cooldown**: 100m easy swim
- **Total Distance**: 650m

## Tips
- Go all-out during sprint sets
- Focus on explosive starts and turns
- Use longer rest between sprints for quality
- Practice race starts and finishes

## Scaling Options
- **Intermediate**: As written
- **Advanced**: Increase sprint distance to 50m
- **Beginner**: Reduce sprint sets or increase rest

## Technical Notes
- Focus on powerful strokes and fast kick
- Practice streamlining off walls
- Work on acceleration and top speed
- Quality over quantity for sprint work
