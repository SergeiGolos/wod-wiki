# Individual Medley Swimming

**Category**: Swimming  
**Type**: Technical Training  
**Difficulty**: Advanced  

## Description
An Individual Medley (IM) workout practicing all four competitive strokes.

## WOD Wiki Syntax
```
(4) Warmup
  100m IM
  :30 Rest

(4)
  50m Butterfly
  :30 Rest

(4)
  50m Backstroke
  :30 Rest

(4)
  50m Breaststroke
  :30 Rest

(4)
  50m Freestyle
  :30 Rest

200m Cooldown
```

## Breakdown
- **Warmup**: 4 sets of 100m Individual Medley
- **Butterfly Set**: 4 sets of 50m butterfly
- **Backstroke Set**: 4 sets of 50m backstroke
- **Breaststroke Set**: 4 sets of 50m breaststroke
- **Freestyle Set**: 4 sets of 50m freestyle
- **Cooldown**: 200m easy swim
- **Total Distance**: 1400m

## Tips
- Focus on proper technique for each stroke
- Use IM order: Butterfly → Backstroke → Breaststroke → Freestyle
- Work on transitions between strokes
- Practice breathing patterns for each stroke

## Scaling Options
- **Advanced**: As written
- **Intermediate**: Reduce sets or increase rest
- **Beginner**: Focus on one stroke at a time

## Technical Notes
- Butterfly: timing of arms and legs
- Backstroke: consistent rotation
- Breaststroke: proper pullout technique
- Freestyle: high elbow catch and rotation
