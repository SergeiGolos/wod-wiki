# Grace

**Category**: CrossFit Benchmark  
**Type**: For Time  
**Difficulty**: Advanced  

## Description
Grace is a pure Olympic lifting workout that tests explosive power and technique.

```wod
30 Clean & Jerk 135lb
```

## Breakdown
- **Total Reps**: 30
- **Exercise**: Clean & Jerks at 135lb
- **Goal**: Complete as fast as possible

## Target Times
- **Elite**: Under 1:30
- **Advanced**: 1:30-2:30  
- **Intermediate**: 2:30-4:00
- **Beginner**: 4:00+ minutes

## Tips
- Focus on efficient technique over speed
- Use hook grip for better bar control
- Consider singles for heavier weights

## Scaling Options
- **Beginner**: 95lb, focus on form
- **Intermediate**: 115lb, small sets
- **Advanced**: Rx'd as written, consider 155lb

## Technical Notes
- Full squat clean and split jerk recommended
- Use hip explosion for clean power
- Reset between reps for quality
