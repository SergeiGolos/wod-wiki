# ABC (Agile, Burly, Complex)

**Category**: Dan John  
**Type**: Interval Strength  
**Difficulty**: Intermediate  

## Description
Dan John's ABC program combines Olympic lifting, pressing, and squatting in an efficient complex.

```wod
(20) 1:00
  + 2 Clean
  + 1 Press
  + 3 Front Squat
```

## Breakdown
- **Duration**: 20 minutes
- **Format**: EMOM (Every Minute On the Minute)
- **Exercises**: 
  - 2 Cleans
  - 1 Press
  - 3 Front Squats
- **Rest**: Remaining time in each minute

## Tips
- Use appropriate weight - this is technical work
- Focus on quality movement over speed
- Complex means same bar for all movements

## Scaling Options
- **Beginner**: Light bar (65-95lb), focus on form
- **Intermediate**: Moderate weight (95-135lb)
- **Advanced**: Heavier weight (135-185lb)

## Dan John Principles
- "The goal is to keep the goal the goal"
- Quality over quantity
- Complex training for efficiency
- Fundamental human movements

## Technical Notes
- Cleans can be power or full squat
- Use same weight for all movements
- Front squat from clean position
- Press can be strict or push press
