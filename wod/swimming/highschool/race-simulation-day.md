# Race Simulation Day

**Type**: Race Preparation  
**Duration**: 60 minutes  
**Difficulty**: Advanced  

### Description
This pre-meet workout simulates race conditions and prepares swimmers mentally and physically for competition. Short, sharp efforts at race pace prime the nervous system while allowing adequate recovery before the meet. The session includes race-pace rehearsals for primary events and starts practice. Coaches should use this workout the day before competition to ensure swimmers are sharp but not fatigued.

```wod
(3) Warmup
  100m Freestyle
  :20 Rest

(4) Activation
  50m Build
  :20 Rest

(4) Race Pace 100s
  100m Race Pace
  3:00 Rest

(3) Sprint Rehearsal
  25m Dive Sprint
  2:00 Rest

(4) Pace 50s
  50m Race Pace
  1:30 Rest

100m Cooldown
```

### Breakdown
- **Warm-up**: 300m easy swimming to loosen muscles
- **Activation**: 200m progressive build to race pace
- **Race Pace 100s**: 400m at goal race pace with full recovery
- **Sprint Rehearsal**: 75m working on starts and initial speed
- **Pace 50s**: 200m maintaining goal pace
- **Cooldown**: 100m easy recovery

### Collectible Metrics
- Actual race pace times vs. goal times
- Start reaction times
- Perceived readiness rating (1-10)
