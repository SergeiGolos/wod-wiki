# Bodyweight Pyramid Workout

**Category**: Bodyweight Training  
**Type**: For Time  
**Difficulty**: Intermediate  

## Description
A pyramid-style bodyweight workout that starts high and decreases reps each round.

```wod
For Time:
  100 Burpees
  75 Situps
  50 Pushups
  25 Handstand Pushups
```

## Breakdown
- **Format**: For Time (complete as fast as possible)
- **Exercises**: 
  - Burpees: 100 reps
  - Situps: 75 reps
  - Pushups: 50 reps
  - Handstand Pushups: 25 reps
- **Goal**: Complete all exercises in sequence

## Target Times
- **Elite**: Under 15 minutes
- **Advanced**: 15-25 minutes
- **Intermediate**: 25-35 minutes
- **Beginner**: 35+ minutes

## Tips
- Break burpees into manageable sets early
- Push through the middle sets mentally
- Focus on efficient movement patterns
- Use appropriate scaling for HSPU

## Scaling Options
- **Beginner**: Reduce reps (50/40/30/15), use pike pushups
- **Intermediate**: Reduce HSPU to hand-release pushups
- **Advanced**: Rx'd as written, add weight vest

## Technical Notes
- Full burpee: chest to ground, jump with clap
- Situps: anchor feet or use abmat
- Pushups: chest to floor, full extension
- HSPU: head to floor, full lockout
