# Diane

**Category**: CrossFit Benchmark  
**Type**: For Time  
**Difficulty**: Advanced  

## Description
Diane combines heavy deadlifts with demanding gymnastics work, testing both strength and skill.

## WOD Wiki Syntax
```
(21-15-9)
  Deadlift 225lb
  Handstand Pushups
```

## Breakdown
- **Rounds**: 3 rounds with descending rep scheme (21-15-9)
- **Exercises**: 
  - Deadlifts at 225lb
  - Handstand Pushups
- **Goal**: Complete as fast as possible

## Target Times
- **Elite**: Under 3 minutes
- **Advanced**: 3-6 minutes  
- **Intermediate**: 6-9 minutes
- **Beginner**: 9+ minutes

## Tips
- Focus on deadlift technique under fatigue
- Scale handstand pushups appropriately
- Break sets early to maintain form

## Scaling Options
- **Beginner**: Reduce deadlift weight to 135lb, use pike pushups
- **Intermediate**: 185lb deadlifts, handstand pushup progressions
- **Advanced**: Rx'd as written, consider 275lb for elite

## Safety Notes
- Maintain proper deadlift form when tired
- Use spotters or safety bars for heavy lifts
- Scale handstand pushups to avoid neck strain
