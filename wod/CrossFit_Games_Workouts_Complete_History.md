## 2007 CrossFit Games

**Location:** Aromas, California  
**Date:** June 30 - July 1, 2007  
**First-ever CrossFit Games**

### Day 1 - Saturday

**Event 1**
- **Format:** For time
- **Workout:**
  - 1000 meter Row
  - Then 5 rounds of:
    - 25 Pull-ups
    - 7 Push jerks (135 lbs men / 95 lbs women)

**Event 2**
- **Format:** For time
- **Workout:** Trail Run (Approx. 5K)

### Day 2 - Sunday

**Event 3**
- **Format:** For time
- **Workout:**
  - 5 rounds of:
    - 10 Push-ups
    - 10 Sit-ups
    - 10 Squats
    - 10 Ring rows

**Event 4**
- **Format:** For max reps
- **Workout:**
  - Max rep Snatch in 3 minutes
  - Men: 75 lbs / Women: 55 lbs

**Winners:** James FitzGerald (Men), Jolie Gentry (Women)
