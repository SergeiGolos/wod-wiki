# Chelsea

**Category**: CrossFit Benchmark  
**Type**: EMOM (Every Minute on the Minute)  
**Difficulty**: Intermediate  

## Description
Chelsea is a 30-minute EMOM that tests consistency and pacing over an extended period.

```wod
(30) :60 EMOM
  + 5 Pullups
  + 10 Pushups
  + 15 Air Squats
```

## Breakdown
- **Duration**: 30 minutes
- **Format**: EMOM (complete work every minute on the minute)
- **Exercises**: 
  - Pullups: 5 reps
  - Pushups: 10 reps
  - Air Squats: 15 reps
- **Goal**: Complete all reps within each minute

## Tips
- Start with manageable pace - the volume adds up
- Use remaining rest time efficiently
- Focus on consistent movement quality

## Performance
- **Complete**: All 30 rounds completed
- **Incomplete**: Record total rounds completed

## Scaling Options
- **Beginner**: Reduce reps or use assisted variations
- **Intermediate**: Scale difficulty as needed for consistency
- **Advanced**: Add weight or more challenging variations
