# KB Axe Heavy

**Category**: StrongFirst  
**Type**: Interval Training  
**Difficulty**: Advanced  

## Description
A high-intensity kettlebell interval workout focusing on power and conditioning.

## WOD Wiki Syntax
```
(20) 1:00 
  4 KB Swings 106lb
```

## Breakdown
- **Duration**: 20 minutes
- **Format**: EMOM (Every Minute On the Minute)
- **Exercise**: 4 heavy kettlebell swings per minute (106lb/48kg)
- **Rest**: Remaining time in each minute after completing swings

## Tips
- Use explosive hip power, not arm strength
- Maintain perfect form despite fatigue
- Focus on aggressive hip snap

## Scaling Options
- **Beginner**: 16kg, 3-4 swings
- **Intermediate**: 24kg, 4 swings
- **Advanced**: 32kg-48kg, 4-5 swings

## StrongFirst Principles
- Hardstyle tension at lockout
- Power breathing for force production
- Fast and aggressive movement

## Technical Notes
- Heavy swings require perfect setup
- Use lats to protect shoulders
- Maintain vertical forearm at lockout
- This is a conditioning session, not strength
