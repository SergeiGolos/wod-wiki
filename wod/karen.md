# Karen

**Category**: CrossFit Benchmark  
**Type**: For Time  
**Difficulty**: Intermediate  

## Description
Karen is a simple but brutal workout that tests muscular endurance and mental toughness.

## WOD Wiki Syntax
```
150 Wall Ball Shots 20lb
```

## Breakdown
- **Total Reps**: 150
- **Exercise**: Wall Ball Shots at 20lb (10ft target)
- **Goal**: Complete as fast as possible

## Target Times
- **Elite**: Under 6 minutes
- **Advanced**: 6-10 minutes  
- **Intermediate**: 10-15 minutes
- **Beginner**: 15+ minutes

## Tips
- Break into manageable sets early
- Focus on efficient squat and throw technique
- Use consistent breathing pattern

## Scaling Options
- **Beginner**: 100 reps, 14lb ball, 9ft target
- **Intermediate**: 150 reps, 14lb ball, 10ft target
- **Advanced**: Rx'd as written, consider 20lb to 12ft target

## Technical Notes
- Full squat depth (hip crease below knee)
- Hit target height consistently
- Use hips to propel ball, not just arms
