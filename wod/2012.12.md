# 2012 CrossFit Games - Event 12

**Location: Home Depot Center, Carson, California  
Date: July 11-15, 2012**

- **Format:** For time
- **Workout:**
- 3 rounds of:
- 5 Muscle-ups
- 12 Snatches (155 lbs men / 105 lbs women)
- 2 rounds of:
- 5 Muscle-ups
- 12 Snatches (175 lbs men / 120 lbs women)
- 1 round of:
- 5 Muscle-ups
- 12 Snatches (195 lbs men / 135 lbs women)
