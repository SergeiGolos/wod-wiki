# 2013 CrossFit Games - Event 11 "Cinco"

**Category:** Competition
**Type:** For Time
**Location:** StubHub Center, Carson, California
**Date:** July 28, 2013 (Day 5 - Sunday)

## Description

5 rounds of a front squat, chest-to-bar pull-up, and box jump triplet.

```wod
(5)
  5 Front Squats 185lb
  5 Chest to Bar Pullups
  5 Box Jumps 30in
```

| Men | 185 lb | 30" |
| Women | 125 lb | 24" |
