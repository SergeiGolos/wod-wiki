# Keith Weber Kettlebell Workouts

**Category**: High-Intensity Kettlebell Cardio  
**Founder**: Keith Weber  
**Philosophy**: "Awaken the athlete within." - High-intensity, follow-along workouts that push physical limits.

---

## Overview

Keith Weber is a physical therapist and one of the first Russian Kettlebell Challenge (RKC) certified trainers in Canada, having received his certification in 2002. He gained widespread recognition through his "Extreme Kettlebell Cardio Workout" DVD series, which has become legendary in the kettlebell community for its intensity and effectiveness. The program was famously endorsed by Joe Rogan, who called it "one of the most difficult things I've ever done."

Keith's approach combines his physical therapy background with high-intensity kettlebell training, creating workouts that are both challenging and technically sound. His programs emphasize perfect form at high intensity, pushing practitioners to their physical limits while maintaining safety. The Extreme Kettlebell Cardio series features follow-along workouts where Keith demonstrates each exercise and counts off repetitions, making it accessible for home practitioners.

## Workout 1: Extreme Kettlebell Cardio Workout 1 (Full Body)

**Category**: Keith Weber Signature  
**Type**: High-Intensity Circuit  
**Difficulty**: Advanced

### Description

Extreme Kettlebell Cardio Workout 1 is the flagship workout from Keith Weber's DVD series. This full-body circuit combines multiple kettlebell movements into a relentless sequence designed to push heart rate, build strength endurance, and test mental fortitude. The workout flows continuously from one exercise to the next with minimal rest.

The circuit targets every major muscle group while maintaining elevated heart rate throughout. Keith demonstrates perfect technique and counts off each repetition, allowing practitioners to follow along. The intensity is self-regulated through weight selection, but the format demands sustained effort regardless of weight choice.

```wod
Extreme Kettlebell Cardio Circuit
(2)
  15 Two-Hand KB Swing 20kg
  15 Goblet Squat 20kg
  15 Pushups
  :30 Rest

(2)
  15 Clean & Press Left 16kg
  15 Clean & Press Right 16kg
  :30 Rest

(2)
  15 KB Snatch Left 16kg
  15 KB Snatch Right 16kg
  :30 Rest

(2)
  15 Turkish Get Up Left 12kg
  15 Turkish Get Up Right 12kg
  :30 Rest

(2)
  15 Two-Hand KB Swing 20kg
  :15 Rest
  15 Goblet Squat 20kg
```

### Breakdown

- **Rounds/Duration**: 25-35 minutes
- **Exercises**: Swing, Goblet Squat, Push-ups, Clean & Press, Snatch, Turkish Get-Up
- **Goal**: Complete all rounds with maximum intensity

### Collectible Metrics

- Total time to complete
- Weight used for each movement
- Quality maintained throughout

## Workout 2: The Man Maker (Leg Focus)

**Category**: Keith Weber Lower Body  
**Type**: High-Intensity Lower Body  
**Difficulty**: Advanced

### Description

The Man Maker is Keith Weber's leg-focused workout designed to build lower body strength and endurance through high-volume squatting and lunging variations. The workout takes approximately 6 minutes to complete but delivers an intense stimulus that challenges even conditioned athletes.

The session uses goblet squats, lunges, and swing variations to target the legs from multiple angles. The continuous format with minimal rest creates significant metabolic demand while building muscular endurance in the quads, glutes, and hamstrings.

```wod
(3)
  20 Goblet Squat 20kg
  :15 Rest

(3)
  10 Lunge Left 16kg
  10 Lunge Right 16kg
  :15 Rest

(3)
  15 Sumo Deadlift 24kg
  :15 Rest

(3)
  20 Two-Hand KB Swing 20kg
  :15 Rest
```

### Breakdown

- **Rounds/Duration**: 6-10 minutes
- **Exercises**: Goblet Squat, Lunge, Sumo Deadlift, Swing
- **Goal**: Maximum leg burn in minimum time

### Collectible Metrics

- Total reps completed
- Weight used
- Time to complete

## Workout 3: The Kettlebell Fitness Test

**Category**: Keith Weber Assessment  
**Type**: Fitness Benchmark  
**Difficulty**: Advanced

### Description

The Kettlebell Fitness Test is Keith Weber's benchmark workout designed to assess overall conditioning and kettlebell proficiency. The test combines multiple movements into a single, continuous effort that reveals strengths and weaknesses across different physical qualities.

The test flows through swings, cleans, presses, snatches, and squats, challenging both strength and cardiovascular capacity. Success requires not only physical conditioning but also the ability to pace oneself strategically across different movements.

```wod
Fitness Test
50 Two-Hand KB Swing 20kg
25 Clean & Press Left 16kg
25 Clean & Press Right 16kg
25 KB Snatch Left 16kg
25 KB Snatch Right 16kg
50 Goblet Squat 20kg
50 Two-Hand KB Swing 20kg
```

### Breakdown

- **Rounds/Duration**: Single continuous effort
- **Exercises**: Swing, Clean & Press, Snatch, Goblet Squat
- **Goal**: Complete as fast as possible with good form

### Standards

| Level | Time |
|-------|------|
| Beginner | 20+ minutes |
| Intermediate | 15-20 minutes |
| Advanced | 12-15 minutes |
| Elite | Under 12 minutes |

### Collectible Metrics

- Total time to completion
- Weight used
- Movement quality throughout

## Workout 4: Upper Body Blast

**Category**: Keith Weber Upper Body  
**Type**: Upper Body Strength Endurance  
**Difficulty**: Intermediate to Advanced

### Description

The Upper Body Blast focuses on pressing, pulling, and core strength through a continuous circuit of upper-body movements. The workout uses both bilateral and unilateral movements to develop balanced upper-body strength and muscular endurance.

The circuit includes pressing variations, rowing movements, and core work, creating comprehensive upper-body development. The continuous format with minimal rest creates significant metabolic demand in addition to muscular stimulus.

```wod
(3)
  10 Clean & Press Left 16kg
  10 Clean & Press Right 16kg
  :20 Rest

(3)
  10 Bent Over Row Left 16kg
  10 Bent Over Row Right 16kg
  :20 Rest

(3)
  10 Pushups
  10 Halo Left 12kg
  10 Halo Right 12kg
  :20 Rest

(3)
  10 Floor Press Left 16kg
  10 Floor Press Right 16kg
  :20 Rest
```

### Breakdown

- **Rounds/Duration**: 15-20 minutes
- **Exercises**: Clean & Press, Row, Push-up, Halo, Floor Press
- **Goal**: Complete all rounds with quality

### Collectible Metrics

- Total pressing volume
- Weight progression
- Quality maintained

## Workout 5: The Finisher (Metabolic Conditioning)

**Category**: Keith Weber Metabolic  
**Type**: High-Intensity Finisher  
**Difficulty**: Intermediate to Advanced

### Description

The Finisher is designed to be performed at the end of a training session to maximize metabolic output and test mental toughness. The short, intense format uses high-repetition swings and squats to create maximum fatigue in minimum time.

The workout can be used as a standalone conditioning session or appended to a strength workout for additional conditioning stimulus. The simplicity of the movements allows for maximum intensity with minimal technical degradation under fatigue.

```wod
The Finisher
(5)
  20 Two-Hand KB Swing 20kg
  10 Goblet Squat 20kg
  :30 Rest

Tabata Finish
(8)
  :20 KB Swing 16kg
  :10 Rest
```

### Breakdown

- **Rounds/Duration**: 8-12 minutes
- **Exercises**: Two-Hand Swing, Goblet Squat
- **Goal**: Maximum intensity in minimum time

### Collectible Metrics

- Total reps completed
- Time to complete
- Heart rate response

## Workout 6: Double Kettlebell Extreme

**Category**: Keith Weber Advanced  
**Type**: Double Kettlebell Circuit  
**Difficulty**: Advanced

### Description

Double Kettlebell Extreme represents the next level of Keith Weber's programming, using two kettlebells to increase both load and complexity. The double kettlebell format requires greater coordination and stability while allowing for heavier loads on bilateral movements.

The workout flows through double cleans, presses, squats, and swings, creating a comprehensive full-body stimulus. The increased load demands technical proficiency and creates significant cardiovascular challenge.

```wod
(3)
  10 Double Clean 20kg
  :20 Rest

(3)
  10 Double Press 20kg
  :20 Rest

(3)
  10 Double Front Squat 20kg
  :20 Rest

(3)
  10 Double Swing 20kg
  :20 Rest

(3)
  + 5 Double Clean 20kg
  + 5 Double Press 20kg
  + 5 Double Front Squat 20kg
  :45 Rest
```

### Breakdown

- **Rounds/Duration**: 20-25 minutes
- **Exercises**: Double Clean, Double Press, Double Front Squat, Double Swing
- **Goal**: Complete all movements with double bells

### Collectible Metrics

- Weight progression
- Total volume
- Quality maintained with doubles
