## Workout 5: StrongFirst Snatch Test

**Category**: StrongFirst Certification Standard  
**Type**: Max Reps  
**Difficulty**: Advanced

### Description

The StrongFirst Snatch Test is the certification standard for StrongFirst SFG (StrongFirst Girya) instructors. This test requires 100 snatches in 5 minutes with a 24kg kettlebell for men or 12kg for women. The test is typically performed as part of the certification weekend, where candidates must demonstrate not only the physical capacity to complete the test but also technical proficiency and safety throughout.

The snatch test challenges both the alactic and aerobic energy systems, requiring explosive power in each snatch while developing significant cardiovascular demand over the five minutes. Pacing strategies vary, but most successful candidates use a structured approach with planned rest periods, often breaking the 100 reps into sets with brief pauses in the rack or overhead position.

```wod
5:00 ? KB Snatch 24kg
```
