## Workout 2: The Rite of Passage

**Category**: StrongFirst Strength Program  
**Type**: Progressive Strength  
**Difficulty**: Intermediate to Advanced

### Description

The Rite of Passage is StrongFirst's premier press-focused program, designed to build impressive overhead pressing strength using a structured laddering approach. The program uses "ladders" - progressively increasing rep schemes that allow for high volume without excessive fatigue. A typical ladder progresses from 1 rep to 2 reps to 3 reps, then resets. This structure allows for significant total volume while maintaining quality throughout each set.

The program progresses through light, medium, and heavy days, incorporating clean and press ladders along with pull-up or chin-up work. Over weeks and months, practitioners progressively increase the height of their ladders and the weight of their kettlebell, building substantial pressing power. The Rite of Passage has produced countless practitioners capable of pressing half their bodyweight or more overhead with a single kettlebell.

```wod
(5) Light Day
  + 1 Clean & Press
  + 2 Clean & Press
  + 3 Clean & Press
  :60 Rest

(3) Pullups

(5) Medium Day
  + 1 Clean & Press
  + 2 Clean & Press
  + 3 Clean & Press
  :60 Rest

(3) Pullups

(3) Heavy Day
  + 1 Clean & Press
  + 2 Clean & Press
  + 3 Clean & Press
  + 4 Clean & Press
  + 5 Clean & Press
  :60 Rest

(5) Pullups
```

### Breakdown

- **Rounds/Duration**: Variable based on ladder height
- **Exercises**: Clean & Press, Pull-ups
- **Goal**: Build to pressing a kettlebell half your bodyweight

### Collectible Metrics

- Maximum ladder height achieved
- Weight pressed
- Total volume per session
