## Workout 4: Upper Body Blast

**Category**: Keith Weber Upper Body  
**Type**: Upper Body Strength Endurance  
**Difficulty**: Intermediate to Advanced

### Description

The Upper Body Blast focuses on pressing, pulling, and core strength through a continuous circuit of upper-body movements. The workout uses both bilateral and unilateral movements to develop balanced upper-body strength and muscular endurance.

The circuit includes pressing variations, rowing movements, and core work, creating comprehensive upper-body development. The continuous format with minimal rest creates significant metabolic demand in addition to muscular stimulus.

```wod
(3)
  10 Clean & Press Left 16kg
  10 Clean & Press Right 16kg
  :20 Rest

(3)
  10 Bent Over Row Left 16kg
  10 Bent Over Row Right 16kg
  :20 Rest

(3)
  10 Pushups
  10 Halo Left 12kg
  10 Halo Right 12kg
  :20 Rest

(3)
  10 Floor Press Left 16kg
  10 Floor Press Right 16kg
  :20 Rest
```

### Breakdown

- **Rounds/Duration**: 15-20 minutes
- **Exercises**: Clean & Press, Row, Push-up, Halo, Floor Press
- **Goal**: Complete all rounds with quality

### Collectible Metrics

- Total pressing volume
- Weight progression
- Quality maintained
