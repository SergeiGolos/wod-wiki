## Workout 3: The Kettlebell Fitness Test

**Category**: Keith Weber Assessment  
**Type**: Fitness Benchmark  
**Difficulty**: Advanced

### Description

The Kettlebell Fitness Test is Keith Weber's benchmark workout designed to assess overall conditioning and kettlebell proficiency. The test combines multiple movements into a single, continuous effort that reveals strengths and weaknesses across different physical qualities.

The test flows through swings, cleans, presses, snatches, and squats, challenging both strength and cardiovascular capacity. Success requires not only physical conditioning but also the ability to pace oneself strategically across different movements.

```wod
Fitness Test
50 Two-Hand KB Swing 20kg
25 Clean & Press Left 16kg
25 Clean & Press Right 16kg
25 KB Snatch Left 16kg
25 KB Snatch Right 16kg
50 Goblet Squat 20kg
50 Two-Hand KB Swing 20kg
```

### Breakdown

- **Rounds/Duration**: Single continuous effort
- **Exercises**: Swing, Clean & Press, Snatch, Goblet Squat
- **Goal**: Complete as fast as possible with good form
