## Workout 6: Double Kettlebell Extreme

**Category**: Keith Weber Advanced  
**Type**: Double Kettlebell Circuit  
**Difficulty**: Advanced

### Description

Double Kettlebell Extreme represents the next level of Keith Weber's programming, using two kettlebells to increase both load and complexity. The double kettlebell format requires greater coordination and stability while allowing for heavier loads on bilateral movements.

The workout flows through double cleans, presses, squats, and swings, creating a comprehensive full-body stimulus. The increased load demands technical proficiency and creates significant cardiovascular challenge.

```wod
(3)
  10 Double Clean 20kg
  :20 Rest

(3)
  10 Double Press 20kg
  :20 Rest

(3)
  10 Double Front Squat 20kg
  :20 Rest

(3)
  10 Double Swing 20kg
  :20 Rest

(3)
  + 5 Double Clean 20kg
  + 5 Double Press 20kg
  + 5 Double Front Squat 20kg
  :45 Rest
```

### Breakdown

- **Rounds/Duration**: 20-25 minutes
- **Exercises**: Double Clean, Double Press, Double Front Squat, Double Swing
- **Goal**: Complete all movements with double bells

### Collectible Metrics

- Weight progression
- Total volume
- Quality maintained with doubles
