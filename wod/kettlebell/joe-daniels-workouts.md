# Joe Daniels (Swing This) Kettlebell Workouts

**Category**: Kettlebell Sport & Hardstyle Fusion  
**Founder**: Joe Daniels  
**Philosophy**: "Train for love of training." - Bridging hardstyle and sport techniques for comprehensive development.

---

## Overview

Joe Daniels is the founder of Swing This Kettlebell Club and has been coaching kettlebell training for over 16 years. His unique approach blends hardstyle kettlebell techniques with sport (Girevoy) methodology, creating a comprehensive training system that develops both explosive power and endurance. Based in Covington, Kentucky, Joe has trained everyone from competitive athletes to beginners seeking general fitness.

Joe's programs, available through Kettlebell Muscle Gain (KBMuscle.com), include offerings for various goals including muscle gain, sport preparation, and general conditioning. His "For Love of Training" program has received widespread acclaim for introducing practitioners to both hardstyle and sport techniques in an accessible format. Joe emphasizes injury prevention through proper biomechanics and uses his experience as a float tank practitioner to incorporate recovery modalities.

## Workout 1: For Love of Training (8-Week Program)

**Category**: Joe Daniels Signature Program  
**Type**: Comprehensive Training Block  
**Difficulty**: Intermediate

### Description

"For Love of Training" is Joe Daniels' flagship 8-week kettlebell muscle gaining program that systematically introduces practitioners to both hardstyle and sport-style kettlebell techniques. The program covers a wide range of movements including swings, cleans, presses, snatches, squats, and various accessory movements, building a complete foundation in kettlebell training.

The program progresses through phases, starting with foundational movement patterns and advancing to more complex combinations. Each session is designed to be completed in 30-45 minutes, making it practical for those with busy schedules while still providing significant training stimulus.

```wod
Phase 1 Week 1-2
(5)
  + 5 KB Swing 20kg
  + 3 Goblet Squat 20kg
  :30 Rest

(3)
  5 Clean & Press Left 20kg
  5 Clean & Press Right 20kg
  :45 Rest

Phase 2 Week 3-4
(5)
  + 3 Clean & Press Left 20kg
  + 3 Front Squat Left 20kg
  + 3 Clean & Press Right 20kg
  + 3 Front Squat Right 20kg
  :45 Rest

(3)
  10 Two-Hand KB Swing 20kg
  :30 Rest
```

### Breakdown

- **Rounds/Duration**: 8 weeks, 3-4 sessions per week
- **Exercises**: Progressive kettlebell movements
- **Goal**: Build comprehensive kettlebell skills and muscle

### Collectible Metrics

- Weight progression
- Movement quality
- Session consistency

## Workout 2: Outerlimits Protocol

**Category**: Joe Daniels Strength  
**Type**: EMOM Strength  
**Difficulty**: Advanced

### Description

The Outerlimits Protocol is Joe Daniels' high-intensity strength protocol that uses single heavy reps performed every 30 seconds for 20-40 minutes. This extended EMOM-style format builds both absolute strength and strength endurance, challenging practitioners to maintain power output under fatigue.

The protocol can be performed with single kettlebell clean and press or double kettlebells. The single rep format allows for heavier weights than traditional rep schemes while the fixed rest intervals create significant training density. This approach builds both neurological efficiency and work capacity.

```wod
(40) :30 Outerlimits
  1 Clean & Press 32kg
```

### Breakdown

- **Rounds/Duration**: 20-40 minutes
- **Exercises**: Clean & Press (single or double)
- **Goal**: Maintain quality reps under fatigue

### Standards

| Level | Duration | Weight (Men) | Weight (Women) |
|-------|----------|--------------|----------------|
| Beginner | 20 min | 24kg | 12kg |
| Intermediate | 30 min | 28kg | 16kg |
| Advanced | 40 min | 32kg | 20kg |

### Collectible Metrics

- Total quality reps
- Weight used
- Form breakdown point

## Workout 3: Tuesday Tens

**Category**: Joe Daniels Conditioning  
**Type**: AMRAP  
**Difficulty**: Intermediate

### Description

Tuesday Tens is a conditioning-focused workout that uses sets of 10 reps across multiple movements. The workout is designed to be accessible while still providing significant conditioning stimulus. The rep scheme of 10 allows for sustained effort while maintaining form.

The workout flows through various movement patterns, creating a full-body training effect. Rest periods are kept short to maintain elevated heart rate while still allowing for quality movement. This format is easily scalable by adjusting weight or rest periods.

```wod
20:00 AMRAP
  10 Two-Hand KB Swing 20kg
  10 Goblet Squat 20kg
  10 Pushups
  :30 Rest
```

### Breakdown

- **Rounds/Duration**: 20 minutes
- **Exercises**: Two-Hand Swing, Goblet Squat, Push-ups
- **Goal**: Complete maximum quality rounds

### Collectible Metrics

- Total rounds completed
- Quality of movement
- Heart rate response

## Workout 4: KBOMG (Kettlebell Only Muscle Gain) - Full Body

**Category**: Joe Daniels Hypertrophy  
**Type**: Full Body Hypertrophy  
**Difficulty**: Intermediate to Advanced

### Description

The KBOMG program is Joe Daniels' comprehensive kettlebell-only muscle building system. This full-body session combines pressing, pulling, squatting, and hinging movements to create balanced muscular development using only kettlebells.

The program uses moderate weights with controlled tempos and strategic volume to create the metabolic stress necessary for hypertrophy. Unlike traditional bodybuilding, the kettlebell movements require and develop coordination and stability alongside muscle mass.

```wod
(4) Press Block
  8 Clean & Press Left 20kg
  8 Clean & Press Right 20kg
  :60 Rest

(4) Squat Block
  10 Goblet Squat 24kg
  :45 Rest

(4) Pull Block
  10 Bent Over Row Left 20kg
  10 Bent Over Row Right 20kg
  :45 Rest

(4) Conditioning Finisher
  15 Two-Hand KB Swing 24kg
  :30 Rest
```

### Breakdown

- **Rounds/Duration**: 30-40 minutes
- **Exercises**: Clean & Press, Goblet Squat, Row, Swing
- **Goal**: Build muscle with kettlebells only

### Collectible Metrics

- Weight progression
- Total volume
- Body measurements

## Workout 5: Fundamentals Program

**Category**: Joe Daniels Beginner  
**Type**: Skill Development  
**Difficulty**: Beginner

### Description

The Fundamentals Program is Joe Daniels' entry-level program designed to teach proper kettlebell technique from the ground up. The program covers the essential movements: swing, goblet squat, clean, press, and Turkish get-up. Each session builds on the previous one, ensuring solid foundations before progressing.

The program is designed for those new to kettlebells or those wanting to refine their technique. Sessions are shorter and focused on quality over quantity, establishing the movement patterns that will support long-term progress.

```wod
Fundamentals Session
(3)
  5 Two-Hand KB Swing 16kg
  :30 Rest

(3)
  5 Goblet Squat 16kg
  :30 Rest

(3)
  5 KB Deadlift 16kg
  :30 Rest

(2)
  3 Turkish Get Up Left 12kg
  3 Turkish Get Up Right 12kg
  :60 Rest
```

### Breakdown

- **Rounds/Duration**: 20-25 minutes
- **Exercises**: Swing, Goblet Squat, Deadlift, Turkish Get-Up
- **Goal**: Establish proper technique

### Collectible Metrics

- Movement quality
- Weight progression
- Confidence with movements

## Workout 6: Sport Preparation Session

**Category**: Joe Daniels Sport Training  
**Type**: Sport-Specific Endurance  
**Difficulty**: Advanced

### Description

This session prepares athletes for kettlebell sport competition, focusing on the ability to maintain lifts for extended durations. The workout uses sport-style technique with relaxation between repetitions to build the efficiency necessary for 10-minute sets.

The session includes both jerk and snatch practice, following the structure of kettlebell sport competition. Rest periods are strategic, allowing for quality work while building the specific endurance required for competition.

```wod
Sport Preparation
10:00 ? KB Jerk 16kg
5:00 Rest
10:00 ? KB Snatch 16kg

Technical Practice
(5)
  1:00 KB Clean 16kg
  :30 Rest
```

### Breakdown

- **Rounds/Duration**: 25-30 minutes
- **Exercises**: Jerk, Snatch, Clean
- **Goal**: Build sport-specific endurance

### Collectible Metrics

- Total reps per 10-minute set
- Pace consistency
- Technique efficiency

## Workout 7: Overhead Squat Session

**Category**: Joe Daniels Mobility & Strength  
**Type**: Strength/Mobility Complex  
**Difficulty**: Intermediate

### Description

The overhead squat is a demanding movement that combines mobility, stability, and strength. This session focuses on developing the overhead squat pattern through progressive loading and supporting movements. The workout includes thoracic spine mobility work and shoulder stability drills.

Joe Daniels has emphasized the overhead kettlebell squat as a diagnostic tool and developmental exercise. The movement reveals limitations in mobility and stability while simultaneously addressing them through practice.

```wod
Warm-up
(3)
  5 Halos 12kg
  5 Arm Bars Left 12kg
  5 Arm Bars Right 12kg

Main Work
(5)
  3 Overhead Squat Left 16kg
  3 Overhead Squat Right 16kg
  :45 Rest

(3)
  10 Two-Hand KB Swing 20kg
  :30 Rest
```

### Breakdown

- **Rounds/Duration**: 20-25 minutes
- **Exercises**: Halo, Arm Bar, Overhead Squat, Swing
- **Goal**: Develop overhead mobility and strength

### Collectible Metrics

- Depth achieved
- Weight progression
- Mobility improvements
