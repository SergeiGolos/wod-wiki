## Workout 6: The Park Bench Program

**Category**: Dan John General Fitness  
**Type**: Maintenance / Easy Strength  
**Difficulty**: Beginner to Intermediate

### Description

The Park Bench Program represents Dan John's philosophy of "park bench" training - consistent, sustainable work that builds over time, as opposed to "bus bench" training where you're constantly waiting for results. The program is designed for long-term practice with minimal fatigue, perfect for those with busy lives who want to maintain strength and fitness.

The workout uses basic movement patterns with moderate volume, focusing on consistency over intensity. It can be performed 3-5 times per week indefinitely, making it ideal for maintenance phases or as a foundation for more intense training blocks.

```wod
(3) Park Bench
  5 Goblet Squat 24kg
  5 KB Swing 24kg
  5 Pushups
  :60 Rest

(3)
  3 Clean & Press Left 24kg
  3 Clean & Press Right 24kg
  :60 Rest

(3)
  5 KB Swing 24kg
  :30 Rest
```

### Breakdown

- **Rounds/Duration**: 15-20 minutes
- **Exercises**: Goblet Squat, Swing, Push-up, Clean & Press
- **Goal**: Consistent daily practice

### Collectible Metrics

- Weekly consistency
- Weight progression
- Energy levels
