## Workout 1: 10,000 Swing Challenge

**Category**: Dan John Signature Program  
**Type**: Strength Endurance Challenge  
**Difficulty**: Intermediate to Advanced

### Description

The 10,000 Swing Challenge is Dan John's most famous kettlebell program, designed to build incredible work capacity, grip strength, and mental fortitude over four weeks. The program requires completing 500 swings per workout, four workouts per week, for a total of 10,000 swings. Between sets of swings, practitioners perform low-volume strength movements to maintain and build strength.

The program follows a structured approach with specific swing rep schemes per set, interspersed with strength movements like goblet squats, presses, or pull-ups. The high volume of swings develops powerful hips, a strong posterior chain, and exceptional conditioning. Many practitioners report significant improvements in body composition, work capacity, and mental toughness after completing the challenge.

### Breakdown

- **Rounds/Duration**: 20 workouts over 4 weeks
- **Exercises**: Two-Hand Kettlebell Swing, Goblet Squat (or press, pull-up)
- **Goal**: Complete 10,000 total swings

### Weekly Structure

| Week | Workouts | Total Swings |
|------|----------|--------------|
| Week 1 | 5 | 2,500 |
| Week 2 | 5 | 2,500 |
| Week 3 | 5 | 2,500 |
| Week 4 | 5 | 2,500 |

### Strength Movement Options (Between Swing Sets)

- Goblet Squats
- Overhead Press
- Pull-ups / Chin-ups
- Dips
