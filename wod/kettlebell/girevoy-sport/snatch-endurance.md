## Workout 3: Snatch Endurance

**Category**: Girevoy Sport Training  
**Type**: Snatch-Specific Endurance  
**Difficulty**: Intermediate to Advanced

### Description

Snatch endurance training develops the capacity to maintain the snatch for extended durations. Training approaches vary from shorter, intense sets to longer, sub-maximal efforts. The goal is to build both the muscular endurance and technical efficiency to maintain pace for 10 minutes.

Training sets may use various structures: fixed time sets, rep-based sets, or pace-based training. Key technical elements include relaxation in the overhead position, efficient drop technique, and hand transitions that minimize grip fatigue.

### Snatch Endurance Session

```wod
(5)
  1:00 KB Snatch Left 16kg
  :30 Rest
  1:00 KB Snatch Right 16kg
  :30 Rest
```
### Build Phase

```wod
(3)
  3:00 KB Snatch 16kg
  1:00 Rest
```
### Test

```wod
5:00 ? KB Snatch 16kg
```


### Breakdown

- **Rounds/Duration**: 25-30 minutes
- **Exercises**: Kettlebell Snatch
- **Goal**: Build snatch endurance progressively

### Pace Targets

| Level | Pace (rpm) | 10-min Target |
|-------|------------|---------------|
| Beginner | 8-10 rpm | 80-100 reps |
| Intermediate | 10-12 rpm | 100-120 reps |
| Advanced | 12-14 rpm | 120-140 reps |
| Elite | 14+ rpm | 140+ reps |

- Minutes continuous
