# 2008 CrossFit Games - Event 1 "Fran"

**Category:** CrossFit Benchmark / Competition
**Type:** For Time
**Location:** Aromas, California
**Date:** July 5, 2008 (Day 1 - Saturday)

## Description

The classic CrossFit benchmark workout "Fran" performed at the 2008 CrossFit Games. One of the most well-known CrossFit workouts worldwide.

```wod
(21-15-9)
  Thrusters 95lb
  Pullups
```

## Scaling

| Level | Thrusters |
|-------|-----------|
| Men | 95 lb |
| Women | 65 lb |

## Breakdown

- **Modality:** Couplet of weightlifting and gymnastics
- **Time Domain:** Short (2-8 minutes expected)
- **Primary Movements:** Thruster, pull-up
- **Intensity:** Very high - designed to be a sprint

## Collectible Metrics

- **Time:** Total time to complete the workout
- Fran times are widely tracked as a CrossFit benchmark
