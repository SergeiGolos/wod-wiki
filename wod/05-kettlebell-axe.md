# Kettlebell AXE

**Category**: StrongFirst  
**Type**: EMOM / Alactic Conditioning  
**Difficulty**: Intermediate to Advanced

## Description

Pavel Tsatsouline's "high-speed, low-drag alternative to HIIT" - develops strength endurance through powerful, submaximal reps with controlled rest. The protocol uses 4-6 explosive reps at 80-90% effort per minute for 20-40 minutes.

---

## Session 1: AXE Heavy Swings (Standard)

The core AXE protocol with two-hand heavy swings.

```wod
(20) 1:00 EMOM
  + 5 Heavy KB Swings ?kg
```

## Collectible Metrics

- **Rounds completed**: Out of target (20-40)
- **Reps per round**: Should be 4-6 quality reps
- **Weight**: Heavy enough to require focus
- **Power quality**: Each rep at 80-90% effort

---

## Session 2: AXE Extended Session

Full 40-minute AXE session for advanced practitioners.

```wod
(40) 1:00 EMOM
  + 5 Heavy KB Swings ?kg
```

## Collectible Metrics

- **Rounds completed**: Target is all 40
- **Stop condition**: When power output drops or form degrades
- **Total volume**: 200 swings if all rounds completed

## Session 3: AXE One-Arm Swings

Alternating arms each minute for unilateral work.

```wod
(20) 1:00 EMOM
  + 5 KB Swings Left ?kg
  + 5 KB Swings Right ?kg
```

### Session Format (Alternating):

```wod
(10) 1:00
  + 5 KB Swings Left ?kg
(10) 1:00
  + 5 KB Swings Right ?kg
```

## Collectible Metrics

- **Reps per side**: Equal volume left and right
- **Asymmetry**: Note any differences between sides
- **Weight**: Typically 70-80% of two-hand swing weight

## Weight Selection Guide

| Level | Men (2-Hand Swing) | Women (2-Hand Swing) | Men (1-Arm) | Women (1-Arm) |
|-------|-------------------|---------------------|-------------|---------------|
| Beginner | 24kg | 16kg | 20kg | 12kg |
| Intermediate | 28-32kg | 20-24kg | 24kg | 16kg |
| Advanced | 32-40kg | 24-28kg | 28kg | 20kg |

---

## Auto-Regulation Rules

| Signal | Action |
|--------|--------|
| Power dropping | Stop session |
| Form degrading | Stop session |
| Heart rate not recovering | Reduce reps or stop |
| Feeling strong | Continue, don't add reps |
| Quality maintained | Build to 40 minutes |

---

## AXE vs HIIT Comparison

| Aspect | AXE | Traditional HIIT |
|--------|-----|-----------------|
| Reps per interval | 4-6 | 15-30+ |
| Effort level | 80-90% | 100%+ |
| Lactic acid | Minimal | High |
| Recovery impact | Low | Can be high |
| Strength interference | Minimal | Possible |
