# 2009 CrossFit Games - Event 2

**Category:** Competition
**Type:** For Time
**Location:** Aromas, California
**Date:** July 11, 2009 (Day 1 - Saturday)

## Description

7 rounds of a snatch and box jump couplet with a 10-minute time cap.

```wod
(7)
  7 Snatches 135lb
  7 Box Jumps 30in
```

## Scaling

| Level | Snatches | Box Height |
|-------|----------|------------|
| Men | 135 lb | 30" |
| Women | 95 lb | 24" |

## Breakdown

- **Modality:** Weightlifting and plyometrics
- **Time Domain:** Moderate with cap (10 minutes max)
- **Primary Movements:** Snatch, box jump
- **Intensity:** High

## Collectible Metrics

- **Time:** Total time to complete (or reps at time cap)
