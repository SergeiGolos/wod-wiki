# ABC Single Bell

**Category**: Dan John  
**Type**: Kettlebell Complex  
**Difficulty**: Intermediate  

## Description
Dan John's kettlebell complex using asymmetrical loading for additional stability challenge.

```wod
(20)
  + 1 Clean & Press Left
  + 1 Clean & Press Right 
  + 2 Front Squat Right
  + 1 Clean & Press Left
  + 1 Clean & Press Right 
  + 2 Front Squat Right
  :30 Rest
```

## Breakdown
- **Duration**: 20 minutes
- **Format**: Complete complex, then :30 rest
- **Exercises**: 
  - Clean & Press: 1 rep left, 1 rep right (twice)
  - Front Squats: 2 reps holding kettlebell on right side
- **Rest**: 30 seconds between complexes

## Tips
- Focus on core stability with asymmetrical loading
- Use appropriate weight - form is crucial
- Complete all movements before rest

## Scaling Options
- **Beginner**: Light kettlebell (8-12kg)
- **Intermediate**: Moderate kettlebell (16-24kg)
- **Advanced**: Heavy kettlebell (28-32kg)

## Dan John Principles
- "Simple, but not easy"
- Asymmetrical loading for core strength
- Complex training efficiency
- Fundamental movement patterns

## Technical Notes
- Maintain upright torso during single-sided squats
- Use hip drive for cleans
- Press can be strict or push press
- Switch sides systematically
