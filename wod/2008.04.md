# 2008 CrossFit Games - Event 4 "Grace"

**Category:** CrossFit Benchmark / Competition
**Type:** For Time
**Location:** Aromas, California
**Date:** July 6, 2008 (Day 2 - Sunday)

## Description

The classic CrossFit benchmark workout "Grace" - 30 clean and jerks for time.

```wod
30 Clean & Jerks 135lb
```

| Level | Clean & Jerks |

| Men | 135 lb |

## Breakdown

- **Modality:** Single-modality weightlifting
- **Time Domain:** Short (2-6 minutes expected)
- **Primary Movement:** Clean and jerk
- **Intensity:** Very high - designed to be a sprint

## Collectible Metrics

- **Time:** Total time to complete 30 reps
- Grace times are widely tracked as a CrossFit benchmark
