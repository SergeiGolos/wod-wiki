# 2018 CrossFit Games - Event 3 - "Martha"

**Location: Alliant Energy Center, Madison, Wisconsin  
Date: August 1-5, 2018**

- **Format:** For time
- **Workout:**
- 5 rounds of:
- 10 Dumbbell snatches (50 lbs men / 35 lbs women)
- 12 Box jumps (30" men / 24" women)
- 9 Chest-to-bar pull-ups
- 6 Push jerks (135 lbs men / 95 lbs women)
