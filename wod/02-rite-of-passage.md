# Rite of Passage (ROP)

**Category**: StrongFirst  
**Type**: Strength / Ladders  
**Difficulty**: Intermediate to Advanced

## Description

Pavel Tsatsouline's legendary pressing program designed to build overhead strength toward a bodyweight press. Uses a ladder system with Clean & Press and Pull-Ups across three intensity days (Heavy, Medium, Light) plus variety days for recovery.

---

## Session 1: Heavy Day (Monday)

The main strength-building session with heavy pressing ladders.

```wod
(1-2-3) Heavy
  - Clean & Press ?kg
  - Pullups
(1-2-3)
  - Clean & Press ?kg
  - Pullups
(1-2-3)
  - Clean & Press ?kg
  - Pullups
```

### Alternative: Higher Volume Heavy Day

```wod
(1-2-3-4-5) Heavy
  - Clean & Press ?kg
  - Pullups
(1-2-3-4-5)
  - Clean & Press ?kg
  - Pullups
(1-2-3-4-5)
  - Clean & Press ?kg
  - Pullups
```

## Collectible Metrics

- **Weight**: Bell weight used for pressing
- **Total reps**: Sum of all press reps across ladders
- **Pull-up progression**: Weight added when 5 clean reps achieved

## Session 4: Variety Day - Swing Focus (Tuesday/Thursday)

Active recovery with moderate swing volume.

```wod
(5) Variety
  - 20 2-Hand KB Swings ?kg
  :60 Rest

(5)
  - 10 Halos ?kg
  :30 Rest
```

### Alternative: Snatch Practice

```wod
10:00 Variety
  - KB Snatches ?kg
```

## Collectible Metrics

- **Volume**: Total swings or snatches completed
- **Technique**: Focus on quality, not intensity
- **Recovery**: Should feel refreshed, not fatigued

---

## Session 5: Variety Day - Mobility Focus

Movement practice and flexibility work.

```wod
(Warmup)
  - 10 Halos Left ?kg
  - 10 Halos Right ?kg
  - 10 Prying Goblet Squats ?kg
  - 30 Hip Bridges
  - 30 Arm Bars
```

## Collectible Metrics

- **Quality of movement**: Not a timed session
- **Mobility gains**: Range of motion improvements
- **Recovery**: Should enhance, not deplete

## Session 6: 1-2-3 Ladder (Week 1)

Foundation phase - building the ladder pattern.

```wod
(1-2-3)
  - Clean & Press Left ?kg
  - Clean & Press Right ?kg
  - Pullups
(1-2-3)
  - Clean & Press Left ?kg
  - Clean & Press Right ?kg
  - Pullups
(1-2-3)
  - Clean & Press Left ?kg
  - Clean & Press Right ?kg

```

## Collectible Metrics

- **Total presses**: 18 (3 ladders × 6 presses each)
- **Bell weight**: Starting weight for cycle
- **RPE**: Should feel manageable, not maximal
