# 2011 CrossFit Games - Event 6

**Category:** Competition
**Type:** For Time
**Location:** Home Depot Center, Carson, California
**Date:** July 30, 2011 (Day 2 - Saturday)

## Description

A row, burpee, and ground-to-overhead sandwich workout.

```wod
500m Row
30 Burpees
30 Ground to Overhead 115lb
500m Row
```

## Scaling

| Level | Ground to Overhead |
|-------|-------------------|
| Men | 115 lb |
| Women | 75 lb |

## Breakdown

- **Modality:** Mixed cardio and weightlifting
- **Time Domain:** Moderate (8-12 minutes expected)
- **Primary Movements:** Rowing, burpee, ground-to-overhead
- **Intensity:** High

## Collectible Metrics

- **Time:** Total time to complete
