# Isabel

**Category**: CrossFit Benchmark  
**Type**: For Time  
**Difficulty**: Advanced  

## Description
Isabel is a pure Olympic lifting workout focused on snatch technique and power.

## WOD Wiki Syntax
```
30 Snatch 135lb
```

## Breakdown
- **Total Reps**: 30
- **Exercise**: Snatches at 135lb
- **Goal**: Complete as fast as possible

## Target Times
- **Elite**: Under 2 minutes
- **Advanced**: 2-4 minutes  
- **Intermediate**: 4-6 minutes
- **Beginner**: 6+ minutes

## Tips
- Focus on perfect technique - snatches are technical
- Use hook grip for bar control
- Consider power snatches if mobility is limited

## Scaling Options
- **Beginner**: 95lb, focus on form
- **Intermediate**: 115lb, small sets
- **Advanced**: Rx'd as written, consider 155lb

## Technical Notes
- Full squat snatch recommended for efficiency
- Use hips for power, not arms
- Quick elbow turnover for the catch
