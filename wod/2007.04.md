# 2007 CrossFit Games - Event 4

**Category:** Competition
**Type:** Max Reps
**Location:** Aromas, California
**Date:** July 1, 2007 (Day 2 - Sunday)

## Description

A max rep snatch test within a 3-minute time window. Athletes perform as many snatches as possible at a fixed weight.

```wod
3:00 ? Snatch 75lb
```

## Scaling

| Level | Weight |
|-------|--------|
| Men | 75 lb |
| Women | 55 lb |

## Breakdown

- **Modality:** Weightlifting
- **Time Domain:** Fixed 3 minutes
- **Primary Movement:** Snatch (full squat or power)

## Collectible Metrics

- **Reps:** Total number of successful snatches completed within 3 minutes
- The `?` makes reps the collectible value
