# Dry Fighting Weight (DFW)

**Category**: StrongFirst  
**Type**: AMRAP / Ladders  
**Difficulty**: Intermediate

## Description

Pavel Tsatsouline's program for athletes who need strength without bulk. Uses 30-minute sessions of Clean & Press and Front Squat ladders with auto-regulation. The goal is maximum quality reps within the time window.

---

## Session 1: Standard DFW Session (30 Minutes)

The core session - ladders of C&P and Front Squat for 30 minutes.

```wod
30:00
  + 1 Double KB Clean & Press ?kg
  + 1 Double KB Front Squat ?kg
  + 2 Double KB Clean & Press ?kg
  + 2 Double KB Front Squat ?kg
  + 3 Double KB Clean & Press ?kg
  + 3 Double KB Front Squat ?kg
```

## Collectible Metrics

- **Total reps**: Combined C&P and Front Squat reps
- **Ladders completed**: How many full 1-2-3 ladders
- **Quality**: Auto-regulate to maintain form
