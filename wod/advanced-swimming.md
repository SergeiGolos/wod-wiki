# Advanced Swimming

**Category**: Swimming  
**Type**: High-Intensity Training  
**Difficulty**: Advanced  

## Description
A challenging swimming workout with higher volume and intensity for experienced swimmers.

## WOD Wiki Syntax
```wod
(4) Warmup
  100m Swim
  :30 Rest

(8)
  50m Kick
  :30 Rest

(8)
  50m Pull
  :30 Rest

(8)
  50m Swim
  :30 Rest

400m Cooldown
```

## Breakdown
- **Warmup**: 4 sets of 100m swimming
- **Kick Set**: 8 sets of 50m kicking
- **Pull Set**: 8 sets of 50m pulling
- **Main Set**: 8 sets of 50m swimming
- **Cooldown**: 400m easy swim
- **Total Distance**: 1800m

## Tips
- Maintain consistent pace across all sets
- Use pace clock or stopwatch for intervals
- Focus on technique under fatigue
- Challenge yourself with faster intervals

## Scaling Options
- **Advanced**: As written
- **Elite**: Reduce rest to :20 or increase distances
- **Intermediate**: Reduce sets or add rest

## Technical Notes
- High-volume workout - ensure proper fueling
- Focus on race-pace technique
- Use different strokes for variety
- Monitor effort level throughout
