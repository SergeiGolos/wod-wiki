# Complex AMRAP Workout

**Category**: CrossFit Style  
**Type**: AMRAP (As Many Rounds As Possible)  
**Difficulty**: Advanced  

## Description
A challenging 20-minute AMRAP combining gymnastics skills and bodyweight movements.

```wod
20:00 AMRAP
  + 5 Handstand Pushups
  + 10 Single-leg Squats
  + 15 Pullups
```

## Breakdown
- **Duration**: 20 minutes
- **Format**: AMRAP (complete as many rounds as possible)
- **Exercises**: 
  - Handstand Pushups: 5 reps
  - Single-leg Squats (Pistols): 10 reps (5 per leg)
  - Pullups: 15 reps
- **Goal**: Complete maximum number of rounds in 20 minutes

## Target Rounds
- **Elite**: 10+ rounds
- **Advanced**: 7-9 rounds
- **Intermediate**: 4-6 rounds
- **Beginner**: 1-3 rounds

## Tips
- This is a skill-based workout - scale appropriately
- Focus on quality movement over speed
- Consider alternating pistol legs

## Scaling Options
- **Beginner**: Pike pushups, box pistols, jumping pullups
- **Intermediate**: Handstand pushup progressions, assisted pistols, band pullups
- **Advanced**: Rx'd as written

## Technical Notes
- Full range of motion on all movements
- Use efficient pistol technique (heel down, chest up)
- Break sets strategically to avoid failure
