# Creating Structured Workout Programs

Now that you've learned the basics, let's explore how to structure complete workout programs over multiple days and weeks.

## Weekly Structure

You can organize workouts by days of the week:

```
# Monday - Upper Body
5 Rounds:
  10 Pull-ups
  15 Push-ups
  20 Dips

# Wednesday - Lower Body
5 Rounds:
  20 Air Squats
  15 Lunges (each leg)
  10 Jump Squats

# Friday - Full Body
AMRAP 20:00
  400m Run
  15 Thrusters 95lb
  10 Burpees
```

## Progressive Overload

Track progress by incrementing weights or reps:

```
# Week 1
5x5 Back Squat 185lb

# Week 2
5x5 Back Squat 195lb

# Week 3
5x5 Back Squat 205lb
```

## EMOM (Every Minute on the Minute)

```
EMOM 15:00
  Min 1: 5 Power Cleans 135lb
  Min 2: 10 Box Jumps 24"
  Min 3: 15 Wall Balls 20lb
```

## Tabata Intervals

```
(8) Tabata
  :20 Work
  :10 Rest
```
