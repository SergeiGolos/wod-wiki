Fran

21-15-9 Thrusters + Pull-ups Benchmark
MET 8.5
Tier Moderate
🏋️ Weightlifting

Classic CrossFit benchmark combining barbell thrusters and pull-ups. Three descending rounds of 21-15-9 reps. Time your finish.

The MET value of 8.5 reflects sustained high-power output across both movements. Intensity tier is Moderate — accessible for most athletes at appropriate loading.

Programming note: Rest 2–3 minutes between rounds for athletes new to the benchmark. Experienced athletes aim for unbroken thrusters in round 1.
