Designer Notes

All 4 concepts use the WOD design system verbatim: #f5f2ed light, #2e3440 dark, Inter + Geist Mono, pill CTAs, rounded-2xl cards, and the green radial gradient wash.

Component coverage: Every concept maps to existing components (AttentionWidget, CodeExampleWidget, SyntaxGroupWidget, WodCompanion, MacOSChrome, RuntimeTimerPanel, FullscreenTimer, MarkdownCanvasPage). Implementation should reuse rather than rebuild.

Mobile: Each prototype includes a responsive note. Default behavior is single-column stacking.

Recommendation: Concept 4 (Minimalist Hero + Expandable Sandbox) has the strongest first impression and most app-like HUD — best alignment with the storytelling flow in WOD-634. Concept 1 (Split-Pane) is the most direct sandbox feel and easiest to implement. Consider CEO/Serge preference as tiebreaker.
