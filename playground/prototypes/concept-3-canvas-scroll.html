📱 Mobile Behavior — Concept 3
  • • Two columns → single column: Prose collapses first. Sticky editor moves below prose at <900px.
  • • "Open Editor" CTA: A sticky button at the bottom of screen lets user jump to the editor panel at any scroll position.
  • • Editor/Timer tabs preserved: The tab switcher (✏️ Editor / ▶ Timer) stays on mobile, full width.
  • • Prose scrolls above editor: Feature bullets use compact card rows (icon + text), not the 3-col grid.
  • • Scroll progress bar: Retained as a thin 2px bar at the top of the phone screen.
