## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile OR row 250m OR bike 2 miles.

```wod
(3)
  25m Quadrupedal Movement
  10 Push Ups
  10 Pull Ups or Jumping Pull Ups
  10 Movement You Suck At
```

Perform a movement that you "suck at." If it is already in the warm up, perform 15 repetitions of that movement instead of 10. For instance, if you suck at sit ups and there are sit ups in the warm up, do 3 rounds of 15 instead of 3 rounds of 10. If you suck at handstand push ups, perform 3 rounds of 10 handstand push ups (or a progression) and add that to the warm up.

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

On the minute for 20 minutes perform 5 push ups, 5 sit ups and 5 air squats.

```wod
(20) :60 EMOM
  + 5 Push Ups
  + 5 Sit Ups
  + 5 Air Squats
```

### Avancée — Advanced

Progression across four 5-minute EMOM blocks:
- Minutes 1–5: 5 / 5 / 5
- Minutes 6–10: 10 / 5 / 5
- Minutes 11–15: 10 / 10 / 5
- Minutes 16–20: 10 / 10 / 10

```wod
(5) :60 EMOM
  + 5 Push Ups
  + 5 Sit Ups
  + 5 Air Squats
(5) :60 EMOM
  + 10 Push Ups
  + 5 Sit Ups
  + 5 Air Squats
(5) :60 EMOM
  + 10 Push Ups
  + 10 Sit Ups
  + 5 Air Squats
(5) :60 EMOM
  + 10 Push Ups
  + 10 Sit Ups
  + 10 Air Squats
```

### Traceur — Expert

Same progression as Avancée, adding 2 burpees after each round. If you cannot complete all movements within the minute, stop the exercise, complete 2 burpees, then move on to the next minute’s repetitions.

```wod
(5) :60 EMOM
  + 5 Push Ups
  + 5 Sit Ups
  + 5 Air Squats
  + 2 Burpees
(5) :60 EMOM
  + 10 Push Ups
  + 5 Sit Ups
  + 5 Air Squats
  + 2 Burpees
(5) :60 EMOM
  + 10 Push Ups
  + 10 Sit Ups
  + 5 Air Squats
  + 2 Burpees
(5) :60 EMOM
  + 10 Push Ups
  + 10 Sit Ups
  + 10 Air Squats
  + 2 Burpees
```
