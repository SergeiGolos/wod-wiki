## Warm Up

We have a sold-out ZombieFit Class today at 6:30p.m. Make sure to check the scheduler a couple hours before to see if there are any cancellations!

```wod
Run 1/4 mile | Row 250m | Bike 0.5 miles
(3)
  10 Push Ups
  10 Air Squats
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Movement You Suck At
```

If you "suck" at a movement, perform that movement for the warm up. If it is already listed in the warm up, perform 15 repetitions of that movement instead of 10. For instance, if you suck at sit ups and sit ups are in the warm up, do 3 rounds of 15 sit ups. If you suck at handstand push ups, perform 3 rounds of 5 handstand push ups (or an appropriate progression) and add that to the warm up.

## WOD

Time yourself for your first repetition, then multiply that time by 4. That is your rest time after each subsequent run. This is a full-effort sprint.

### Étudiant — Beginner

```wod
(5)
  100m Sprint
```

### Avancée — Advanced

```wod
(8)
  100m Sprint
```

### Traceur — Expert

```wod
(11)
  100m Sprint
```
