- First Rule of ZombieFit
- 30 days of ZombieFit by Kyle Wood

Run 1/4 mile OR row 250m OR bike .5 miles.

3 rounds of:
- 5 Push Ups
- 5 Tuck Jumps
- 5 Sit Ups
- 5 "I suck at this"

Perform a movement that you suck at. If it is already in the warm up, perform 10 repetitions of that movement instead of 5. For instance, if you suck at sit ups and there are sit ups in the warm up, do 3 rounds of 10 instead of 3 rounds of 5. If you suck at handstand push ups, perform 3 rounds of 5 handstand push ups (or a progression) and add that to the warm up.

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

As quickly as possible, perform 5 rounds of the following:

```wod
(5)
  100m Sprint
  :30 Rest
  15 Air Squats
```

### Avancée — Advanced

As quickly as possible, perform 5 rounds of the following:

```wod
(5)
  100m Sprint
  :15 Rest
  15 Air Squats
```

### Traceur — Expert

As quickly as possible, perform 5 rounds of the following:

```wod
(5)
  100m Sprint
  25 Air Squats
```
