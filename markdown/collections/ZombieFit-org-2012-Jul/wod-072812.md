## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile OR row 250m OR bike .5 miles.

3 rounds of:
- 10 Push Ups
- 10 Air Squats
- 10 Sit Ups
- 10 of a movement you "suck at" (see note)

Perform a movement that you suck at. If it is already in the warm up, perform 15 repetitions of that movement instead of 10. For instance, if you suck at sit ups and there are sit ups in the warm up, do 3 rounds of 15 instead of 3 rounds of 10. If you suck at handstand push ups, perform 3 rounds of 5 handstand push ups (or an appropriate progression) and add that to the warm up.

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

Perform the following for time:

```wod
(3)
  25m Quadrupedal Movement
  5 Five-Foot Precisions
  10 Dips
```

### Avancée — Advanced

Perform the following for time:

```wod
(5)
  25m Quadrupedal Movement
  5 Five-Foot Precisions
  10 Dips
```

### Traceur — Expert

Perform the following for time:

```wod
(5)
  25m Quadrupedal Movement
  10 Eight-Foot Precisions
  20 Dips
```
