## Warm Up

Warm up options include:
*   Run 1/4 mile
*   Row 250m
*   Bike 2 miles

Perform 3 rounds of the following circuit:
*   25m quadrupedal movement
*   10 push ups
*   10 pull ups / jumping pull ups
*   10 isuckatthis** (perform a movement that you suck at)

***Note on Warm Up Scaling:** If the chosen movement (isuckatthis**) was already included in the warm up circuit, perform 15 repetitions of that movement instead of 10. For example, if you struggle with sit ups, perform 3 rounds of 15 sit ups. If you struggle with handstand push ups, perform 3 rounds of 10 handstand push ups (or their progression) and add this to the warm up.*

## WOD

In as few sets as possible, complete the following circuit:

### Étudiant
```wod
(1)
  30 Jumping Jacks
  30 Push Ups
  30 Jumping Pull Ups
  30 Air Squats
  30 Five-Foot Precisions
  30 Sit Ups
```

### Avancée
```wod
(1)
  50 Jumping Jacks
  50 Push Ups
  50 Jumping Pull Ups
  50 Air Squats
  50 Six-Foot Precisions
  50 Sit Ups
```

### Traceur
```wod
(1)
  50 Burpees
  50 Push Ups
  50 Pull Ups
  50 Air Squats
  50 Six-Foot Precisions
  50 Sit Ups
```
