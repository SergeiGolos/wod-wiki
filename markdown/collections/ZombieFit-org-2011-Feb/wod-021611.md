## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile OR bike 1/2 mile OR row 250m.

Followed by:

```wod
(3)
  50m Quadrupedal Movement
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Jumping Pull Ups
```

May substitute push-ups if you don't have a pull-up bar.
