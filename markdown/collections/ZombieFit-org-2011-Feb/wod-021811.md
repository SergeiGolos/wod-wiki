## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile OR bike 1/2 mile OR row 250m, followed by:

3 rounds of:
- 50m quadrupedal movement
- 10 sit-ups
- 10 jumping pull-ups (may sub with push-ups if you don't have a pull-up bar)

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

```wod
(4)
  200m Sprint
  15 Air Squats
```

### Avancée — Advanced

```wod
(4)
  200m Sprint
  25 Air Squats
  25 Sit Ups
```

### Traceur — Expert

```wod
(4)
  200m Sprint
  25 Air Squats
  25 Push Ups
  25 Sit Ups
```
