## Warm Up

Here in Chicago we’re preparing for the Snowpocalypse, so I hope everyone in the path of this storm stays warm and safe. To those readers in warmer parts of the world... I hate you.

```wod
Run 1/4 mile | Bike 1/2 mile | Row 500m
10 Push Ups
10 Sit Ups
10 Jumping Pull Ups
10 Air Squats
```

## WOD

Perform for time, 21-15-9 repetitions of the following:

### Étudiant — Beginner

```wod
(21-15-9)
  Five-Foot Precisions
  Jumping Pull Ups
```

### Avancée — Advanced

```wod
(21-15-9)
  Six-Foot Precisions
  Pull Ups
```

### Traceur — Expert

```wod
(21-15-9)
  Seven-Foot Precisions
  Pull-Up Burpees
```
