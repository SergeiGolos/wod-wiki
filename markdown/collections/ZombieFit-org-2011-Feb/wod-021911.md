## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile | bike 1/2 mile | row 250m followed by:

3 rounds of:
- 50m quadrupedal movement
- 10 Sit Ups
- 10 Jumping Pull-Ups (may substitute push-ups if you don’t have a pull-up bar)

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

On the minute for 20 minutes:

```wod
(20) :60 EMOM
  + 5 Push Ups
  + 5 Sit Ups
  + 5 Air Squats
```

### Avancée — Advanced

Progressive EMOM — increase reps every 5 minutes:

```wod
(5) :60 EMOM
  + 5 Push Ups
  + 5 Sit Ups
  + 5 Air Squats
(5) :60 EMOM
  + 10 Push Ups
  + 5 Sit Ups
  + 5 Air Squats
(5) :60 EMOM
  + 10 Push Ups
  + 10 Sit Ups
  + 5 Air Squats
(5) :60 EMOM
  + 10 Push Ups
  + 10 Sit Ups
  + 10 Air Squats
```

### Traceur — Expert

Same as Avancée, plus 2 burpees after each minute:

```wod
(5) :60 EMOM
  + 5 Push Ups
  + 5 Sit Ups
  + 5 Air Squats
  + 2 Burpees
(5) :60 EMOM
  + 10 Push Ups
  + 5 Sit Ups
  + 5 Air Squats
  + 2 Burpees
(5) :60 EMOM
  + 10 Push Ups
  + 10 Sit Ups
  + 5 Air Squats
  + 2 Burpees
(5) :60 EMOM
  + 10 Push Ups
  + 10 Sit Ups
  + 10 Air Squats
  + 2 Burpees
```

If you cannot complete all movements within the minute timeframe, stop the exercise, complete 2 burpees, and move on to the next minute’s repetitions.
