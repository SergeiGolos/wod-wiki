## Warm Up

Warm up options include:
*   Run 1/4 mile
*   Bike 1/2 miles
*   Row 250m

Followed by 3 rounds of:
*   50m quadrupedal movement
*   10 sit ups
*   10 jumping pull ups (may sub with push ups if you don’t have a pull up bar)

## WOD

### Étudiant

```wod
(20) :60 EMOM / 5 Pushups / 5 Situps / 5 Air Squats
```

### Avancée

This workout is structured over four consecutive 5-minute blocks, performed with no rest between blocks.

**Block 1 (Minutes 1-5):**
```wod
(5) :60 EMOM / 5 Pushups / 5 Situps / 5 Air Squats
```

**Block 2 (Minutes 6-10):**
```wod
(5) :60 EMOM / 10 Pushups / 5 Situps / 5 Air Squats
```

**Block 3 (Minutes 11-15):**
```wod
(5) :60 EMOM / 10 Pushups / 10 Situps / 5 Air Squats
```

**Block 4 (Minutes 16-20):**
```wod
(5) :60 EMOM / 10 Pushups / 10 Situps / 10 Air Squats
```

### Traceur

This workout follows the structure of Avancée, but requires 2 burpees after completing the reps in each minute.

**Block 1 (Minutes 1-5):**
```wod
(5) :60 EMOM / 5 Pushups / 5 Situps / 5 Air Squats + 2 Burpees
```

**Block 2 (Minutes 6-10):**
```wod
(5) :60 EMOM / 10 Pushups / 5 Situps / 5 Air Squats + 2 Burpees
```

**Block 3 (Minutes 11-15):**
```wod
(5) :60 EMOM / 10 Pushups / 10 Situps / 5 Air Squats + 2 Burpees
```

**Block 4 (Minutes 16-20):**
```wod
(5) :60 EMOM / 10 Pushups / 10 Situps / 10 Air Squats + 2 Burpees
```
