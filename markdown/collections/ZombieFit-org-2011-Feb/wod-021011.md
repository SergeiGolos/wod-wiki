## Warm Up

**Option 1:** ?mile Run (1/4 mile)
**Option 2:** ?m Row (250m)
**Option 3:** ?mile Bike (2 miles)

Perform 3 rounds of the following circuit:
*   25m quadrupedal movement
*   10 Push Ups
*   10 Pull Ups / Jumping Pull Ups
*   10 isuckatthis\*\* (perform a movement that you suck at).

**Scaling Note:** If any movement in the warm up is also one you suck at, perform 15 repetitions of that specific movement instead of 10. For example, if you suck at sit ups and they are listed in the warm-up circuit, perform 3 rounds of 15 sit ups instead of 3 rounds of 10.
