## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile | row 250m | bike 1/2 miles.

3 rounds of:
- 25m quadrupedal movement
- 10 sit ups
- 10 pull ups | jumping pull ups
- 10 double unders | 25 single unders

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

```wod
(5)
  15 Lunges // alternating feet
  5 Burpees
```

### Avancée — Advanced

```wod
(5)
  15 Lunges // alternating feet
  5 Burpee Pull Ups
```

### Traceur — Expert

```wod
(5)
  15 Lunges // alternating feet
  10 Burpee Pull Ups
```

## Cool Down

Three 100m sprints with 2 minutes rest in between each run.
