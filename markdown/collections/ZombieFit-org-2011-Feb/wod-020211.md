## Warm Up

```wod
Run 1/4 mile OR Bike 1/2 mile OR Row 500m
10 Push Ups
10 Sit Ups
10 Jumping Pull Ups
10 Air Squats
```

## WOD

For this WOD you need to make sure you have proper form and range of motion.

### Étudiant — Beginner

Keep your form: feet turned out ~30 degrees, shoulder-width stance, knees tracking over toes, squat as low as you can while keeping back and head up.

```wod
100 Air Squats
```

### Avancée — Advanced

```wod
200 Air Squats
```

### Traceur — Expert

```wod
350 Air Squats
```

## Cool Down

```wod
50 Jumping Jacks
```
