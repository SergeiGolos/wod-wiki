## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile OR row 250m OR bike 2 miles.

```wod
(3)
  25m Quadrupedal Movement
  10 Push Ups
  10 Pull Ups / Jumping Pull Ups
  10 Movement You Suck At
```

Perform a movement that you "suck at." If that movement is already in the warm up, perform 15 repetitions of that movement instead of 10. For instance, if you suck at sit ups and there are sit ups in the warm up, do 3 rounds of 15 instead of 3 rounds of 10. If you suck at handstand push ups, perform 3 rounds of 10 handstand push ups (or their progression) and add that to the warm up.

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

Perform the following movements for time:

```wod
(21-15-9)
  Burpees
  Push Ups
```

### Avancée — Advanced

Hold a handstand against a wall for 20 seconds in between each round (after the sets of 21, 15 and 9).

```wod
(21-15-9)
  Burpees
  Push Ups
  :20 Handstand Against Wall
```

### Traceur — Expert

```wod
(21-15-9)
  Burpees
  Handstand Push Ups
```

This WOD will not work unless you maintain proper form on the movements. Don’t collapse in your burpees, maintain proper range of motion on your push ups, and touch your forehead to the ground on the handstand push ups.
