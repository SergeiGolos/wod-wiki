## WOD

WOD 331 is performed for time, following a 21-15-9 rep scheme.

### Étudiant

```wod
(21-15-9)
  Burpees
  Pushups
```

**Notes:** No specific rest requirements between sets.

### Avancée

```wod
(21-15-9)
  Burpees
  Pushups
```

*In between each set (of 21, 15, and 9 for both movements), hold a handstand against a wall for 20 seconds.*

### Traceur

```wod
(21-15-9)
  Burpees
  Handstand Pushups
```

**Notes:** This WOD requires maintaining proper form: do not collapse in burpees, maintain range of motion on pushups, and touch your forehead to the ground during handstand pushups.
