## Warm Up

Warm up options: run 1/4 mile OR row 250m OR bike 2 miles.

Perform 3 rounds of the following circuit:
*   25m quadrupedal movement
*   10 push ups
*   10 pull ups/jumping pull ups
*   10 isuckatthis** (perform a movement that you suck at)

***Note on Warm Up Scaling:** If any movement listed above is one you particularly struggle with, perform 15 repetitions of that specific movement instead of the prescribed 10. For example, if you suck at handstand push ups, you would perform 3 rounds of 10 Handstand Push Ups (or progression) and add this to the warm up.*

### Traceur
For time:
```wod
(100) Pull-up OR Precision Burpee
```
*\*Precision Burpee Note:** Instead of jumping up and clapping your hands over your head at the end of the burpee, you will either perform a pull-up or a five to six foot precision jump. Then after the movement, drop down into your press.*
