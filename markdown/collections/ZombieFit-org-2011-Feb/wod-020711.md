## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile | row 250m | bike 2 miles.

```wod
(3)
  25m Quadrupedal Movement
  10 Push Ups
  10 Pull Ups | Jumping Pull Ups
  10 Isuckatthis // perform a movement you suck at
```

Perform a movement that you suck at. If it is already in the warm up, perform 15 repetitions of that movement instead of 10. For instance, if you suck at sit ups and there are sit ups in the warm up, do 3 rounds of 15 instead of 3 rounds of 10. If you suck at handstand push ups, perform 3 rounds of 10 handstand push ups (or their progression) and add that to the warm up.

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

```wod
50 Burpees
```

### Avancée — Advanced

```wod
100 Burpees
```

### Traceur — Expert

```wod
100 Pull-Up | Precision Burpees
```

Instead of jumping up and clapping your hands over your head at the end of the burpee, perform either a pull-up or a five- to six-foot precision jump. Then after the movement you drop down into your press.
