## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile OR row 250m OR bike 1/2 miles.

```wod
(3)
  25m Quadrupedal Movement
  10 Jumping Jacks
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Pull Ups / Jumping Pull Ups
  10 Air Squats
```

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

On the minute for 10 minutes. If you cannot complete the round within the minute, stop, perform 2 burpees, then start a new round.

```wod
(10) :60 EMOM
  + 5 Push Ups
  + 7 Sit Ups
  + 9 Jumping Jacks
```

### Avancée — Advanced

On the minute for 15 minutes. Pull-ups may be substituted with jumping pull-ups. If you cannot complete the round within the minute, stop, perform 2 burpees, then start a new round.

```wod
(15) :60 EMOM
  + 5 Pull Ups
  + 7 Push Ups
  + 9 Air Squats
```

### Traceur — Expert

On the minute for 20 minutes. If you cannot complete the round within the minute, stop, perform 2 burpees, then start a new round.

```wod
(20) :60 EMOM
  + 5 L-Sit Pull Ups
  + 9 Push Ups
  + 11 Air Squats
```
