## Warm Up

**Cardio Choice:**
*   Run ?¼ mile
*   Row 250m
*   Bike ½ miles

**Circuit (3 Rounds):**
Perform 3 rounds of the following circuit:
*   25m quadrupedal movement
*   10 jumping jacks
*   10 sit ups
*   10 pull ups/jumping pull ups
*   10 air squats

## WOD

### Étudiant (Beginner)

**Goal:** Complete 10 minutes EMOM.
Perform the following movements every minute on the minute (:60). If you fail to complete the round within the time limit, stop and perform 2 burpees before starting the next round.

```wod
(10) :60 EMOM / +5 Pushups / +7 Situps / +9 Jumping Jacks
```

### Avancée (Intermediate)

**Goal:** Complete 15 minutes EMOM.
Perform the following movements every minute on the minute (:60). If you fail to complete the round within the time limit, stop and perform 2 burpees before starting the next round.

```wod
(15) :60 EMOM / +5 Pullups / +7 Pushups / +9 Air Squats
```

### Traceur (Advanced)

**Goal:** Complete 20 minutes EMOM.
Perform the following movements every minute on the minute (:60). If you fail to complete the round within the time limit, stop and perform 2 burpees before starting the next round.

```wod
(20) :60 EMOM / +5 L-sit Pullups / +9 Pushups / +11 Air Squats
```
