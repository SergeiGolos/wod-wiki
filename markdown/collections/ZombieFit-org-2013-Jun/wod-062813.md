## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile OR row 250m OR bike 1/2 mile.

3 rounds of:
- 10 Push Ups
- 10 Sit Ups
- 10 Pull Ups or Jumping Pull Ups

## WOD

```wod
200 Air Squats
```

### Avancée — Advanced

```wod
400 Air Squats
```

### Traceur — Expert

```wod
500 Air Squats
```
