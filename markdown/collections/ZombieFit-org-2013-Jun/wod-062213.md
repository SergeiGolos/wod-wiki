## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile OR row 250m OR bike 1/2 mile.

3 rounds of:
- 10 Jumping Jacks
- 10 Sit Ups
- 10 Pull Ups (may sub with jumping pull ups)
- 10 Air Squats

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

```wod
(21-15-9)
  12 inch Box Jumps
  Push Ups
  Sit Ups
```

### Avancée — Advanced

```wod
(21-15-9)
  24 inch Box Jumps
  Push Ups
  Knees to Elbows
```

### Traceur — Expert

If needed, substitute parkour squats for box jumps.

```wod
(21-15-9)
  36 inch Box Jumps
  Push Ups
  Knees to Elbows
```
