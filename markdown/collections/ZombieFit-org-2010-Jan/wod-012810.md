## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile | bike 2 miles | row 250m followed by:

```wod
(3)
  50m Quadrupedal Movement
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Push Ups
  10 Double Unders
```

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

21-15-9 repetitions of: push ups, sit ups, burpees.

```wod
(21-15-9)
  Push Ups
  Sit Ups
  Burpees
```

### Avancée — Advanced

21-15-9 repetitions of: push ups, sit ups, precision burpees.

```wod
(21-15-9)
  Push Ups
  Sit Ups
  Precision Burpees
```

### Traceur — Expert

21-15-9 repetitions of: handstand push ups, sit ups, precision burpees.

```wod
(21-15-9)
  Handstand Push Ups
  Sit Ups
  Precision Burpees
```

## Cool Down

```wod
50m Reverse Quadrupedal Movement
```

A precision burpee is when you replace the jump at the top of a normal burpee with a 5 or 6 foot precision (broad jump).
