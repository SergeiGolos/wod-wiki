```wod
(3)

  10 Air Squats
  10 Sit Ups
```

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner
Perform the ladder (21-15-9) for time, then a 10 minute AMRAP.

```wod
(21-15-9)
  Alternating Leg Lunges
  Push Ups
  50m Quadrupedal Movement

10:00 AMRAP
  25m Quadrupedal Movement
  5 Push Ups
  10 Sit Ups
  15 Jumping Jacks
```

### Avancée — Advanced
Lunges both legs (42 total reps for the first set) with 100m quadrupedal movement between sets. Then a 10 minute AMRAP.

```wod
(21-15-9)
  Lunges (both legs; 42 total for first set)
  Push Ups
  100m Quadrupedal Movement

10:00 AMRAP
  50m Quadrupedal Movement
  5 Burpees
  10 Sit Ups
  15 Jumping Jacks
```

### Traceur — Expert
One-legged squats (pistols — perform all reps on one leg then the other) with 100m quadrupedal movement between sets. Then a 20 minute AMRAP.

```wod
(21-15-9)
  One-Legged Squats (Pistols)
  Push Ups
  100m Quadrupedal Movement

20:00 AMRAP
  50m Quadrupedal Movement
  5 Burpees
  10 Sit Ups
  15 Jumping Jacks
```

## Cool Down

```wod
75 Air Squats
50 Alternating Lunges
```
