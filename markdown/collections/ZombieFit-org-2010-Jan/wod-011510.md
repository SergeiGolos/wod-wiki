## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile OR row 250m OR bike 2 miles.

```wod
(3)
  10 Squats
  10 Push Ups
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Pull Ups
```

Substitute jumping pull-ups if needed.

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

```wod
(15-9-7)
  12 inch Box Jumps
  Push Ups
  Sit Ups
```

### Avancée — Advanced

```wod
(21-15-9)
  24 inch Box Jumps
  Push Ups
  Sit Ups
```

### Traceur — Expert

```wod
(21-15-9)
  24 inch Box Jumps
  Push Ups
  Elbows to Knees
```

If needed, substitute parkour squats for box jumps.

## Cool Down

```wod
100m Quadrupedal Movement
```

* When completing a WOD for time, complete the exercises as quickly as possible with minimal/no rest between repetitions and sets. Attempt a sustainable pace. If you have no experience with metabolic conditioning workouts, perform several WODs at an Étudiant level before moving to Avancée or Traceur.
* When performing box jumps, land with both feet firmly planted, stand to full height and drive the hips forward. Pick up your knees when performing the jumps!
