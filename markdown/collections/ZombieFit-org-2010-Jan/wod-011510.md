## Warm Up

**Cardio Options:**
*   Run 1/4 mile
*   Row 250m
*   Bike 2 miles

**Circuit (3 Rounds):**
Perform 3 rounds of the following circuit, focusing on form and controlled movement:
*   10 Squats
*   10 Push Ups
*   10 Sit Ups
*   10 Pull Ups (Sub with jumping pull ups if needed)

## WOD

### Étudiant

```wod
(15-9-7) 12 inch box jumps / push ups / sit ups
```

### Avancée

```wod
(21-15-9) 24 inch box jumps / push ups / sit ups
```

### Traceur

```wod
(21-15-9) 24 inch box jumps / push ups / elbows to knees
```

## Cool Down

Perform a 100m quadrupedal movement.

## Notes & Scaling

*Remember, that when completing a WOD for time, you must complete the exercises as quickly as possible with minimal/no rest in between repetitions and sets. Attempt to go at a pace that is sustainable.*

*If you do not have any experience with metabolic conditioning type workouts, I strongly suggest you perform several WOD’s at an etudiant level prior to moving on to avancee or traceur.*

**Scaling Notes:**
*   When performing box jumps, ensure that you are landing with both feet firmly planted. Stand up to your full height and activate (push forward) your hips. Don’t forget to pick up your knees when performing the jumps!
*   Subparkour squats can be used for box jumps if needed.
