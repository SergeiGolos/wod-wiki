## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile OR bike 2 miles OR row 250m

```wod
(3)
  50m Quadrupedal Movement
  10 Double Unders
  10 Lunges
  10 Jumping Jacks
```

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

8 rounds :20 work / :10 rest for each movement. 1 minute rest between Tabatas.

```wod
(8) :20
  Air Squats
1:00 Rest
(8) :20
  Push Ups
```
