## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile | row 250m | bike 2 miles.

Then practice your parkour roll. If you have no training with a parkour roll, or need some help with your roll, use a progression from a qualified coach (Ryan Doyle progression referenced). Perform at least 50 rolls using both sides of your body. Learning to roll properly and instinctually is essential for taking your training outside.

```wod
50 Parkour Rolls (both sides)
```

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

```wod
10:00 AMRAP
  1 Parkour Roll each side
  5 Push Ups
  10 Air Squats
  15 Sit Ups
```

### Avancée — Advanced

```wod
10:00 AMRAP
  1 Parkour Roll each side
  5 Pull Ups
  10 Push Ups
  15 Air Squats
```

### Traceur — Expert

Now, when performing this WOD, TAKE YOUR TIME WHEN PERFORMING THE PARKOUR ROLL. Concentrate on the form and technique, then move through the rest of the round. Parkour rolls can be dangerous if not done correctly. Do not flip over your neck, or over the center of your back. If ANY part of the roll hurts you are doing it wrong. Stop the timer, and practice the roll until you can perform it pain free.

```wod
20:00 AMRAP
  1 Parkour Roll each side
  5 Pull Ups
  10 Push Ups
  15 Air Squats
```

## Cool Down

```wod
100 Parkour Rolls (either side or alternating)
```
