## Warm Up

Special thanks to Ana and Mary from WGN TV for coming out and spending some time this morning. I think I can speak for everyone when I say that it was a blast!

Also, welcome to all those coming to the site for the first time. Please take a minute and look at the links to the lower right-hand side under the Start Here link list.

Please use caution when starting any physical fitness program. When performing the ZombieFit WOD’s for the first time, make sure that you understand the movements, and can perform them accurately. If at any time your form deteriorates, slow down.

```wod
Run 1/4 mile OR row 250m OR bike 2 miles
(3)
  10 Air Squats
  10 Push Ups
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Pull Ups
```

You may substitute jumping pull-ups for regular pull-ups.

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

Perform 5 rounds for time of:

```wod
(5)
  25m Quadrupedal Movement
  25 Jumping Jacks
  5 Five-Foot Precisions
```

### Avancée — Advanced

Perform 5 rounds for time of:

```wod
(5)
  50m Quadrupedal Movement
  5 Burpees
  10 Five-Foot Precisions
```

### Traceur — Expert

Perform 5 rounds for time of:

```wod
(5)
  50m Quadrupedal Movement
  10 Burpees
  10 Six-Foot Precisions
```

## Cool Down

```wod
100 Push Ups
100 Air Squats
100 Sit Ups
100 Pull Ups
```

Only pick one exercise. When performing the exercises, go to failure on every set.
