Please use caution when starting any physical fitness program. When performing the ZombieFit WOD’s for the first time, make sure that you understand the movements, and can perform them accurately. If at any time your form deteriorates, slow down.

## WOD

### General Warm-up
*Choose one:* 1/4 mile Run OR 250m Row OR 2 miles Bike.

#### Core Workout (All Levels)
```wod
(3)
  10 Air Squats ?
  10 Pushups ?
  10 Situps ?
  10 Pullups ?
```

### Étudiant
Perform 5 rounds for time of:
```wod
(5)
  25m Quadrupedal Movement ?
  25 Jumping Jacks ?
  5 Five-Foot Precisions ?
```

### Avancée
Perform 5 rounds for time of:
```wod
(5)
  50m Quadrupedal Movement ?
  5 Burpees ?
  10 Five-Foot Precisions ?
```

### Traceur
Perform 5 rounds for time of:
```wod
(5)
  50m Quadrupedal Movement ?
  10 Burpees ?
  10 Six-Foot Precisions ?
```

## Cool Down

Perform a total of 100 repetitions (total count) using only one exercise, choosing from the following options: Pushups, Air Squats, Situps, or Pullups. Go to failure on every set.
