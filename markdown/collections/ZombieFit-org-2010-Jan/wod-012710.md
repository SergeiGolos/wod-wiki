**Standing Press Warm-up:**
Start with light weight and move toward 75% of your max effort. This is a warm up, so every set should be somewhat easily performed. If you’re going to failure, lower the weight! If you do not have any experience with the press movement, use a pvc pipe or broomstick (or just the bar) and practice the movement.
*   Reps: 5, 5, 3, 3, 1, 1

## WOD

### Étudiant

Perform for time:
```wod
20 Pushups
10 Press ?lb
20 Air Squats
10 Press ?lb
20 Jumping Pullups
10 Press ?lb
```
*Note: Use just the pvc pipe, broomstick or 45lb bar (depending on your strength and ability with the movement) for all press movements. Concentrate on proper form.*

### Avancée

Perform for time:
```wod
25 Pushups
10 Press ?lb (at 45% max effort)
25 Air Squats
10 Press ?lb (at 45% max effort)
25 Pullups
10 Press ?lb (at 45% max effort)
```
*Note: Perform presses at 45% of your max effort press. If you do not know your max effort press, but can do a handstand push up, perform a press using 40% of your bodyweight.*

### Traceur

Perform for time:
```wod
50 Pushups
10 Press ?lb (at 65% max effort)
50 Air Squats
10 Press ?lb (at 65% max effort)
50 Pullups
10 Press ?lb (at 65% max effort)
```
*Note: Perform presses at 65% of your max effort press. If you do not know your max effort press, but can do a handstand push up, perform a press using 60% of your bodyweight.*

## Cool Down

In as few sets as possible, complete 50 dips.
