## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile OR bike 2 miles OR row 250m, followed by the warm-up rounds below.

Start the press warm-up light and build toward ~75% of your max effort. This is a warm up — keep every set controlled. If you have no experience with the press, use a PVC pipe, broomstick, or just the bar to practice the movement.

```wod
(3)
  50m Quadrupedal Movement
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Double Unders

5 Press
5 Press
3 Press
3 Press
1 Press
1 Press
```

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

Using PVC pipe, broomstick, or 45lb bar; focus on form.

```wod
10 Press // PVC / Broomstick / 45lb
20 Push Ups
10 Press // PVC / Broomstick / 45lb
20 Air Squats
10 Press // PVC / Broomstick / 45lb
20 Jumping Pull Ups
10 Press // PVC / Broomstick / 45lb
```

### Avancée — Advanced

Use ~45% of your max press (or ~40% bodyweight if you can do a handstand push-up).

```wod
10 Press 45% 1RM
25 Push Ups
10 Press 45% 1RM
25 Air Squats
10 Press 45% 1RM
25 Pull Ups
10 Press 45% 1RM
```

### Traceur — Expert

Use ~65% of your max press (or ~60% bodyweight if you can do a handstand push-up).

```wod
10 Press 65% 1RM
50 Push Ups
10 Press 65% 1RM
50 Air Squats
10 Press 65% 1RM
50 Pull Ups
10 Press 65% 1RM
```

## Cool Down

In as few sets as possible, complete 50 dips.

```wod
50 Dips
```
