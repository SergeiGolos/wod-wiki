## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile | row 250m | bike 2 miles.

3 rounds of 10:
- 10 squats
- 10 sit-ups
- 10 double unders (or 50 single unders)
- 10 push-ups

## WOD

```wod

  Air Squats
1:00 Rest
(8) :20 Tabata

1:00 Rest
(8) :20 Tabata
  5-Foot Precisions
1:00 Rest
(8) :20 Tabata
  Jumping Pull Ups
```

## Cool Down

Go home and watch some parkour videos.

## Post

Post total number of repetitions performed to comments.
