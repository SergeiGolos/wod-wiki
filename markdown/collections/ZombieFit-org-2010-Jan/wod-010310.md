## Warm Up

```wod
(3)
  10 Air Squats
  10 Double Unders
  10 Jumping Jacks
  10 Pull Ups
```

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

```wod
(21-15-9)
  Lunges
  Push Ups
  50m Quadrupedal Movement
```

### Avancée — Advanced

```wod
(21-15-9)
  One-Legged Squats (Pistols) - alternating each leg
  Push Ups
  100m Quadrupedal Movement
```

### Traceur — Expert

Perform pistols without alternating: complete all reps on one leg, then all reps on the other.

```wod
(21-15-9)
  One-Legged Squats (Pistols) - same-leg sets (complete all reps on left, then all reps on right)
  Push Ups
  100m Quadrupedal Movement
```
