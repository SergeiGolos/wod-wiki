## Warm Up

**Cardio Choice:**
*   Run 1/4 mile
*   Row 250m
*   Bike 2 miles

**Circuit (3 Rounds):**
Complete 3 rounds of the following circuit:
*   10 Squats
*   10 Push Ups
*   10 Double Unders (or 50 Single Unders)
*   10 Push Ups

## WOD

### Étudiant
In 10 minutes, complete as many rounds as possible of:

```wod
(10) AMRAP / 25m Quadrupedal Movement / 5 Pushups / 10 Situps / 15 Jumping Jacks
```

### Avancée
In 10 minutes, complete as many rounds as possible of:

```wod
(10) AMRAP / 50m Quadrupedal Movement / 5 Burpees / 10 Situps / 15 Jumping Jacks
```

### Traceur
In 20 minutes, complete as many rounds as possible of:

```wod
(20) AMRAP / 50m Quadrupedal Movement / 5 Burpees / 10 Situps / 15 Jumping Jacks
```

## Cool Down

*   50 alternating lunges.
