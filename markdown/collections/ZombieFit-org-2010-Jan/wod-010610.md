## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile | bike 2 miles | row 250m followed by:

```wod
3 rounds
  10 Double Unders
  10 Jumping Jacks
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Dips
```

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

```wod
(10) :60 EMOM
  + 5 Jumping Pull Ups
  + 5 Push Ups
  + 10 Air Squats
```

### Avancée — Advanced

```wod
(10) :60 EMOM
  + 5 Pull Ups
  + 10 Push Ups
  + 15 Air Squats
```

### Traceur — Expert

```wod
(20) :60 EMOM
  + 5 Pull Ups
  + 10 Push Ups
  + 15 Air Squats
```

This WOD was shamelessly stolen from Tri-Cities CrossFit. If you’re in the St. Charles area and looking for a great workout, I highly suggest you check them out.
