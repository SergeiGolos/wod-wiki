## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

Half Tabata: 4 sets per exercise (20s work / 10s rest).

```wod
(4) :30 Tabata
  Air Squats
(4) :30 Tabata
  Push Ups
(4) :30 Tabata
  Sit Ups
(4) :30 Tabata
  Pull Ups
Total Reps ?
```

### Avancée — Advanced

Full Tabata: 8 sets per exercise (20s work / 10s rest). Use substitutions as necessary (e.g., jumping pull-ups, knee push-ups).

```wod
(8) :30 Tabata
  Air Squats
(8) :30 Tabata
  Push Ups
(8) :30 Tabata
  Sit Ups
(8) :30 Tabata
  Pull Ups
Total Reps ?
```

### Traceur — Expert

Full Tabata with strict, full range of motion for every movement.

```wod
(8) :30 Tabata
  Air Squats
(8) :30 Tabata
  Push Ups
(8) :30 Tabata
  Sit Ups
(8) :30 Tabata
  Pull Ups
Total Reps ?
```
