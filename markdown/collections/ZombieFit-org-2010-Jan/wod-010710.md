## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile OR bike 2 miles OR row 250m.

3 rounds of:
- 10 Double Unders
- 10 Push Ups
- 10 Sit Ups
- 10 Dips

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

If you do not have a box to jump on, perform tuck jumps as a substitution.

```wod
(5)
  10 Jumping Pull Ups
  25 Air Squats
  10 24 inch Box Jumps
```

### Avancée — Advanced

```wod
(5)
  10 Pull Ups
  30 Air Squats
  10 24 inch Box Jumps
```

### Traceur — Expert

```wod
(5)
  10 Pull Ups
  10 25lb Dumbbell Squat Cleans
  10 24 inch Box Jumps
```

## Cool Down

Perform 30 repetitions of either a handstand push-up (HSPU) or the corresponding regression/substitute or progressional movement.
