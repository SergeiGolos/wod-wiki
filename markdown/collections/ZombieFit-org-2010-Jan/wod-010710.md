## Warm Up

Thanks to all those who came out to Excel last night!! Had an awesome class with a lot of good progression.

**Warm Up Options:**
*   Run 1/4 mile OR Bike 2 miles OR Row 250m
*   Followed by: (3) rounds for time of: 10 Double Unders, 10 JPush Ups, 10 Sit Ups, and 10 Dips.

## WOD

### Étudiant

```wod
(5)
  10 Jumping Pullups
  25 Air Squats
  10 Box Jumps 24in
```

*Note: If you do not have a box to jump on, perform tuck jumps as a sub.*

### Avancée

```wod
(5)
  10 Pullups
  30 Air Squats
  10 Box Jumps 24in
```

### Traceur

```wod
(5)
  10 Pullups
  10 Dumbbell Squat Cleans ?lb
  10 Box Jumps 24in
```

## Cool Down

Perform 30 repetitions of either a HSPU, or the corresponding sub and/or progressional movement.
