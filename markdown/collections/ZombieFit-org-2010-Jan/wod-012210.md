## Warm Up

Choose one option for warm up:
*   `?mile Run` (1/4 mile)
*   `?m Row` (250m)
*   `?mile Bike` (2 miles)

### General Conditioning Rounds
Perform 3 rounds of the following circuit:
```wod
(3)
  10 Air Squats
  10 Push Ups
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Pull Ups
```
*(Note: Jumping pull ups may substitute for regular pull ups.)*

### Back Squat Strength Work
Perform sets of back squats, working up to your highest 5 rep max.
*   **Sets:** 5, 5, 5 reps.
*   **Scaling/Notes:** If new to the movement, practice with a PVC pipe, broomstick, or 45lb bar. If no rack is available, perform 100 air squats, focusing on form (lumbar spine should not round; break 90 degrees if possible).

## Cool Down
