
## WOD

Everyone: Back squat sets of 5, 5, 5 — try to work up to your highest 5-rep max.  
If new to the back squat, practice with a PVC pipe, broomstick, or the 45lb bar.  
If you do not have access to a squat rack or smith machine, perform 100 air squats, concentrating on form and technique. Your lumbar spine should not be rounded at the bottom of your squat. Break 90 degrees if possible.
