## Warm Up

Warm up options (choose one):
*   Run 1/4 mile
*   Bike 2 miles
*   Row 250m

Followed by 3 rounds of:
*   10 sit ups
*   10 push ups
*   10 air squats
*   10 pull ups (may sub jumping pull ups)

## WOD

### Étudiant

```wod
(10-8-6-4-2-4-6-8-10)
  Burpees
  Sit Ups
```

### Avancée

```wod
(10-8-6-4-2-4-6-8-10)
  Burpees
  Sit Ups
  Double Unders
```

### Traceur

```wod
(10-8-6-4-2-4-6-8-10)
  Burpee Pullups
  Sit Ups
  Double Unders
```

## Cool Down

In the lowest number of sets, perform 50 push ups.
