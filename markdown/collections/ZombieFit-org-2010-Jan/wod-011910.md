## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile | row 250m | bike 2 miles.

```wod
(3)
  50m Quadrupedal Movement
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Pull Ups
```

Substitute jumping pull-ups if needed.

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

```wod
(10) :60 EMOM
  + 25 Single Unders
  + 5 Push Ups
  + 5 Air Squats
```

### Avancée — Advanced

```wod
(15) :60 EMOM
  + 5 Double Unders
  + 5 Push Ups
  + 10 Air Squats
```

### Traceur — Expert

```wod
(20) :60 EMOM
  + 5 Double Unders
  + 10 Push Ups
  + 10 Air Squats
```

## Cool Down

```wod
100 Alternating Leg Lunges
```

*This means that for every minute you complete the stated exercises. You only have that minute to complete the exercises. If you cannot complete the set in that minute time frame, scale down one of the exercise repetition counts so that you can complete the entire sequence of exercises in one minute.*
