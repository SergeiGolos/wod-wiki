## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile OR bike 2 miles OR row 250m.

3 rounds of 10:
- 25m quadrupedal movement
- 5 foot precisions
- Push Ups
- Double Unders

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

```wod
(20) :60 EMOM
  + 3 Push Ups
  + 5 Sit Ups
  + 5 Air Squats
```

### Avancée — Advanced

```wod
(5) :60 EMOM
  + 5 Push Ups
  + 5 Sit Ups
  + 5 Air Squats
(5) :60 EMOM
  + 10 Push Ups
  + 5 Sit Ups
  + 5 Air Squats
(5) :60 EMOM
  + 5 Push Ups
  + 10 Sit Ups
  + 5 Air Squats
(5) :60 EMOM
  + 5 Push Ups
  + 5 Sit Ups
  + 10 Air Squats
```

### Traceur — Expert

Same as Avancée, plus perform 2 burpees after each round. If you cannot complete all movements within the minute, stop the exercise, complete 2 burpees, then move on to the next minute’s repetitions.

```wod
(5) :60 EMOM
  + 5 Push Ups
  + 5 Sit Ups
  + 5 Air Squats
  + 2 Burpees
(5) :60 EMOM
  + 10 Push Ups
  + 5 Sit Ups
  + 5 Air Squats
  + 2 Burpees
(5) :60 EMOM
  + 5 Push Ups
  + 10 Sit Ups
  + 5 Air Squats
  + 2 Burpees
(5) :60 EMOM
  + 5 Push Ups
  + 5 Sit Ups
  + 10 Air Squats
  + 2 Burpees
```
