## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile OR row 250m OR bike 1/2 mile.

```wod
(3)
  25m Quadrupedal Movement
  10 Push Ups
  10 Pull Ups / Jumping Pull Ups
  10 I Suck At This
```

Perform a movement you "suck at." If that movement is already in the warm up, perform 15 repetitions of that movement instead of 10. For example, if you suck at sit-ups and there are sit-ups in the warm up, do 3 rounds of 15 instead of 3 rounds of 10. If you suck at handstand push-ups, perform 3 rounds of 10 HSPU (or an appropriate progression) and add that to the warm up.

## WOD

Skill work: Max effort precision/broad jump. Focus on form and landing — take the jump UP and stick the landing (do not tip or fall backwards).

```wod
5 Precision Jumps 4ft
5 Precision Jumps 5ft
5 Precision Jumps 6ft
5 Precision Jumps increase distance until max
Max Effort Precision Jump ?ft
10 Max Effort Broad Jumps
```
