## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile OR row 250m OR bike 0.5 miles.

```wod
(3)
  10 Air Squats
  10 Push Ups
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Pull Ups or Jumping Pull Ups
  10 Movement You "Suck At" (perform a movement you suck at)
```

Perform a movement that you "suck at." If that movement is already in the warm up, do 15 repetitions of that movement instead of 10. For example, if you suck at sit ups and they are in the warm up, do 3 rounds of 15 instead of 10. If you suck at handstand push ups, perform 3 rounds of 10 handstand push ups (or progressions) and add that to the warm up.

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

```wod
(20) :60 EMOM
  + 2 Push Ups
  + 4 Air Squats
  + 6 Jumping Jacks
  If you do not complete the repetitions in the minute, stop the clock and perform 2 Burpees. Then restart the clock and continue.
```

### Avancée — Advanced

```wod
(20) :60 EMOM
  + 5 Push Ups
  + 7 Air Squats
  + 9 Jumping Jacks
  If you do not complete the repetitions in the minute, stop the clock and perform 2 Burpees. Then restart the clock and continue.
```

### Traceur — Expert

```wod
(20) :60 EMOM
  + 2 Burpees
  + 5 Push Ups
  + 7 Air Squats
  + 9 Jumping Jacks
  If you do not complete the repetitions in the minute, stop the clock and perform 5 Burpees. Then restart the clock and continue.
```
