## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile OR bike 1/2 mile OR row 250m followed by:

```wod
(3)
  25m Quadrupedal Movement
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Push Ups
```

## WOD

Three rounds for time.

### Étudiant — Beginner

```wod
(3)
  50m Ground Kongs
  25 Air Squats
  25 Jumping Pull Ups
```

### Avancée — Advanced

```wod
(3)
  50m Ground Kongs
  50 Air Squats
  50 Jumping Pull Ups
```

### Traceur — Expert

```wod
(3)
  50m Ground Kongs
  50 Air Squats
  25 Pull Ups
```

## Cool Down

```wod
(3)
  Handstand Hold Against Wall to failure
```
