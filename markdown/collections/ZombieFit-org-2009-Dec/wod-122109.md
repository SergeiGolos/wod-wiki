## Warm Up

Warm up options include running 1/4 mile, biking 2 miles, or rowing 250m. This should be followed by 3 rounds of:
*   10 Double Unders
*   10 Air Squats
*   10 Pull Ups
*   10 Sit Ups

## WOD

### Étudiant

Back squat skill work. Use very light weight or a broomstick/pvc pipe and practice the squat movement. Ensure feet are shoulder width apart, toes pointed slightly outside, and track knees over toes when lowering into the squat position. Concentrate on form and technique; there should be full hip activation at the top of the squat.

```wod
100 Back Squats (Skill Work)
```

### Avancée

Back squat skill work is performed for max effort. If you do not have access to a rack or barbell with appropriate weight, perform 500 air squats for time. When performing air squats, ensure depth below 90 degrees and full hip activation at the top. Do not cheat.

```wod
1-7 Back Squats (Max Effort)
OR
500 Air Squats For Time
```
