## Warm Up

3 rounds of:
- 10 Push Ups
- 10 Sit Ups
- 10 Squats
- 10 Pull Ups

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

Choose one exercise from the referenced video (examples include wall burpees). Take your time and make sure you understand the movement prior to performing it. Practice a few reps before starting your sets.

```wod
(3)
  400m Run
  5 Wall Burpees
```
