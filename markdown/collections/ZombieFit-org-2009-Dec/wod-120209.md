## Warm Up

3 rounds of:
*   10 Push Ups
*   10 Sit Ups
*   10 Squats
*   10 Pull Ups

## WOD

### Étudiant

This workout is performed for time. The athlete runs 400m followed by 5 repetitions of a specified exercise (e.g., Wall Burpees).

```wod
(3)
  400m ?m
  5 ? reps (Wall Burpees or other video exercise)
```

*Note: Video courtesy of Eva T. The athlete should practice the movement several times before starting.*
