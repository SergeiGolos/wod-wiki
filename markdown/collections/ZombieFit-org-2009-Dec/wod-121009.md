## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile | bike 2 miles | row 500m followed by:

```wod
3 rounds
  10 Air Squats
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Jumping Pull Ups
```

May substitute push-ups if you don’t have a pull-up bar.

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

```wod
(21-15-9)

  Air Squats
```
