## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile OR bike 2 miles OR row 500m.

```wod
3 rounds
  10 Air Squats
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Jumping Pull Ups
```

May substitute push ups if you don’t have a pull up bar.

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

```wod
(5)
  50m Quadrupedal Movement
  5 Five-Foot Precisions
  10 Dips
```

### Avancée — Advanced

```wod
(5)
  50m Quadrupedal Movement
  5 Seven-Foot Precisions
  20 Dips
```

### Traceur — Expert

```wod
(5)
  100m Quadrupedal Movement
  5 Eight-Foot Precisions
  30 Dips
```

## Cool Down

```wod
25 Pull Ups
```

May be dead hang, kipping, jumping, or assisted.
