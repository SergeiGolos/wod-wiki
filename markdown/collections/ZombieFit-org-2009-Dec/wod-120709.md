## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile OR bike 2 miles OR row 500m followed by:

```wod
(3)
  10 Push Ups
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Air Squats
  10 Pull Ups
```

## WOD

Stabilize yourself: find a rail!

### Étudiant — Beginner

```wod
(1)
  10 Skull Crushers
  10 Tuck Jumps (silent landing)
  10 second L-Sit
```

### Avancée — Advanced

```wod
(2)
  10 Skull Crushers
  10 Tuck Jumps (silent landing)
  10 second L-Sit
```

### Traceur — Expert

```wod
(3)
  10 Skull Crushers
  10 Tuck Jumps (silent landing)
  10 second L-Sit
```

## Cool Down

```wod
50 Jumping Jacks
```
