## WOD

### Étudiant (Beginner)

In 10 minutes AMRAP of:
```wod
(AMRAP 10m)
  5 Push Ups
  10 Sit Ups
  15 Air Squats
  50m Quadrupedal Movement
```

### Avancée (Intermediate)

In 10 minutes AMRAP of:
```wod
(AMRAP 10m)
  5 Push Ups
  10 Sit Ups
  15 Air Squats
  Vault/100m Quadrupedal Movement
```
*(Note: After each round (if possible) perform some kind of vault. If you cannot perform a vault, perform 100m quadrupedal movement.)*

### Traceur (Advanced)

In 20 minutes AMRAP of:
```wod
(AMRAP 20m)
  5 Push Ups
  10 Sit Ups
  15 Air Squats
  Vault/100m Quadrupedal Movement
```
*(Note: After each round (if possible) perform some kind of vault. If you cannot perform a vault, perform 100m quadrupedal movement.)*
