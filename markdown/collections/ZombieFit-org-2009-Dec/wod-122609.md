## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile | bike 2 miles | row 250m followed by:

3 rounds of:
- 10 Air Squats
- 10 Double Unders
- 10 Push Ups
- 10 Jumping Jacks

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

```wod
(5)
  10 Jumping Pull Ups
  10 Sit Ups
  10 12in Box Jumps
```

### Avancée — Advanced

```wod
(5)
  10 Pull Ups
  10 Sit Ups
  10 24in Box Jumps
```

### Traceur — Expert

```wod
(5)
  10 L-Sit Pull Ups
  10 Knees To Elbows
  10 36in Box Jumps
```

## Cool Down

```wod
200m Reverse Quadrupedal Movement
```
