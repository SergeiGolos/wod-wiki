## Warm Up

Warm up options include running 1/4 mile, biking 2 miles, or rowing 250m. This should be followed by 3 rounds of:
*   10 Air Squats
*   10 Double Unders
*   10 Push Ups
*   10 Jumping Jacks

## WOD

### Étudiant

```wod
(5)
  10 jumping pull ups
  10 sit ups
  10 twelve inch box jumps
```

### Avancée

```wod
(5)
  10 pull ups
  10 sit ups
  10 twenty-four inch box jumps
```

### Traceur

```wod
(5)
  10 L-sit pull ups
  10 knees to elbows
  10 thirty-six inch box jumps
```

## Cool Down

200m reverse quadrupedal movement.
