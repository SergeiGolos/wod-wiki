## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile or bike 2 miles or row 250m.

Followed by 3 rounds of:
- 10 Air Squats
- 10 Sit Ups
- 10 Push Ups
- 10 Pull Ups

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

```wod
(3)
  5 Lunges with each leg
  100m Quadrupedal Movement
```

### Avancée — Advanced

```wod
(5)
  10 Lunges with each leg
  100m Quadrupedal Movement
```

### Traceur — Expert

```wod
(5)
  10 Pistols with each leg
  100m Quadrupedal Movement
```
