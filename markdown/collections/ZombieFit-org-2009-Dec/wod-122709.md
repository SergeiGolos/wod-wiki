## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile or bike 2 miles or row 250m followed by:

```wod
(3)
  10 Air Squats
  10 Double Unders
  10 Jumping Jacks
  10 Pull Ups
```

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

Complete as many rounds as possible in 10 minutes of:

```wod
10:00 AMRAP
  5 Foot Precision
  50m Quadrupedal Movement
  10 Push Ups
```

### Avancée — Advanced

Complete as many rounds as possible in 15 minutes of:

```wod
15:00 AMRAP
  7 Foot Precision
  50m Quadrupedal Movement
  10 Push Ups
```

### Traceur — Expert

Complete as many rounds as possible in 20 minutes of:

```wod
20:00 AMRAP
  7 Foot Precision
  50m Quadrupedal Movement
  10 Push Ups
```

## Cool Down

```wod
25 Pull Ups (kipping, dead hang or jumping)
```
