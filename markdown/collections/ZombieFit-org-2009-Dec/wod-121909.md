## Warm Up

Warm up options include:
*   Run 1/4 mile
*   Bike 2 miles
*   Row 250m

Followed by:
(3) rounds for time:
*   10 Double Unders
*   10 Air Squats
*   10 Pull Ups
*   10 Sit Ups

## WOD

### Étudiant

```wod
100 Pushups For Time
```
*Scaling Note:* If unable to complete a full pushup, substitute with 200 pushups on knees. Remember to keep the back flat and butt down regardless of scaling.

### Avancée

```wod
250 Pushups For Time
```

### Traceur

```wod
400 Pushups For Time
```
