## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile OR bike 2 miles OR row 250m followed by:

```wod
(3)
  10 Double Unders
  10 Air Squats
  10 Pull Ups
  10 Sit Ups
```

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

If you cannot do a full push, do 200 push ups on your knees. Remember to keep your back flat and your butt down regardless of how it is scaled.

```wod
100 Push Ups
```

### Avancée — Advanced

```wod
250 Push Ups
```

### Traceur — Expert

```wod
400 Push Ups
```
