## WOD

**Precision Ladder:** Continuously perform precision jumps, increasing the number of repetitions each minute until failure.

### Étudiant
Stand in place and mark your starting spot. Find a second spot approximately 4 feet away to jump toward.
```wod
Ladder: Continuous Precision Jumps (Target distance: ~4ft)
(1) :? Reps / (2) :? Reps / (3) :? Reps ... until failure
```

### Avancée
Pick a precision length of 65% of your max broad jump. If possible, incorporate jumping over a gap or up to a ledge.
```wod
Ladder: Continuous Precision Jumps (Target distance: 65% Max Broad Jump)
(1) :? Reps / (2) :? Reps / (3) :? Reps ... until failure
```

### Traceur
Pick a precision length of 85% of your max broad jump. If possible, incorporate jumping over a gap or up to a ledge.
```wod
Ladder: Continuous Precision Jumps (Target distance: 85% Max Broad Jump)
(1) :? Reps / (2) :? Reps / (3) :? Reps ... until failure
```
