## Warm Up

Choose one option for the initial cardio, followed by two rounds of the following circuit:
*   Run 1/4 mile OR Bike 2 miles OR Row 500m.
*   **Circuit:** 250m quadrupedal movement + 50 jumping jacks (Repeat 2 times).

## WOD

### Étudiant

Perform 3 total rounds of the following circuit, focusing on max effort for each 30-second interval. There is no rest between exercises within a round. Take a 2 minute break after completing Round 1. Count your total reps across all three rounds.

```wod
(3)
  :30 Pushups ?
  :30 Situps ?
  :30 Air Squats ?
  :30 Jumping Pullups ?
```

### Avancée

Perform 5 total rounds of the following circuit, focusing on max effort for each 30-second interval. Take a 2 minute rest break between each round. Count your total reps across all five rounds.

```wod
(5)
  :30 Pushups ?
  :30 Situps ?
  :30 Air Squats ?
  :30 Pullups ?
```

### Traceur

Perform 5 total rounds of the following circuit, focusing on max effort for each 30-second interval. Take a 2 minute rest break between each round. Count your total reps across all five rounds.

```wod
(5)
  :30 Handstand Pushups ?
  :30 Situps ?
  :30 Air Squats ?
  :30 Muscle Ups ?
```
