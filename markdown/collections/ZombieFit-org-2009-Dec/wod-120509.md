## Warm Up

Warm up options include running 1/4 mile, biking 2 miles, or rowing 500m. This should be followed by 3 rounds of:
*   10 Air Squats
*   10 Sit Ups
*   10 Jumping Pull Ups (May substitute with Push Ups if a pull up bar is unavailable).

## WOD

### Étudiant

Complete as many rounds in 10 minutes as possible (AMRAP) of:

```wod
(10 min AMRAP)
  5 jumping pull ups
  10 push ups
  15 air squats
```

### Avancée

Complete as many rounds in 10 minutes as possible (AMRAP) of:

```wod
(10 min AMRAP)
  5 pull ups
  10 push ups
  15 air squats
```

### Traceur

Complete as many rounds as possible in 20 minutes (AMRAP) of:

```wod
(20 min AMRAP)
  5 pull ups
  10 push ups
  15 air squats
```

## Cool Down

Perform 100 jumping jacks.
