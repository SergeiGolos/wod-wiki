## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile | bike 2 miles | row 500m followed by:

```wod
(3)
  10 Air Squats
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Jumping Pull Ups
```

May substitute jumping pull-ups with push-ups if you don't have a pull-up bar.
