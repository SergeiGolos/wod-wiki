## Warm Up

```wod
Run 1/4 mile OR Bike 1/2 mile OR Row 250m
(3)
  10 Air Squats
  10 Push Ups
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Air Squats
  10 Pull Ups
```

May substitute jumping pull-ups if needed.

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

Make sure your knee touches the ground on every lunge.

```wod
200m Lunge Walk
100m Lunge Walk Backwards
```

### Avancée — Advanced

Make sure your knee touches the ground on every lunge.

```wod
300m Lunge Walk
200m Lunge Walk Backwards
```

### Traceur — Expert

Make sure your knee touches the ground on every lunge.

```wod
400m Lunge Walk
300m Lunge Walk Backwards
```
