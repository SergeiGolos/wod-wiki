## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile | bike 1/2 mile | row 250m.

```wod
3 rounds
  10 Air Squats
  10 Push Ups
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Air Squats
  10 Pull Ups
```

(Scale pull-ups to jumping pull-ups if needed.)

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

For time, complete the following:

```wod
(15-9-7)
  5-Foot Precisions
  Dips
  Sit Ups
  100m Sprint | Bike 1/4 mile | Row 125m
```

### Avancée — Advanced

For time, complete the following:

```wod
(21-15-9)
  6-Foot Precisions
  Dips
  Sit Ups
  100m Sprint | Bike 1/4 mile | Row 125m
```

### Traceur — Expert

For time, complete the following:

```wod
(21-15-9)
  6-Foot Precisions
  Dips
  Elbows to Knees
  100m Sprint | Bike 1/4 mile | Row 125m
```

## Cool Down

```wod
3 Plank Hold to Failure
```
