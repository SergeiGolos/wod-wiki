## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile or bike 1/2 miles or row 250m followed by:

```wod
(3)
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Push Ups
  10 Air Squats
  10 Pull Ups / Handstand Push Ups
```

## WOD

On the minute, for 10 minutes, complete the following. Complete the prescribed movements at the start of each minute, then rest the remainder of the minute.

### Étudiant — Beginner

```wod
(10) :60 EMOM
  + 100m Sprint
```

### Avancée — Advanced

```wod
(10) :60 EMOM
  + 100m Sprint
  + 10 Air Squats
```

### Traceur — Expert

```wod
(10) :60 EMOM
  + 100m Sprint
  + 10 Air Squats
  + 5 Push Ups
```
