## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile, bike 1/2 mile, | row 250 m.

```wod
(3)
  10 Air Squats
  10 Push Ups
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Air Squats
  10 Pull Ups // may substitute with jumping
50 Safety Rolls
```

This is skill work, so do not rush the exercises. Take your time.

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

```wod
(21-15-9)
  Jumping Pull Ups
  Push Ups
  Air Squats
```

### Avancée — Advanced

```wod
(21-15-9)
  Jumping Pull Ups
  Safety Roll
  Push Ups
  Safety Roll
  Air Squats
  Safety Roll
  5 Burpees
```

### Traceur — Expert

```wod
(21-15-9)
  Jumping Pull Ups
  2 Safety Rolls
  Vault
  Push Ups
  2 Safety Rolls
  Vault
  Air Squats
  2 Safety Rolls
  Vault
  5 Burpees
```

ONLY PERFORM SAFETY ROLLS IN THE WOD IF YOU ARE CONFIDENT THAT YOU ARE DOING THEM CORRECTLY. If at ANY time you are performing a roll and it hurts any area of your body YOU ARE DOING IT WRONG. Safety rolls are a fundamental skill in parkour, but there is some risk of injury. Check out our tutorial and practice the movement before you do the WOD.
