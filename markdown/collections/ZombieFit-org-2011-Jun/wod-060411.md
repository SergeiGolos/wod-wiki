## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile | bike 1/2 mile | row 250m.

3 rounds of:
- 10 Air Squats
- 10 Push Ups
- 10 Sit Ups
- 10 Air Squats
- 10 Pull Ups (may substitute jumping pull-ups if needed)

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

In as few sets as possible, complete:

```wod
30 Push Ups
30 Air Squats
30 Jumping Pull Ups
30 Sit Ups
```

### Avancée — Advanced

In as few sets as possible, complete:

```wod
50 Push Ups
50 Air Squats
50 Jumping Pull Ups
50 Sit Ups
```

### Traceur — Expert

In the fewest sets possible, complete:

```wod
75 Push Ups
75 Air Squats
75 Jumping Pull Ups
75 Sit Ups
```

## Cool Down

2x100m sprint
