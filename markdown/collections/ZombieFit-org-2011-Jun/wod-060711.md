## Warm Up

Warm Up: run 1/4 mile, bike 1/2 mile, or row 250 m.

3 rounds of:
- 10 Air Squats
- 10 Push Ups
- 10 Sit Ups
- 10 Air Squats
- 10 Pull Ups (may substitute with jumping pull-ups)

## WOD

Precision Ladder
With a continuously running clock do one precision the first minute, two precisions the second minute, three precisions the third minute… continuing as long as you are able. Use as many sets each minute as needed.

### Étudiant — Beginner

Stand in place and mark the spot where your feet are. Then find a spot (either visually or by marking it) approximately 4 feet away. That spot is where you’ll be precisioning to for the above WOD.

```wod
:60 Intervals
  1 Precision // add 1 Precision each minute; continue until unable
```

### Avancée — Advanced

Pick a precision length of 85% of your max broad jump. If possible, precision over a gap or up to a ledge.

```wod
:60 Intervals
  1 Precision // add 1 Precision each minute; continue until unable
```

### Traceur — Expert

Max effort every precision. If possible, precision over a gap or to a ledge.

```wod
:60 Intervals
  1 Precision // add 1 Precision each minute; continue until unable
```

## Cool Down

In the smallest number of sets possible, complete 50 push ups and 50 pull ups. (May substitute with chin-ups, jumping pull-ups, or L-sit pull-ups depending on ability.)
