## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile, bike 1/2 mile, | row 250m.

3 rounds of:
- 10 Parkour squats | air squats
- 10 Sit ups
- 50 ft quadrupedal movement

Skill Work: Precision practice. Perform 25 (Étudiant) or 50 (Avancée and Traceur) precisions at 65% max effort. Concentrate on your form. Do not bounce out of your landing — stick the landing, pause, stand up, and count to 2. This is not a conditioning exercise; take the time needed to perform these movements correctly.

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

For time, complete 15-9-7 repetitions of the following. After each round, complete a 100m sprint or bike 1/4 mile or row 125m.

```wod
(15-9-7)
  5-Foot Precisions
  Dips
  Sit Ups
  100m Run | Bike 1/4 mile | Row 125m
```

### Avancée — Advanced

For time, complete 21-15-9 repetitions of the following. After each round, complete a 100m sprint or bike 1/4 mile or row 125m.

```wod
(21-15-9)
  6-Foot Precisions
  Dips
  Sit Ups
  100m Run | Bike 1/4 mile | Row 125m
```

### Traceur — Expert

For time, complete 21-15-9 repetitions of the following. After each round, complete a 100m sprint or bike 1/4 mile or row 125m.

```wod
(21-15-9)
  6-Foot Precisions
  Dips
  Elbows to Knees
  100m Run | Bike 1/4 mile | Row 125m
```

## Cool Down

Perform a plank hold to failure, 3 rounds.
