Run 1/4 mile or bike 1 mile or row 250m followed by:

```wod
3 rounds
  10 Air Squats
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Push Ups
  10 Pull Ups
```

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

On the minute, for 10 minutes, complete the following repetitions:

```wod
(10) :60 EMOM
  + 3 Push Ups
  + 5 Jumping Pull Ups // or 5 Sit Ups
  + 9 Air Squats
```

### Avancée — Advanced

On the minute, for 15 minutes, complete the following repetitions:

```wod
(15) :60 EMOM
  + 5 Push Ups
  + 7 Pull Ups // or 7 Sit Ups
  + 9 Air Squats
```

### Traceur — Expert

On the minute, for 20 minutes, complete the following repetitions:

```wod
(20) :60 EMOM
  + 5 Handstand Push Ups
  + 7 Pull Ups // or 7 Sit Ups
  + 9 Air Squats
```

If you fail to complete the round in the given minute, stop the clock, perform 5 burpees, then start the clock and continue the next minute’s movements.
