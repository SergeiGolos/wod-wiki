## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile | row 250m | bike 1/2 mile.

3 rounds of:
- 25m quadrupedal movement
- 10 push ups
- 10 sit ups
- 10 pull ups | jumping pull ups
- 10 "I suck at this" (perform a movement you suck at)

If the movement you suck at is already in the warm up, perform 15 repetitions of that movement instead of 10. For example, if you suck at sit ups and sit ups are in the warm up, do 3 rounds of 15. If you suck at handstand push ups, perform 3 rounds of 10 handstand push ups (or a progression) and add that to the warm up.

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

```wod
(21-15-9)
  18 Inch Box Jumps
  Jumping Pull Ups
```

### Avancée — Advanced

```wod
(21-15-9)
  24 Inch Box Jumps
  Pull Ups
```

### Traceur — Expert

```wod
(21-15-9)
  24 Inch Box Jumps
  Pull Ups
  Double Unders
```

If you do not have a box available, perform parkour squats (tuck jumps) as a substitute for box jumps.

## Cool Down

Hold a plank position to failure twice.
