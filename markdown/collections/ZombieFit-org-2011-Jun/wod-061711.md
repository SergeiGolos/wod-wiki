## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile or bike 2 miles or row 250m, followed by:

```wod
(3)
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Push Ups
  10 Air Squats
  10 Pull Ups or Handstand Push Ups
```

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

```wod
(10-8-6-4-2-4-6-8-10)
  Burpees
  Sit Ups
```

### Avancée — Advanced

```wod
(10-8-6-4-2-4-6-8-10)
  Burpees
  Sit Ups
  Double Unders
```

### Traceur — Expert

```wod
(10-8-6-4-2-4-6-8-10)
  Burpee Pull Ups
  Sit Ups
  Double Unders
```

## Cool Down

```wod
100m Walking Lunges
```
