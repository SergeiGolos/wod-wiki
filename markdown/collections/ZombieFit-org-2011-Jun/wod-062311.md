## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile OR row 250m OR bike 1/2 mile.

```wod
(3)
  25m Quadrupedal Movement
  10 Dips
  10 Five-Foot Precisions
  10 Push Ups
```

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

Complete as many rounds as possible in 20 minutes of:

```wod
20:00 AMRAP
  5 Jumping Pull Ups
  10 Push Ups
  15 Air Squats
```

### Avancée — Advanced

Complete as many rounds as possible in 20 minutes of:

```wod
20:00 AMRAP
  5 Pull Ups
  10 Push Ups
  15 Air Squats
```

### Traceur — Expert

Complete as many rounds as possible in 20 minutes of:

```wod
20:00 AMRAP
  5 L-Sit Pull Ups
  10 Push Ups
  15 Air Squats
```

## Cool Down

```wod
10 Handstand Push Ups
```

Concentrate on your form. If possible do them free-standing.
