## Warm Up

```wod
Run 1/4 mile
Bike 1/2 mile
Row 250m
(3)
  10 Air Squats
  10 Push Ups
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Air Squats
  10 Pull Ups
```

Pull-ups may be substituted with jumping pull-ups if needed.

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

On the minute, for ten minutes, complete the following:

```wod
(10) :60 EMOM
  + 2 Burpees
  + 4 Push Ups
  + 6 Sit Ups
```

### Avancée — Advanced

On the minute, for ten minutes, complete the following:

```wod
(10) :60 EMOM
  + 3 Burpees
  + 5 Push Ups
  + 7 Sit Ups
```

### Traceur — Expert

On the minute, for ten minutes, complete the following:

```wod
(10) :60 EMOM
  + 5 Burpees
  + 7 Push Ups
  + 9 Sit Ups
```

If you cannot complete the movements within the minute time frame, you must immediately stop what you are doing and start the next round from the beginning with an additional 2 burpees. For example, if you are doing the Avancée level and miss a round, you would start the next round with 5 burpees.
