## Warm Up

ZombieFit was just entered into a drawing for the chance to win a $50,000 grant. If you have a moment, please take the time to go to this link and vote for us.

Run 1/4 mile, bike 1/2 mile, or row 250m.

3 rounds of:
- 10 Air Squats
- 10 Push Ups
- 10 Sit Ups
- 10 Air Squats
- 10 Pull Ups (may sub with jumping if needed)
