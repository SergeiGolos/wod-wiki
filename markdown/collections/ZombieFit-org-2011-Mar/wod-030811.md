## Warm Up

Warm Up: Run 1/4 mile or bike 2 miles or row 250m, followed by 3 rounds of:
*   10 sit ups
*   10 push ups
*   10 air squats
*   10 pull ups (or handstand push ups)

## WOD

WOD 348 - Complete as many rounds as possible in 20 minutes.

### Étudiant

```wod
(20) AMRAP / 2 Pushups / 5 Situps / 7 Air Squats / 10 Jumping Jacks
```

### Avancée

```wod
(20) AMRAP / 2 Pushups / 5 Situps / 7 Air Squats / 10 Jumping Pullups
```

*Note: Jumping pull ups may sub with jump-rope or tuck jumps.*

### Traceur

```wod
(20) AMRAP / 5 Pushups / 7 Situps / 9 Air Squats / 12 Jumping Pullups
```

*Note: Jumping pull ups may sub with jump-rope or tuck jumps.*
