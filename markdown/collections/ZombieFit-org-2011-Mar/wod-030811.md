## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile or bike 2 miles or row 250m followed by:

3 rounds of:
- 10 Sit Ups
- 10 Push Ups
- 10 Air Squats
- 10 Pull Ups or Handstand Push Ups

## WOD

20:00 AMRAP — Complete as many rounds as possible in 20 minutes.

### Étudiant — Beginner

```wod
20:00 AMRAP
  2 Push Ups
  5 Sit Ups
  7 Air Squats
  10 Jumping Jacks
```

### Avancée — Advanced

May substitute jump-rope or tuck jumps for jumping pull ups.

```wod
20:00 AMRAP
  2 Push Ups
  5 Sit Ups
  7 Air Squats
  10 Jumping Pull Ups
```

### Traceur — Expert

May substitute jump-rope or tuck jumps for jumping pull ups.

```wod
20:00 AMRAP
  5 Push Ups
  7 Sit Ups
  9 Air Squats
  12 Jumping Pull Ups
```
