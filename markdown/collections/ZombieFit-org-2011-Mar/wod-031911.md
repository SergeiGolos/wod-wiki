## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile | row 250m | bike 1/2 mile.

```wod
(3)
  25m Quadrupedal Movement
  10 Push Ups
  10 Pull Ups | Jumping Pull Ups
  10 I Suck At This // perform a movement you suck at
```

Perform a movement that you suck at. If it is already in the warm up, perform 15 repetitions of that movement instead of 10. For instance, if you suck at sit ups and there are sit ups in the warm up, do 3 rounds of 15 instead of 3 rounds of 10. If you suck at handstand push ups, perform 3 rounds of 10 handstand push ups (or their progression) and add that to the warm up.

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

For time, and in as few sets as possible complete:

```wod
150 Air Squats
```

### Avancée — Advanced

For time, and in as few sets as possible complete:

```wod
200 Air Squats
```

### Traceur — Expert

For time, and in as few sets as possible complete:

```wod
250 Air Squats
```

## Post

Post # of sets to comments.

*This means that you must go to failure on each set, and complete the required repetitions as quickly as possible. Do not cheat! Achieve full squat depth as well as make sure to fully activate the hips when finishing the squat (standing up).*
