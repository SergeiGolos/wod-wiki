## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile OR row 250m OR bike 2 miles.

```wod
(3)
  25m Quadrupedal Movement
  10 Push Ups
  10 Pull Ups or Jumping Pull Ups
  10 Isuckatthis
```

Perform a movement that you "suck at." If that movement is already in the warm up, perform 15 repetitions of that movement instead of 10. For example, if you suck at sit ups and sit ups are in the warm up, do 3 rounds of 15 instead of 10. If you suck at handstand push ups, perform 3 rounds of 10 handstand push ups (or a progression) and add that to the warm up.

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner
15-7-9 repetitions for time:

```wod
(15-7-9)
  18 inch Box Jumps
  Jumping Pull Ups
  Alternating Leg Lunges
```

### Avancée — Advanced
21-15-9 repetitions for time:

```wod
(21-15-9)
  24 inch Box Jumps
  Pull Ups
  Alternating Leg Lunges
```

### Traceur — Expert
21-15-9 repetitions for time:

```wod
(21-15-9)
  30 inch Box Jumps
  Pull Ups
  Alternating Leg Pistols
```

Substitute tuck jumps for box jumps if you don’t have something to jump to.
