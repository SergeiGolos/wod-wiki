## Warm Up

Warm Up options: run 1/4 mile OR row 250m OR bike 2 miles.

Perform 3 rounds of: 25m quadrupedal movement, 10 push ups, 10 pull ups/jumping pull ups, and 10 isuckatthis**
*Note:* **isuckatthis** = perform a movement that you suck at. If it is already in the warm up, then perform 15 repetitions of that movement instead of 10. For instance, if I suck at sit ups, and there are sit ups in the warm up, I’ll do 3 rounds of 15 instead of 3 rounds of ten. If I suck at handstand push ups, I’ll perform 3 rounds of 10 handstand push ups (or their progression) and add that to the warm up.

## WOD

### Étudiant
(15-7-9)
```wod
18 inch box jumps (sub with tuck jumps if you don't have something to jump to)
jumping pull ups
alternating leg lunges
```

### Avancée
(21-15-9)
```wod
24 inch box jumps (sub with tuck jumps if you don't have something to jump to)
pull ups
alternating leg lunges
```

### Traceur
(21-15-9)
```wod
30 inch box jumps (sub with tuck jumps if you don't have something to jump to)
pull ups
alternating leg pistols
```
