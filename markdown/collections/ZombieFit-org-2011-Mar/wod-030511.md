## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile | QM 50m | row 250m.

```wod
(3)
  10 Squats
  10 Tuck Jumps
  10 Five-Foot Precisions
```

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

3 rounds of timed intervals. Repeat the listed movements as many times as possible during each interval.

```wod
(3)
  :60
    Tuck Jumps
    5-Foot Precision
  :120
    Safety Roll
    5 Push Ups
  :180
    10m Quadrupedal Movement
    10 Sit Ups
```

### Avancée — Advanced

3 rounds of extended timed intervals. Repeat movements as many times as possible during each interval.

```wod
(3)
  :120
    Tuck Jumps
    6-Foot Precision
  :180
    Safety Roll
    5 Push Ups
  :240
    15m Quadrupedal Movement
    15 Sit Ups
```

### Traceur — Expert

3 rounds of longer timed intervals. Repeat movements as many times as possible during each interval.

```wod
(3)
  :180
    Tuck Jumps
    7-Foot Precision
  :240
    Safety Roll
    5 Push Ups
  :300
    20m Quadrupedal Movement
    20 Sit Ups
10 Hand Stand Push Ups
```
