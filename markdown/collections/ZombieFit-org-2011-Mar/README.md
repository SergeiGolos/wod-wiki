# ZombieFit.org Mar 2011

Welcome to the March collection! This month's programming focuses on building explosive endurance and mastering foundational movements, blending classic CrossFit conditioning with raw urban agility inspired by parkour principles. Expect a mix of AMRAPs, For Time efforts, and skill-based challenges designed to keep you moving through the concrete jungle.

## 🗓️ Monthly Schedule Overview

This month's workouts were spread across several key dates, focusing on varied modalities from running and biking to pure bodyweight strength circuits.

### Week 1 (Mar 4)
*   **WOD 346:** A foundational circuit emphasizing high-volume bodyweight work (Pushups, Sit Ups, Jumping Jacks). Focus was placed on completing the entire workout in as few sets as possible.

### Week 2 (Mar 7 - Mar 9)
*   **WOD 347:** Introduced speed and precision with a mix of sprints and specific parkour movements (five-foot precisions).
*   **WOD 348:** A classic AMRAP format over 20 minutes, testing muscular endurance across multiple bodyweight stations.
*   **WOD 349:** Focused on high-rep volume for both strength (dips) and technical skills (precisions/lunges), emphasizing efficiency in completing sets.

### Week 3 (Mar 12)
*   **WOD 350:** A comprehensive warm-up followed by a movement challenge, requiring athletes to incorporate their weakest physical skill into the routine for maximum adaptation.
