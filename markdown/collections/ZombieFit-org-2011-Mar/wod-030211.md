## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile OR row 250m OR bike 1/2 mile.

3 rounds of:
- 25m Quadrupedal Movement
- 10 Push Ups
- 10 Sit Ups
- 10 Pull Ups or Jumping Pull Ups

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

Complete as many rounds as possible in 15 minutes.

```wod
15:00 AMRAP
  5 12-inch Box Jumps
  10 Jumping Pull Ups
  10 Sit Ups
```

### Avancée — Advanced

Complete as many rounds as possible in 15 minutes.

```wod
15:00 AMRAP
  5 24-inch Box Jumps
  10 Pull Ups
  10 Sit Ups
```

### Traceur — Expert

Complete as many rounds as possible in 15 minutes.

```wod
15:00 AMRAP
  5 24-inch Box Jumps
  10 Pull Ups
  10 Sit Ups
```
