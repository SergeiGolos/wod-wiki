Warm Up:

- Run 1/4 mile OR row 250 m OR bike 2 miles.
- 3 rounds of:
  - 25 m quadrupedal movement
  - 10 push-ups
  - 10 pull-ups or jumping pull-ups
  - 10 "I suck at this" — perform a movement that you suck at. If it is already in the warm up, perform 15 repetitions of that movement instead of 10. For instance, if you suck at sit-ups and there are sit-ups in the warm up, do 3 rounds of 15 instead of 3 rounds of 10. If you suck at handstand push-ups, perform 3 rounds of 10 handstand push-ups (or their progression) and add that to the warm up.

## WOD

WOD 354 — CrossFit WOD "Annie"

50-40-30-20-10 reps of double-unders and sit-ups. If you don’t have a jump rope available, substitute double-unders with tuck jumps.

### Étudiant — Beginner

Perform single-unders in rounds of 100-80-60-40-20. If you do not have a jump rope, substitute with tuck jumps in rounds of 30-20-10.

```wod
(5)
  100 Single Unders
  50 Sit Ups
  80 Single Unders
  40 Sit Ups
  60 Single Unders
  30 Sit Ups
  40 Single Unders
  20 Sit Ups
  20 Single Unders
  10 Sit Ups
```

If you do not have a jump rope: substitute Single Unders with Tuck Jumps (suggested rounds: 30-20-10).

### Avancée — Advanced

WOD as prescribed: 50-40-30-20-10 double-unders (or tuck jumps) and sit-ups.

```wod
(50-40-30-20-10)
  Double Unders
  Sit Ups
```
