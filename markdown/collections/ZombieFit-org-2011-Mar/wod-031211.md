## Warm Up

**Cardio Option:** Choose one:
*   Run 1/4 mile
*   Row 250m
*   Bike 2 miles

**Circuit (3 Rounds):** Perform 3 rounds of the following circuit.
*   25m quadrupedal movement
*   10 Pushups
*   10 Pullups / Jumping Pullups
*   10 Isuckatthis (Perform a movement you suck at.)

***Note on Warm Up Scaling:*** *If any movement in the warm up is one that you suck at, perform 15 repetitions of that specific movement instead of 10. If the movement you suck at is not listed above, add it to the circuit for 3 rounds.*
