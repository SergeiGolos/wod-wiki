## Warm Up

```wod
Run 1/4 mile OR Row 250m OR Bike 1/2 mile
(3)
  10 Squats
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Double Unders or 50 Single Unders
  10 Push Ups
```

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

Perform 40 repetitions each of the listed movements. Complete each exercise in as few sets as possible (go to failure on every set).

```wod
40 Dips
40 Jumping Jacks
40 Air Squats
40 Sit Ups
40 Jumping Pull Ups
```

### Avancée — Advanced

Perform 60 repetitions each of the listed movements. Complete each exercise in as few sets as possible (go to failure on every set).

```wod
60 Dips
60 Jumping Jacks
60 Air Squats
60 Sit Ups
60 Pull Ups
```

### Traceur — Expert

Perform 75 repetitions each of the listed movements. Complete each exercise in as few sets as possible (go to failure on every set).

```wod
75 Dips
75 Knees to Elbows
75 Push Ups
75 Air Squats
75 Pull Ups
```
