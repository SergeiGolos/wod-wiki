## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile | bike 2 miles | row 250m.

3 rounds of:
- 10 Sit Ups
- 10 Push Ups
- 10 Air Squats
- 10 Pull Ups | Handstand Push Ups

## WOD

In as few sets as possible, and in any order, complete the following:

### Étudiant — Beginner

```wod
25 Push Ups
25 Sit Ups
200 Jumping Jacks
50 Jumping Pull Ups
25 Five-Foot Precisions
```

### Avancée — Advanced

```wod
50 Push Ups
50 Sit Ups
300 Jumping Jacks
50 Five-Foot Precisions
```

### Traceur — Expert

```wod
75 Push Ups
75 Sit Ups
300 Jumping Jacks
75 Pull Ups
75 Five-Foot Precisions
```
