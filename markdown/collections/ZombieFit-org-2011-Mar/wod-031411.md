## WOD

### Étudiant

```wod
(20) :60 EMOM / 5 Pushups / 5 Situps / 5 Air Squats
```

*Instructions: Perform 5 push ups, 5 sit ups, and 5 air squats on the minute for 20 minutes.*

### Avancée

This is a multi-stage EMOM. The total time is 20 minutes (4 x 5-minute blocks).

**Block 1 (Minutes 0-5):**
```wod
(5) :60 EMOM / 5 Pushups / 5 Situps / 5 Air Squats
```

**Block 2 (Minutes 5-10):**
```wod
(5) :60 EMOM / 10 Pushups / 5 Situps / 5 Air Squats
```

**Block 3 (Minutes 10-15):**
```wod
(5) :60 EMOM / 10 Pushups / 10 Situps / 5 Air Squats
```

**Block 4 (Minutes 15-20):**
```wod
(5) :60 EMOM / 10 Pushups / 10 Situps / 10 Air Squats
```

### Traceur

This is the same as Avancée, but with an added penalty/movement after each round.

**Block 1 (Minutes 0-5):**
```wod
(5) :60 EMOM / 5 Pushups / 5 Situps / 5 Air Squats + 2 Burpees
```

**Block 2 (Minutes 5-10):**
```wod
(5) :60 EMOM / 10 Pushups / 5 Situps / 5 Air Squats + 2 Burpees
```

**Block 3 (Minutes 10-15):**
```wod
(5) :60 EMOM / 10 Pushups / 10 Situps / 5 Air Squats + 2 Burpees
```

**Block 4 (Minutes 15-20):**
```wod
(5) :60 EMOM / 10 Pushups / 10 Situps / 10 Air Squats + 2 Burpees
```

*Instructions: If you cannot complete all movements within the minute time frame, stop the exercise, complete 2 burpees, and then move on to the next minute’s repetitions.*
