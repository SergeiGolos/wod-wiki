## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile OR row 250m OR bike 2 miles.

3 rounds of:
- 25m quadrupedal movement
- 10 push-ups
- 10 pull-ups or jumping pull-ups
- 10 "isuckatthis" (perform a movement you suck at; if that movement already appears in the warm up, perform 15 reps instead of 10 — e.g., if you suck at sit-ups do 15 sit-ups; if you suck at handstand push-ups, perform 10 HSPU or a progression)

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

On the minute for 20 minutes perform 5 push ups, 5 sit ups and 5 air squats.

```wod
(20) :60 EMOM
  + 5 Push Ups
  + 5 Sit Ups
  + 5 Air Squats
```

### Avancée — Advanced

Progress through four 5-minute EMOM blocks, increasing reps each block.

```wod
(5) :60 EMOM
  + 5 Push Ups
  + 5 Sit Ups
  + 5 Air Squats
(5) :60 EMOM
  + 10 Push Ups
  + 5 Sit Ups
  + 5 Air Squats
(5) :60 EMOM
  + 10 Push Ups
  + 10 Sit Ups
  + 5 Air Squats
(5) :60 EMOM
  + 10 Push Ups
  + 10 Sit Ups
  + 10 Air Squats
```

### Traceur — Expert

Same progression as Avancée, but perform 2 burpees after each minute/round. If you cannot complete the prescribed movements within the minute, stop, complete 2 burpees, then move on to the next minute's repetitions.

```wod
(5) :60 EMOM
  + 5 Push Ups
  + 5 Sit Ups
  + 5 Air Squats
  + 2 Burpees
(5) :60 EMOM
  + 10 Push Ups
  + 5 Sit Ups
  + 5 Air Squats
  + 2 Burpees
(5) :60 EMOM
  + 10 Push Ups
  + 10 Sit Ups
  + 5 Air Squats
  + 2 Burpees
(5) :60 EMOM
  + 10 Push Ups
  + 10 Sit Ups
  + 10 Air Squats
  + 2 Burpees
```
