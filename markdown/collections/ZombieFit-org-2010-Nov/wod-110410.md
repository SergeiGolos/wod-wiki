## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile OR row 250m OR bike 1 mile.

```wod
(3)
  25m Quadrupedal Movement
  10 Push Ups
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Pull Ups | Jumping Pull Ups
  10 Isuckatthis
```

If you "isuckatthis" at a movement already in the warm up, perform 15 repetitions of that movement instead of 10. If your "isuckatthis" is a progressional movement (e.g., handstand push ups), perform 3 rounds of 10 (or appropriate progression) and add that to the warm up.

## WOD

With a continuously running clock do one precision the first minute, two precisions the second minute, three precisions the third minute, and continue increasing the number of precisions each minute as long as you are able. Use as many sets each minute as needed.

### Étudiant — Beginner

Stand in place and mark the spot where your feet are. Find a spot approximately 4 feet away — that is the precision target for this level.

```wod
:60
  1 Precision 4ft
  2 Precision 4ft
  3 Precision 4ft
```

Continue increasing by one precision each minute until you are unable to complete the required number within a minute.

### Avancée — Advanced

Pick a precision length of 75% of your max broad jump. If possible precision over a gap or up to a ledge. After you have been unable to perform any more precisions, take a 2:00 break. Then, with a continuously running clock do one push up the first minute, two push ups the second minute, three push ups the third minute… continuing as long as you are able.

```wod
:60
  1 Precision 75% Max Broad Jump
  2 Precision 75% Max Broad Jump
  3 Precision 75% Max Broad Jump
2:00 Rest
:60
  1 Push Up
  2 Push Up
  3 Push Up
```

### Traceur — Expert

Pick a precision length of 75% of your max broad jump. If possible precision over a gap or up to a ledge. After you have been unable to perform any more precisions, take a 2:00 break. Then, with a continuously running clock do one push up the first minute, two push ups the second minute, three push ups the third minute… continuing as long as you are able. After push ups, take a 2:00 break. Then, with a continuously running clock do one squat the first minute, two squats the second minute, three squats the third minute… continuing as long as you are able.

```wod
:60
  1 Precision 75% Max Broad Jump
  2 Precision 75% Max Broad Jump
  3 Precision 75% Max Broad Jump
2:00 Rest
:60
  1 Push Up
  2 Push Up
  3 Push Up
2:00 Rest
:60
  1 Squat
  2 Squat
  3 Squat
```

## Cool Down

```wod
50 Pull Ups
```

May substitute chin-ups, jumping pull-ups, or L-sit pull-ups depending on ability.
