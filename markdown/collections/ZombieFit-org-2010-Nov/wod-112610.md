## WOD

WOD 275

*Note: Before starting, practice the parkour roll. This skill is absolutely necessary for training outside.*

### Étudiant (Beginner/Intermediate)
In 10 minutes, complete as many rounds as possible of:

```wod
(AMRAP 10)
  1 Parkour Roll
  3 Pushups
  6 Air Squats
  9 Sit Ups
```

### Avancée (Advanced)
In 15 minutes, complete as many rounds as possible of:

```wod
(AMRAP 15)
  2 Parkour Rolls
  3 Pullups
  6 Pushups
  9 Air Squats
```

### Traceur (Expert)
In 20 minutes, complete as many rounds as possible of:

```wod
(AMRAP 20)
  2 Parkour Rolls (alternate sides)
  5 Pullups
  10 Pushups
  15 Air Squats
```

**Important Safety Note:** When performing this WOD, take your time when performing the PK Roll. Concentrate on your form and technique. Do not flip over your neck or over the center of your back. If ANY part of the roll hurts you are doing it wrong. Stop the timer, and practice the roll until you can perform it pain free. This is highly suggested to be done on a padded surface (carpet or otherwise).

## Cool Down

Perform 50 precision jumps.
*Scaling:* If you have experience with this movement, go outside and pick a spot to do these repetitions. If not, just pick a point on a flat surface to jump to. These jumps should be at 65% of your max broad jump.
