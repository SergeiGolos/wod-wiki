## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile OR bike 1 mile OR row 250m.

3 rounds of:
- 10 double-unders
- 10 push-ups
- 10 sit-ups
- 10 dips

## WOD

Five rounds for time of:

### Étudiant — Beginner

```wod
(5)
  100m Run
  10 Five-Foot Precision Jumps
  10 Push Ups
  10 Air Squats
```

### Avancée — Advanced

```wod
(5)
  100m Run
  15 Five-Foot Precision Jumps
  15 Push Ups
  15 Air Squats
```

### Traceur — Expert

```wod
(5)
  200m Run
  15 Five-Foot Precision Jumps
  10 Handstand Push Ups
  15 Air Squats
```
