## Warm Up

Warm up options include:
*   Run 1/4 mile
*   Bike 1 mile
*   Row 250m

Followed by:
```wod
(3)
  10 Air Squats
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Pullups
```
*(Note: May substitute pull-ups with push-ups if a bar is unavailable.)*

## WOD

### Étudiant
```wod
(5)
  15 Lunge
  5 Burpee
```

### Avancée
```wod
(5)
  15 Lunge
  5 Burpee Pullup
```

### Traceur
```wod
(5)
  15 Lunge
  10 Burpee Pullup
```

## Cool Down

Three 100m sprints with 2 minutes rest between each run.
