# ZombieFit.org Nov 2010

Welcome to the November 2010 collection! This month's programming focused heavily on combining traditional fitness movements with core parkour skills. Expect a mix of sprints, plyometrics, and specific conditioning drills designed to build explosive power and endurance in both urban running and controlled movement environments.

### Weekly Summary & Themes

**Week of Nov 22:** Focus on foundational strength and speed with WOD 272.
**Nov 23 (WOD 273):** Introduction to Tabata-style parkour conditioning, emphasizing squats, jumps, and precision landings.
**Nov 24 (WOD 274):** A progression day focusing on mastering specific movement patterns and technique refinement.
**Nov 26 (WOD 275):** Weekend recovery and skill work, featuring mandatory practice of the parkour roll.
