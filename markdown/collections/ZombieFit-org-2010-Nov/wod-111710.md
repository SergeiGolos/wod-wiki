## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile or bike 1 mile or row 250m.

3 rounds of:
- 50m quadrupedal movement
- 5 Five-Foot Precisions
- Push Ups
- Double Unders

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

Perform the following movements for time:

```wod
50 Air Squats
5 Five-Foot Precisions
5 Burpees
50 Sit Ups
5 Five-Foot Precisions
5 Burpees
50 Push Ups
5 Five-Foot Precisions
5 Burpees
50 Jumping Pull Ups
5 Five-Foot Precisions
5 Burpees
```

### Avancée — Advanced

Perform the following movements for time:

```wod
50 Air Squats
10 Six-Foot Precisions
5 Burpees
50 Sit Ups
10 Six-Foot Precisions
5 Burpees
50 Push Ups
10 Six-Foot Precisions
5 Burpees
50 Pull Ups
10 Six-Foot Precisions
5 Burpees
```

### Traceur — Expert

Perform the following movements for time:

```wod
50 Air Squats
10 Six-Foot Precisions
10 Burpees
50 Sit Ups
10 Six-Foot Precisions
10 Burpees
50 Push Ups
10 Six-Foot Precisions
10 Burpees
50 Pull Ups
10 Six-Foot Precisions
10 Burpees
```

## Cool Down

10 handstand push ups or 3 thirty-second handstand holds against a wall.
