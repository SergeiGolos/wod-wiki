## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile OR row 250m OR bike 1 mile.

```wod
(3)
  10 Air Squats
  10 Push Ups
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Pull Ups | Jumping Pull Ups
  10 Movement You Suck At // perform a movement you suck at; if it's already in the warm up, do 15 reps instead
```

Perform a movement that you "suck at." If it is already in the warm up, perform 15 repetitions of that movement instead of 10. For instance, if you suck at sit ups and sit ups are in the warm up, do 3 rounds of 15 instead of 3 rounds of 10. If you suck at handstand push ups, perform 3 rounds of 10 handstand push ups (or progression) and add that to the warm up.
