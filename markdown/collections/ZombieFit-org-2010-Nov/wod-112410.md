## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile | bike 1 mile | row 500m.

3 rounds of:
- 50m quadrupedal movement
- 10 Air Squats
- 10 Sit Ups
- 10 Jumping Pull Ups
- 10 Push Ups

## WOD

Today we are working on the press progression of movements. See CrossFit press-progression resources for detailed technique notes.

### Étudiant — Beginner

Practice the press movement. Make sure feet are shoulder width, elbows below and forward of the bar. Pause after each rep and correct your form. This is NOT a conditioning WOD.

```wod
50 Shoulder Press // PVC or broomstick
50 Push Press // PVC or broomstick
50 Push Jerk // PVC or broomstick
```

### Avancée — Advanced

You should be familiar with all three movements. If you are not, perform the exercises at the Étudiant level. If you have some exposure but require a spot, keep the bar | dumbbell weight very light and prioritize form.

```wod
Shoulder Press sets: 5,5,3,3,1,1,1
Push Press sets: 5,5,3,3,1,1,1
Push Jerk sets: 5,5,3,3,1,1,1
```

### Traceur — Expert

The Traceur should be intimately familiar with all three movements. Work towards your one-rep max.

```wod
Shoulder Press sets: 5,5,3,3,1,1,1
Push Press sets: 5,5,3,3,1,1,1
Push Jerk sets: 5,5,3,3,1,1,1
```
