## WOD

**Note:** Today we are working on the press progression of movements. Please read the provided PDF for an excellent write-up by the CrossFit folks regarding press progression.

### Étudiant

*Goal: Practice form.* Use a broomstick or PVC pipe for all repetitions. Pause after each rep and correct your form. Think about the movement each rep. Double check your feet, bar, and elbow position. If possible, perform the movement in front of a mirror.

1.  **Shoulder Press:** 50 repetitions
2.  **Push Press:** 50 repetitions
3.  **Push Jerk:** 50 repetitions

### Avancée
