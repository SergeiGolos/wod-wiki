## Warm Up

Sorry for the late post! I’m in L.A. and did not have access to a computer until now.

Perform the structured warm up below.

```wod
Run 1/4 mile OR Row 250m OR Bike 1 mile
(3)
  10 Air Squats
  10 Push Ups
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Pull Ups | Jumping Pull Ups
  10 "I Suck At This" // movement you suck at
```

Note: "I Suck At This" means perform a movement you feel weak at. If that movement is already included in the warm up, perform 15 reps of it instead of 10. For movements like handstand push-ups use an appropriate progression.

## WOD

Forward quadrupedal movement — please note that this is NOT for time. Concentrate on the movement. Keep your back flat and your hips square. If you’re feeling a little frisky, try to do the movement as if you were on a six inch rail.

### Étudiant — Beginner

```wod
200m Quadrupedal Movement
```

### Avancée — Advanced

```wod
300m Quadrupedal Movement
```

### Traceur — Expert

```wod
400m Quadrupedal Movement
```
