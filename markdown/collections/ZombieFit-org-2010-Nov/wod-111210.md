## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile | bike 1 mile | row 250m.

3 rounds of:
- 10 Double Unders
- 10 Push Ups
- 10 Sit Ups
- 10 Dips

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

For time — choose ONE of the following movements and perform 100 repetitions.

```wod
100 Push Ups
100 Sit Ups
100 Jumping Pull Ups
100 Air Squats
```

### Avancée — Advanced

For time — choose ONE of the following movements and perform 100 repetitions.
If you finish in under 10 minutes, rest 3 minutes and then complete 100 repetitions of one of the other movements.
If you do not finish in under 10 minutes, perform 50 repetitions of two of the other movements.

```wod
100 Push Ups
100 Sit Ups
100 Kipping Pull Ups
100 Air Squats
```

### Traceur — Expert

For time — choose ONE of the following movements and perform 100 repetitions.
If you finish in under 10 minutes, rest 3 minutes and then complete 100 repetitions of one of the other movements.
Attempt to do this for all four movements.

```wod
100 Push Ups
100 Sit Ups
100 Kipping Pull Ups
100 Air Squats
```

## Cool Down

1 x 400m sprint.
