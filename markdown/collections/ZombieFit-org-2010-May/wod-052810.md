## Warm Up

**Option 1 (Distance):**
*   Run ?mile / 4
*   OR Row ?m / 250
*   OR Bike ?mile / 2

**Option 2 (Rounds):**
Perform 3 rounds of:
*   25m quadrupedal movement
*   10 Sit Ups
*   10 Pull Ups (or Jumping Pull Ups)
*   10 Push Ups
*   10 Air Squats

## WOD

### Étudiant

For time, perform 3 rounds of the following:
```wod
(3)
  Sprint ?m
  25 Air Squats
  25 Push Ups
```

### Avancée

For time, perform 3 rounds of the following:
```wod
(3)
  Sprint ?m
  50 Air Squats
  50 Push Ups
```

### Traceur

For time, perform 5 rounds of the following:
```wod
(5)
  Sprint ?m
  50 Air Squats
  50 Push Ups
```
*Note: Attempt to perform the air squats and push ups in as few sets as possible, i.e. go to failure on each set.*

## Cool Down

**Handstand Holds:**
Perform handstand holds against a wall till failure x 3.

**WOD Circuit (For Time):**
```wod
20 Deadlifts 115lb / 20 Five-foot Precisions / 20 Alternating Leg Lunges / 20 Air Squats / 20 Push Ups / 20 Push Presses 65lb / 20 Jumping Pull Ups
```
