## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile | row 250m | bike 2 miles.

```wod
(3)
  25m Quadrupedal Movement
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Pull Ups | Jumping Pull Ups
  10 Push Ups
  10 Air Squats
```

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

For time, perform 3 rounds of the following:

```wod
(3)
  100m Sprint
  25 Air Squats
  25 Push Ups
```

### Avancée — Advanced

For time, perform 3 rounds of the following:

```wod
(3)
  100m Sprint
  50 Air Squats
  50 Push Ups
```

### Traceur — Expert

For time, perform 5 rounds of the following:

Attempt to perform the air squats and push ups in as few sets as possible, i.e. go to failure on each set.

```wod
(5)
  100m Sprint
  50 Air Squats
  50 Push Ups
```

## Cool Down

```wod
(3)
  Handstand Hold Against Wall - hold to failure
```

```wod
20 115lb Deadlifts
20 Five-Foot Precisions
20 Alternating Leg Lunges
20 Air Squats
20 Push Ups
20 65lb Push Presses
20 Jumping Pull Ups
```
