## Warm Up

Warm up options:
*   Run 1/4 mile
*   Row 250m
*   Bike 2 miles

Perform 3 rounds of:
```wod
(3)
  25m Quadrupedal Movement
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Pullups (or jumping pull ups)
  10 Pushups
  10 Air Squats
```

## WOD

### Étudiant

For time, complete 15-9-7 repetitions of:
```wod
(15-9-7)
  5 Foot Precisions
  Pushups
  Sit Ups
```

### Avancée

For time, complete 21-15-9 repetitions of:
```wod
(21-15-9)
  5 Foot Precisions
  Pushups
  Sit Ups
```

### Traceur

For time, complete 21-15-9 repetitions of:
```wod
(21-15-9)
  5 Foot Precisions
  Handstand Pushups
  Sit Ups
```
