## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile | row 250m | bike 1/2 mile.

```wod
(3)
  25m Quadrupedal Movement
  10 Dips
  10 Five-Foot Precisions
  10 Push Ups
```

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

For time, complete 6 rounds of:

```wod
(6)
  100m Sprint
  Rest 1:00
```

### Avancée — Advanced

For time, complete 10 rounds of:

```wod
(10)
  100m Sprint
  Rest 1:00
```

### Traceur — Expert

For time, complete 10 rounds of:

```wod
(10)
  100m Sprint
  Rest :30
```

## Cool Down

```wod
(3)
  Handstand Hold Against Wall till Failure
```
