## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile | bike 1/2 mile | row 250m followed by:

```wod
(3)
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Push Ups
  10 Air Squats
  10 Pull Ups | Handstand Push Ups
```

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

```wod
(10-8-6-4-2-4-6-8-10)
  Burpees
  Sit Ups
```
