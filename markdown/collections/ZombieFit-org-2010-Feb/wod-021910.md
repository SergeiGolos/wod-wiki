## Warm Up

Complete as many rounds as possible in 5 minutes of:
*   5 Push Ups
*   5 Sit Ups
*   5 Squats
*   5 Pull Ups

## WOD

### Étudiant

For time complete as many rounds as possible in ?m of:
```wod
(10)
  10 Push Press 45lb
  5 Foot Precisions
  10 Front Squat 45lb
```
*Note: You may substitute with a pvc pipe or broomstick. Make sure you have proper form on the front squat and push press.*

### Avancée

For time complete as many rounds as possible in ?m of:
```wod
(15)
  10 Push Press 95lb
  6 Foot Precisions
  10 Front Squat 95lb
```

### Traceur

For time complete as many rounds as possible in ?m of:
```wod
(15)

  6 Foot Precisions
  10 Front Squat 115lb
```
