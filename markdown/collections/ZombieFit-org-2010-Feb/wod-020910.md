### Pre-WOD Circuit
Perform 3 rounds of:
```wod
(3)
  10 Air Squats
  10 Push Ups
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Pull Ups
```
*(Note: Jumping pull ups may substitute for regular pull ups.)*

## WOD
### Étudiant
21-15-9 repetitions for time of: push ups, sit ups and jumping jacks.
```wod
(21-15-9) Push Ups / Sit Ups / Jumping Jacks
```

### Avancée
21-15-9 repetitions for time of: burpees, sit ups and push ups.
```wod
(21-15-9) Burpees / Sit Ups / Push Ups
```

### Traceur
21-15-9 repetitions for time of: handstand pushpus, sit ups and push ups.
```wod
(21-15-9) Handstand Pushups / Sit Ups / Push Ups
```

## Cool Down
Perform 100m of walking lunges.
