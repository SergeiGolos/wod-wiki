## Warm Up

Choose one option to start the warm up, followed by 3 rounds of:
*   10 Push Ups
*   10 Air Squats
*   10 Sit Ups
*   10 Jumping Pull Ups

**Options:**
*   Run ?mile (or 1/4 mile)
*   Bike ?miles (or 2 miles)
*   Row ?m (or 500m)

## WOD

### Étudiant

The goal is to perform escalating precision jumps for the duration of the workout. The pattern continues as long as possible:
Minute 1: 1 Precision Jump
Minute 2: 2 Precision Jumps
Minute 3: 3 Precision Jumps
... and so on, until failure.

```wod
(Continuous Effort)
  Escalating Precision Jumps (Target: ~4 feet)
    Minute 1: 1 ?
    Minute 2: 2 ?
    Minute 3: 3 ?
    ... continue until failure
```

### Avancée

The goal is to perform escalating movements after an initial precision jump phase. The pattern continues as long as possible:
**Phase 1 (Precision):** Precision jumps over a gap or up to a ledge, using 75% of max broad jump length.
*After failure in Phase 1, take a 2 minute break.*

**Phase 2 (Push-ups):** The pattern continues as long as possible:
Minute 1: 1 Push Up
Minute 2: 2 Push Ups
Minute 3: 3 Push Ups
... and so on, until failure.

```wod
(Continuous Effort)
  Escalating Precision Jumps (Target: 75% max broad jump)
    [Failure/Break]
  Escalating Push Ups
    Minute 1: 1 ?
    Minute 2: 2 ?
    Minute 3: 3 ?
    ... continue until failure
```

### Traceur

The goal is to perform escalating movements across three phases. The pattern continues as long as possible:
**Phase 1 (Precision):** Precision jumps over a gap or up to a ledge, using 75% of max broad jump length.
*After failure in Phase 1, take a 2 minute break.*

**Phase 2 (Push-ups):** The pattern continues as long as possible:
Minute 1: 1 Push Up
Minute 2: 2 Push Ups
Minute 3: 3 Push Ups
... and so on, until failure.
*After failure in Phase 2, take a 2 minute break.*

**Phase 3 (Squats):** The pattern continues as long as possible:
Minute 1: 1 Squat
Minute 2: 2 Squats
Minute 3: 3 Squats
... and so on, until failure.

```wod
(Continuous Effort)
  Escalating Precision Jumps (Target: 75% max broad jump)
    [Failure/Break]
  Escalating Push Ups
    Minute 1: 1 ?
    Minute 2: 2 ?
    Minute 3: 3 ?
    ... continue until failure
  [Break]
  Escalating Squats
    Minute 1: 1 ?
    Minute 2: 2 ?
    Minute 3: 3 ?
    ... continue until failure
```

## Cool Down

Complete 50 Pull Ups in the smallest number of sets possible.
*(Scaling options include Chin Ups, Jumping Pull Ups, or L-Sit Pull Ups depending on ability level).*
