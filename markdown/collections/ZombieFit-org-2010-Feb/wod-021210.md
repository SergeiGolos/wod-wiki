## Warm Up

```wod
Run 1/4 mile
OR Bike 2 miles
OR Row 500m
(3)
  10 Push Ups
  10 Air Squats
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Jumping Pull Ups
```

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

Stand in place and mark the spot where your feet are. Then find a spot approximately 4 feet away — that spot is where you’ll be precisioning for the workout.

```wod
:60 Precision // 1 the 1st minute, 2 the 2nd minute, 3 the 3rd minute, continue adding 1 each minute as long as able
```

### Avancée — Advanced

Pick a precision length of ~75% of your max broad jump. If possible, precision over a gap or up to a ledge. After you are unable to perform more precisions, take a 2:00 break. Then continue with the push-up minute ladder.

```wod
:60 Precision // 1, then 2, then 3... each minute at ~75% max broad jump; continue until unable
2:00 Break
:60 Push Up // 1, then 2, then 3... each minute; continue until unable
```

### Traceur — Expert

Pick a precision length of ~75% of your max broad jump. If possible, precision over a gap or up to a ledge. After you are unable to perform more precisions, take a 2:00 break. Then do the push-up minute ladder. After failing the push-up ladder, take a 2:00 break and perform the squat minute ladder.

```wod
:60 Precision // 1, then 2, then 3... each minute at ~75% max broad jump; continue until unable
2:00 Break
:60 Push Up // 1, then 2, then 3... each minute; continue until unable
2:00 Break
:60 Squat // 1, then 2, then 3... each minute; continue until unable
```

## Cool Down

```wod
50 Pull Ups // complete in the smallest number of sets possible; may substitute chin-ups, jumping pull-ups, or L-sit pull-ups
```
