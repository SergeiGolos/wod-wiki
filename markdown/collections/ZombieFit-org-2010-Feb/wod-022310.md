## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile | bike 2 miles | row 250m.

```wod
(3)
  50m Quadrupedal Movement
  5 Five-Foot Precisions
  Push Ups
  Double Unders
```

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

```wod
(20) :60 EMOM
  + 5 Push Ups
  + 5 Sit Ups
  + 5 Air Squats
```

### Avancée — Advanced

```wod
(5) :60 EMOM
  + 5 Push Ups
  + 5 Sit Ups
  + 5 Air Squats
(5) :60 EMOM
  + 10 Push Ups
  + 5 Sit Ups
  + 5 Air Squats
(5) :60 EMOM
  + 10 Push Ups
  + 10 Sit Ups
  + 5 Air Squats
(5) :60 EMOM
  + 10 Push Ups
  + 10 Sit Ups
  + 10 Air Squats
```

### Traceur — Expert

Same as Avancée, with 2 burpees after each minute/round. If you cannot complete all movements within the minute, stop the exercise, perform 2 burpees, then move on to the next minute’s repetitions.

```wod
(5) :60 EMOM
  + 5 Push Ups
  + 5 Sit Ups
  + 5 Air Squats
  - 2 Burpees
(5) :60 EMOM
  + 10 Push Ups
  + 5 Sit Ups
  + 5 Air Squats
  - 2 Burpees
(5) :60 EMOM
  + 10 Push Ups
  + 10 Sit Ups
  + 5 Air Squats
  - 2 Burpees
(5) :60 EMOM
  + 10 Push Ups
  + 10 Sit Ups
  + 10 Air Squats
  - 2 Burpees
```
