## Warm Up

Enjoy the decent weather while ya got it! Perform one of the following options:
*   Run 1/4 mile
*   Row 250m
*   Bike 2 miles

**Pre-WOD Routine:** Complete 3 rounds for time of:
```wod
(3)
  10 Air Squats
  10 Pushups
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Pullups
```
*(Note: You may substitute jumping pull ups for regular pull ups.)*

## WOD

### Étudiant (Beginner)

Perform 5 rounds for time of:
```wod
(5) :?
  25m Quadrupedal Movement
  25 Jumping Jacks
  5 five-foot Precisions
```

### Avancée (Intermediate)

Perform 5 rounds for time of:
```wod
(5) :?
  50m Quadrupedal Movement
  5 Burpees
  10 five-foot Precisions
```

### Traceur (Advanced)

Perform 5 rounds for time of:
```wod
(5) :?
  50m Quadrupedal Movement
  10 Burpees
  10 six-foot Precisions
```

## Cool Down

Perform a set to failure on only one of the following exercises:
*   Pushups (100 reps)
*   Air Squats (100 reps)
*   Sit Ups (100 reps)
*   Pullups (100 reps)
