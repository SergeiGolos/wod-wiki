## Warm Up

Sorry for the tardiness of posting the WODs. Enjoy the decent weather while ya got it!

```wod
Run 1/4 mile
OR Row 250m
OR Bike 2 miles
(3)
  10 Air Squats
  10 Push Ups
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Pull Ups
```

You may sub jumping pull ups for regular pull ups.

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

Perform 5 rounds for time.

```wod
(5)
  25m Quadrupedal Movement
  25 Jumping Jacks
  5 Five-Foot Precisions
```

### Avancée — Advanced

Perform 5 rounds for time.

```wod
(5)
  50m Quadrupedal Movement
  5 Burpees
  10 Five-Foot Precisions
```

### Traceur — Expert

Perform 5 rounds for time.

```wod
(5)
  50m Quadrupedal Movement
  10 Burpees
  10 Six-Foot Precisions
```

## Cool Down

```wod
100 Repetitions (pick one: Push Ups, Air Squats, Sit Ups, or Pull Ups) — go to failure on every set
```
