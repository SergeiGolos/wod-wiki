## Warm Up

Warm Up options:
*   Run 1/4 mile OR Bike 2 miles OR Row 250m.
*   Followed by 3 rounds of:
    *   10 Sit Ups
    *   10 Dips
    *   10 Squats
    *   10 Double Unders (May substitute with 2x single unders)

## WOD

### Étudiant

```wod
(5)
  Max Effort Pushups ?
  Max Effort Jumping Pullups ?
```
*Instructions: Take as much rest as needed in between sets.*

### Avancée

```wod
(5)
  Max Effort Pushups ?
  Max Effort Pullups ?
```
*Instructions: Take as much rest as needed in between sets.*

### Traceur

```wod
(5)
  Max Effort Bodyweight Bench Press ?
  Max Effort Pullups ?
```
*Instructions: Take rest between each set. This is a max effort exercise, meaning you should go for the maximum number of repetitions on each set.*
