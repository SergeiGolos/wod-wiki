## Warm Up

```wod
Run 1/4 mile OR Bike 2 miles OR Row 250m
(3)
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Dips
  10 Squats
  10 Double Unders // may sub 20 Single Unders
```

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

Perform 5 rounds of max-effort push ups and max-effort jumping pull ups. Rest as needed between sets.

```wod
(5)
  Max Effort Push Ups ?
  Max Effort Jumping Pull Ups ?
```

### Avancée — Advanced

Perform 5 rounds of max-effort push ups and max-effort pull ups. Rest as needed between sets.

```wod
(5)
  Max Effort Push Ups ?
  Max Effort Pull Ups ?
```

### Traceur — Expert

Perform 5 rounds of max-effort body-weight bench press and max-effort pull ups. Rest as needed between sets.

```wod
(5)
  Max Effort Body-Weight Bench Press ?
  Max Effort Pull Ups ?
```
