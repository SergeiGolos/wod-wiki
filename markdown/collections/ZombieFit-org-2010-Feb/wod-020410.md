## Warm Up

Warm up options:
*   Run 1/4 mile
*   Bike 2 miles
*   Row 250m

Followed by a circuit (3 rounds):
```wod
(3)
  10 Air Squats
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Pullups (? Pushups if no bar)
```

## WOD

### Étudiant
```wod
(5)
  15 Lunges (alternating feet)
  5 Burpees
```

### Avancée
```wod
(5)
  15 Lunges (alternating feet)
  5 Burpee Pullups
```

### Traceur
```wod
(5)
  15 Lunges (alternating feet)
  10 Burpee Pullups
```

## Cool Down

Three 100m sprints with 2 minutes rest in between each run.
