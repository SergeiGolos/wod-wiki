## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile or bike 2 miles or row 250m followed by:

3 rounds of:
- 10 Air Squats
- 10 Sit Ups
- 10 Pull Ups (may substitute Push Ups if no pull-up bar)

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

```wod
(5)
  15 Lunges // alternating feet
  5 Burpees
```

### Avancée — Advanced

```wod
(5)
  15 Lunges // alternating feet
  5 Burpee Pull Ups
```

### Traceur — Expert

```wod
(5)
  15 Lunges // alternating feet
  10 Burpee Pull Ups
```

## Cool Down

Three 100m sprints with 2 minutes rest between each run.
