## Warm Up

Precision Ladder Warm Up

```wod
Deadlift 5, 3, 3, 1, 1
```

Make sure to go up in weight each set. The set of 5 should be easy. Your two sets of 1 repetition should be either a) ~85% of your max effort deadlift; or b) something that is moderately difficult but achievable.

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

Practice deadlift technique with a broomstick or empty bar.

```wod
Practice Deadlift with broomstick or empty bar
```

### Avancée — Advanced

If you know your max effort deadlift, perform 5 sets of 10 repetitions at 55% of your max effort. If you do not know your max effort, use 40% of the one-rep weight achieved during the warm up.

All levels without gym access: 21-15-9 for time of push ups, sit ups, air squats and jumping pull ups.

```wod
5 sets
  10 Deadlift 55% 1RM // or 40% of warm-up single if 1RM unknown
(21-15-9)
  Push Ups
  Sit Ups
  Air Squats
  Jumping Pull Ups
```

### Traceur — Expert

Same as Avancée: strength option with percentages, or bodyweight alternative if no barbell available.

```wod
5 sets
  10 Deadlift 55% 1RM // or 40% of warm-up single if 1RM unknown
(21-15-9)
  Push Ups
  Sit Ups
  Air Squats
  Jumping Pull Ups
```

## Cool Down

Handstand against a wall until failure.
