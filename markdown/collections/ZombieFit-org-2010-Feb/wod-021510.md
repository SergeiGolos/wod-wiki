## Warm Up

**Precision Ladder Warm Up**

*   **Deadlift (for Avancée and Traceur levels):** Perform sets of 5, 3, 3, 1, 1. Ensure you increase the weight for each subsequent set. The initial set of 5 should feel easy.
*   **Final Sets:** Your two sets of 1 repetition should be either:
    a) 85% of your max effort deadlift; OR
    b) Something that is moderately difficult but achievable.

## WOD

### Étudiant

Take a broomstick or empty bar and practice the deadlift.

### Avancée & Traceur

**Deadlift Progression:**
*   If you know your max effort deadlift, perform 5 sets of 10 repetitions at 55% of your max effort.
*   If you do not know your max effort, use 40% of the one-rep weight achieved during the warm up.

**Main Circuit (All Levels):**

Handstand against a wall until failure.
