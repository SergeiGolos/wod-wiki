## Warm Up

Warm up: run 1/4 mile OR bike 2 miles OR row 250m

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

Perform the following movements for time:

```wod
(5)
  10 Air Squats
  10 24in Box Jumps
  10 Lunges (alternating legs)
```

### Avancée — Advanced

Perform the following movements for time:

```wod
(5)
  10 95lb Weighted Squats
  10 24in Box Jumps
  10 Lunges (alternating legs)
```

### Traceur — Expert

Perform the following movements for time:

```wod
(5)
  10 135lb Weighted Squats
  10 24in Box Jumps
  10 Lunges (alternating legs)
```
