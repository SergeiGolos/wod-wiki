## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile | bike 2 miles | row 250m followed by:

3 rounds of:
- 50m quadrupedal movement
- 10 double unders
- 10 lunges (5 each leg)
- 10 sit ups

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

Complete as many rounds as possible in 20 minutes of:

```wod
20:00 AMRAP
  5 Jumping Pull Ups
  10 Push Ups
  15 Air Squats
```

### Avancée — Advanced

Complete as many rounds as possible in 20 minutes of:

```wod
20:00 AMRAP
  5 Pull Ups
  10 Push Ups
  15 Air Squats
```

### Traceur — Expert

Complete as many rounds as possible in 20 minutes of:

```wod
20:00 AMRAP
  5 L-sit Pull Ups
  10 Push Ups
  15 Air Squats
```

## Cool Down

10 handstand push ups. Concentrate on your form. If possible do them free-standing.
