## WOD

### Étudiant

For time, complete the following rep scheme. After *each total round*, complete a sprint: 100m run OR 1/4 mile bike OR 125m row.

```wod
(15-9-7) 5 foot precisions / dips / sit ups
```

### Avancée

For time, complete the following rep scheme. After *each total round*, complete a sprint: 100m run OR 1/4 mile bike OR 125m row.

```wod
(21-15-9) 6 foot precisions / dips / sit ups
```

### Traceur

For time, complete the following rep scheme. After *each total round*, complete a sprint: 100m run OR 1/4 mile bike OR 125m row.

```wod
(21-15-9) 6 foot precisions / dips / elbows to knees
```

## Cool Down

Perform a plank hold until failure, repeated 3 times.
