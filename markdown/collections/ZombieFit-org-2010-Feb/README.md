# ZombieFit.org Feb 2010

Welcome to February's collection! This month focuses on building foundational endurance and mastering classic bodyweight movements. Expect a mix of high-intensity interval work, strength challenges, and conditioning circuits designed to keep you moving like the undead. Remember to prioritize form over speed, especially when tackling these demanding routines!

## Workouts

### WOD 80 (Feb 1)
**Warm Up:** Run 1/4 mile or bike 2 miles or row 250m followed by: 3 rounds of: 10 double unders and 50m quadrupedal movement.
**WOD:**
*   **Étudiant:** Perform 40 repetitions of: dips, jumping jacks, air squats, sit ups, and jumping pull ups. (Goal: Complete in fewest sets possible).

## Monthly Summary

*   **Week of Feb 1st:** Focus on foundational circuits and high volume bodyweight work (Dips, Squats, Pull-ups).
*   **Week of Feb 3rd:** Classic "ladder" style conditioning using rep schemes (21-15-9) for explosive movements.
*   **Week of Feb 4th:** Repetition focus on lunges and burpees, emphasizing endurance over pure strength.
*   **Week of Feb 5th:** Testing raw pushing power with high-rep push-up challenges.
