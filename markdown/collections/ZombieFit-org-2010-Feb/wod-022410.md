## WOD

### Étudiant

For time, complete 21-15-9 repetitions of:
```wod
(21-15-9)
  Pushups
  Jumping Jacks
  Air Squats
```

### Avancée

For time, complete 21-15-9 repetitions. Perform 25m of quadrupedal movement (QM) between each set and finish with a final 25m QM.

*   **Set 1:** Pushups -> **QM**
*   **Set 2:** Jumping Jacks -> **QM**
*   **Set 3:** Air Squats -> **Final QM**

```wod
(21-15-9)
  Pushups
  25m Quadrupedal Movement
  Jumping Jacks
  25m Quadrupedal Movement
  Air Squats
  25m Quadrupedal Movement
```

### Traceur

For time, complete 21-15-9 repetitions. Perform 25m of quadrupedal movement (QM) between each set and finish with a final 25m QM.

*   **Set 1:** Handstand Pushups -> **QM**
*   **Set 2:** Jumping Jacks -> **QM**
*   **Set 3:** Air Squats -> **Final QM**

```wod
(21-15-9)
  Handstand Pushups
  25m Quadrupedal Movement
  Jumping Jacks
  25m Quadrupedal Movement
  Air Squats
  25m Quadrupedal Movement
```
