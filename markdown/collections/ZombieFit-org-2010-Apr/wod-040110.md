## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile OR row 250m OR bike 2 miles.

```wod
(3)
  25m Quadrupedal Movement
  10 Push Ups
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Pull Ups or Jumping Pull Ups
  10 Movement You Struggle With (if that movement is already in the warm up, perform 15)
```

## WOD

Perform the following movements for time:

```wod
25 Air Squats
25m Quadrupedal Movement
25 Push Ups
25m Quadrupedal Movement
25 Sit Ups
25m Quadrupedal Movement
25 Jumping Pull Ups
25m Quadrupedal Movement
25 Five-Foot Precisions
```
