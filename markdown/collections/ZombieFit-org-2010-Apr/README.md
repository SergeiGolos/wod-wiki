# ZombieFit.org Apr 2010

Welcome back to the zombie horde! April programming focuses on blending raw cardio endurance with explosive parkour movements and classic CrossFit conditioning. Expect a mix of interval training, high-volume AMRAPs, and challenging bodyweight circuits designed to keep your undead metabolism burning. Stay agile, stay strong, and remember: failure is just another step in the journey.

### WOD 126 (April 9)
**Category:** ZombieFit / Parkour
**Type:** Tabata / Conditioning

#### Warm Up
Run 1/4 mile OR Row 250m OR Bike 2 miles.
3 rounds of:
*   10 Squats
*   10 Sit-ups
*   10 Double Unders (or 50 Single Unders)
*   10 Pushups

#### WOD
```wod
(TabataParkour)
  Air Squats
  Box Jumps (12 inches)
  Precisions (5 feet)
```
*(Note: This workout uses the Tabata protocol for four exercises, with 1 minute rest between movements.)*

### WOD 124 (April 20)
**Category:** ZombieFit / Parkour
**Type:** Tabata / Conditioning

Run 1/4 mile OR Bike 2 miles OR Row 500m.
Followed by:
3 rounds of:
*   50m quadrupedal movement
*   10 Jumping Jacks
*   5 Burpees
