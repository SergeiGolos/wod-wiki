## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile | bike 1/2 mile | row 250m.

```wod
(3)
  10 Air Squats
  10 Push Ups
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Air Squats
  10 Pull Ups // may substitute Jumping Pull Ups
```

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

3 rounds for time of:

```wod
(3)
  25 Air Squats
  25 Jumping Pull Ups
  25 Sit Ups
```

### Avancée — Advanced

5 rounds for time of:

```wod
(5)
  25 Air Squats
  25 Jumping Pull Ups
  25 Sit Ups
```

### Traceur — Expert

5 rounds for time of:

```wod
(5)
  50 Air Squats
  50 Jumping Pull Ups
  50 Sit Ups
```

## Cool Down

```wod
Tabata Push Ups
```
