## Warm Up

Choose one option for the warm-up circuit:
*   Run 1/4 mile
*   Row 250m
*   Bike 2 miles

Complete 3 rounds of:
*   10 sit ups
*   10 pull ups (or jumping pull ups)
*   10 air squats
*   10 dips
