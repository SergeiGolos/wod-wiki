## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile, bike 1/2 mile, or row 250m.

```wod
(3)
  10 Parkour Squats | Air Squats
  10 Sit Ups
  50ft Quadrupedal Movement
```

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner
Make sure your knee touches the ground on every lunge.

```wod
100m Lunge Walk
100m Lunge Walk Backwards
```

### Avancée — Advanced

```wod
200m Lunge Walk
200m Lunge Walk Backwards
```

### Traceur — Expert
Must be completed in 8-10 minutes.

```wod
300m Lunge Walk
300m Lunge Walk Backwards
```

## Cool Down

```wod
(3)
  100m Sprint
```
