## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile | row 250m | bike 2 miles.

3 rounds of:
- 10 Sit Ups
- 10 Pull Ups | Jumping Pull Ups
- 10 Air Squats
- 10 Dips

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

On the minute for ten minutes:

```wod
(10) :60 EMOM
  + 2 Burpees
  + 4 Push Ups
  + 6 Sit Ups
```

### Avancée — Advanced

On the minute for ten minutes:

```wod
(10) :60 EMOM
  + 3 Burpees
  + 5 Push Ups
  + 7 Sit Ups
```

### Traceur — Expert

On the minute for ten minutes. If you cannot complete the movements within the minute, stop immediately and start the next round from the beginning with an additional 2 burpees (e.g., for Avancée you'd start the next round with 5 burpees).

```wod
(10) :60 EMOM
  + 5 Burpees
  + 7 Push Ups
  + 9 Sit Ups
```
