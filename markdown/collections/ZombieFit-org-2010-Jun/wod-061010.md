## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile OR row 250m OR bike 2 miles.

```wod
(3)
  10 Air Squats
  10 Push Ups
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Pull Ups or Jumping Pull Ups
  10 Movement You Suck At
```

Perform a movement that you suck at. If it is already in the warm up, perform 15 repetitions of that movement instead of 10. For instance, if you suck at sit ups and they are already in the warm up, do 3 rounds of 15 instead of 3 rounds of 10. If you suck at handstand push ups, perform 3 rounds of 10 handstand push ups (or an appropriate progression) and add that to the warm up.

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

Perform the following movements for time:

```wod
(3)
  50ft Quadrupedal Movement
  15 Jumping Pull Ups
  25 Push Ups
```

### Avancée — Advanced

Perform the following movements for time:

```wod
(5)
  50ft Quadrupedal Movement
  15 Pull Ups
  25 Push Ups
```

### Traceur — Expert

Perform the following movements for time:

```wod
(5)
  100ft Quadrupedal Movement
  15 L-Sit Pull Ups
  50 Push Ups
```
