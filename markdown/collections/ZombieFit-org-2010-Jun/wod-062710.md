## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile OR bike 1/2 mile OR row 250m.

```wod
(3)
  10 Air Squats
  10 Push Ups
  10 Sit Ups
  50ft Quadrupedal Movement
```

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

Perform five 100m sprints with 2 minutes rest between each sprint.

```wod
(5)
  100m Sprint
  2:00 Rest
```

### Avancée — Advanced

Perform 5 rounds of a 100m sprint and 25 air squats. Rest one minute in between sets. Attempt to complete each round without resting.

```wod
(5)
  100m Sprint
  25 Air Squats
  1:00 Rest
```

### Traceur — Expert

Perform 7 rounds of a 100m sprint and 25 air squats. Rest one minute in between sets. Attempt to complete each round without resting.

```wod
(7)
  100m Sprint
  25 Air Squats
  1:00 Rest
```
