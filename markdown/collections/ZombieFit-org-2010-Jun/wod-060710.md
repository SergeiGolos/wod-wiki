## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile | row 250m | bike 2 miles.

3 rounds of:
- 10 Air Squats
- 10 Push Ups
- 10 Sit Ups
- 10 Pull Ups | Jumping Pull Ups
- 10 "perform a movement you suck at" (see note below)

Note: Perform a movement that you suck at. If that movement is already in the warm up, perform 15 repetitions of that movement instead of 10. For instance, if you suck at sit ups and sit ups are in the warm up, do 3 rounds of 15 sit ups. If you suck at handstand push ups, perform 3 rounds of 10 handstand push ups (or an appropriate progression) and add that to the warm up.

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

```wod
(3)
  20 Push Ups
  20 Air Squats
  10 Burpees
```

### Avancée — Advanced

```wod
(4)

  10 Burpees
```

### Traceur — Expert

```wod
(5)
  30 Push Ups
  30 Air Squats
  10 Burpees
```
