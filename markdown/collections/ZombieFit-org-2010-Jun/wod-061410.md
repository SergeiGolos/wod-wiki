## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile OR row 250m OR bike 2 miles.

```wod
(3)
  10 Air Squats
  10 Push Ups
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Pull Ups | Jumping Pull Ups
  10 I Suck At This
```

Perform a movement that you "suck at." If that movement is already in the warm up, perform 15 repetitions of that movement instead of 10. For example, if you suck at sit ups and sit ups are in the warm up, do 3 rounds of 15 instead of 3 rounds of 10. If you suck at handstand push ups, perform 3 rounds of 10 handstand push ups (or an appropriate progression) and add that to the warm up.

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

Perform, for time, the following movements:

```wod
20 Jumping Pull Ups
20 Air Squats
20 Five-Foot Precisions
100m Sprint
20 Five-Foot Precisions
20 Air Squats
20 Jumping Pull Ups
```

### Avancée — Advanced

Perform, for time, the following movements:

```wod
30 Jumping Pull Ups
30 Air Squats
30 Five-Foot Precisions
200m Sprint
30 Five-Foot Precisions
30 Air Squats
30 Jumping Pull Ups
```

### Traceur — Expert

Perform, for time, the following movements:

```wod
40 Jumping Pull Ups
40 Air Squats
40 Five-Foot Precisions
400m Sprint
40 Five-Foot Precisions
40 Air Squats
40 Jumping Pull Ups
```
