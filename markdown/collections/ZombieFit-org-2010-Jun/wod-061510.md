## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile | row 250m | bike 2 miles.

3 rounds of:
- 10 Air Squats
- 10 Push Ups
- 10 Sit Ups
- 10 Pull Ups | Jumping Pull Ups
- 10 of a movement you "suck at"

Perform a movement that you suck at. If it is already in the warm up, then perform 15 repetitions of that movement instead of 10. For instance, if you suck at sit ups and there are sit ups in the warm up, do 3 rounds of 15 instead of 3 rounds of 10. If you suck at handstand push ups, perform 3 rounds of 10 handstand push ups (or their progression) and add that to the warm up.

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

For time, complete 21-15-9 repetitions of:

```wod
(21-15-9)
  Tuck Jumps
  Push Ups
  Alternating Leg Lunges
```

### Avancée — Advanced

After every five repetitions of each movement, perform a parkour safety roll.

```wod
(21-15-9)
  Tuck Jumps
  Push Ups
  Alternating Leg Lunges
```

### Traceur — Expert

For time, complete 21-15-9 repetitions of: handstand push ups instead of push ups, and perform lunges for each leg (e.g., 21 left, 21 right, 15 left, 15 right). After every five repetitions of each movement, perform a parkour safety roll.

```wod
(21-15-9)
  Tuck Jumps
  Handstand Push Ups
  Leg Lunges (each leg)
```
