## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile | row 250m | bike 2 miles.

```wod
(3)
  25m Quadrupedal Movement
  100m Sprint
  10 Movement You Suck At
```

Perform a movement that you suck at. If that movement is already in the warm up, perform 15 repetitions of that movement instead of 10. For instance, if you suck at sit ups and there are sit ups in the warm up, do 3 rounds of 15 instead of 3 rounds of 10. If you suck at handstand push ups, perform 3 rounds of 10 handstand push ups (or an appropriate progression) and add that to the warm up.

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

In 10 minutes, perform as many rounds as possible of:

```wod
10:00 AMRAP
  3 Push Ups
  5 Sit Ups
  7 Air Squats
```

### Avancée — Advanced

In 15 minutes, perform as many rounds as possible of:

```wod
15:00 AMRAP
  5 Jumping Pull Ups
  7 Sit Ups
  9 Push Ups
```

### Traceur — Expert

In 20 minutes, perform as many rounds as possible of:

```wod
20:00 AMRAP
  5 Pull Ups
  7 Sit Ups
  9 Push Ups
```
