## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile OR bike 1 mile OR row 250m.

```wod
(3)
  10 Double Unders
  10 Push Ups
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Dips
```

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

For time, perform 100 repetitions of ONE of the following movements: Push Ups, Sit Ups, Jumping Pull Ups or Air Squats.

```wod
100 Push Ups OR 100 Sit Ups OR 100 Jumping Pull Ups OR 100 Air Squats
```

### Avancée — Advanced

For time, perform 100 repetitions of ONE of the following movements: Push Ups, Sit Ups, Kipping Pull Ups or Air Squats.

```wod
100 Push Ups OR 100 Sit Ups OR 100 Kipping Pull Ups OR 100 Air Squats
```

If you finish in under 10 minutes, rest 3 minutes and then complete 100 repetitions of one of the other movements. If you do not finish in under 10 minutes, perform 50 repetitions of two of the other movements.

### Traceur — Expert

For time, perform 100 repetitions of ONE of the following movements: Push Ups, Sit Ups, Kipping Pull Ups or Air Squats.

```wod
100 Push Ups OR 100 Sit Ups OR 100 Kipping Pull Ups OR 100 Air Squats
```

If you finish in under 10 minutes, rest 3 minutes and then complete 100 repetitions of one of the other movements. Attempt to do this for all four movements.

## Cool Down

```wod
400m Sprint
```
