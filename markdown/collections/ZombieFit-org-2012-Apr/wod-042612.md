## Warm Up

Sorry for the lack of posts over the last week — I was traveling for my real job and was unable to get on the site. We will now continue with our regularly scheduled WODs!

Run 1/4 mile | bike 1 mile | row 250m followed by:

3 rounds of:
- 25m quadrupedal movement
- 10 sit ups
- 10 push ups
- 10 jumping pull ups

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

```wod
(8) :20 Air Squats
1:00 Rest
(8) :20 Box Jumps 12in
1:00 Rest
(8) :20 5-Foot Precisions
1:00 Rest
(8) :20 Jumping Pull Ups
```

### Avancée — Advanced

```wod

1:00 Rest

1:00 Rest

1:00 Rest
```

## Post

Post total number of repetitions performed to comments.

For the tabata quadrupedal movement, use each twenty-second work period to go as far as possible while performing the quadrupedal movement. Score yourself by tabulating feet traveled during each Tabata interval.
