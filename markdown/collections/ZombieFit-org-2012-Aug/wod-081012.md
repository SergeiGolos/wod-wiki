## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile OR row 250m OR bike .5 miles.

```wod
(3)
  25m Quadrupedal Movement
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Pull Ups / Jumping Pull Ups
  10 Push Ups
  10 Air Squats
```
