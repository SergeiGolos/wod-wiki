## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile | bike 1 mile | row 250m.

```wod
(3)
  10 Double Unders
  10 Push Ups
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Dips
```

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

If you do not have a box to jump on, perform tuck jumps as a substitute.

```wod
(5)
  10 Jumping Pull Ups
  10 Air Squats
  10 24-inch Box Jumps
```

### Avancée — Advanced

```wod
(5)
  10 Pull Ups
  20 Air Squats
  10 24-inch Box Jumps
```

### Traceur — Expert

```wod
(5)
  10 L-Sit Pull Ups
  30 Air Squats
  10 24-inch Box Jumps
```

## Cool Down

```wod
15 Handstand Push Ups
```

Use a scaled substitute | progression as needed.
