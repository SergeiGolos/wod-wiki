## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile | bike 1 mile | row 250m.

```wod
(3)
  50m Quadrupedal Movement
  5 Five-Foot Precisions
  10 Push Ups
  10 Double Unders
```

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

Perform the following movements for time:

```wod
50 Air Squats
5 Five-Foot Precisions
5 Burpees
50 Sit Ups
5 Five-Foot Precisions
5 Burpees
50 Push Ups
5 Five-Foot Precisions
5 Burpees
50 Jumping Pull Ups
5 Five-Foot Precisions
5 Burpees
```

### Avancée — Advanced

Perform the following movements for time:

```wod
50 Air Squats
10 Six-Foot Precisions
5 Burpees
50 Sit Ups
10 Six-Foot Precisions
5 Burpees
50 Push Ups
10 Six-Foot Precisions
5 Burpees
50 Pull Ups
10 Six-Foot Precisions
5 Burpees
```

### Traceur — Expert

Perform the following movements for time:

```wod
50 Air Squats
10 Six-Foot Precisions
10 Burpees
50 Sit Ups
10 Six-Foot Precisions
10 Burpees
50 Push Ups
10 Six-Foot Precisions
10 Burpees
50 Pull Ups
10 Six-Foot Precisions
10 Burpees
```

## Cool Down

10 handstand push ups | 3 thirty-second handstand holds against a wall.
