## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile OR row 250m OR bike 2 miles.

3 rounds of:
- 25m quadrupedal movement
- 10 push ups
- 10 sit ups
- 10 pull ups or jumping pull ups

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

```wod
(3)
  100m Sprint
  25 Push Ups
  25m Quadrupedal Movement
  50 Air Squats
```

### Avancée — Advanced

```wod
(5)

  50 Air Squats
```

### Traceur — Expert
