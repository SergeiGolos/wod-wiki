## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

For time, perform 100 repetitions of ONE of the following movements:

```wod
100 Push Ups
100 Sit Ups
100 Jumping Pull Ups
100 Air Squats
```

### Avancée — Advanced

For time, perform 100 repetitions of ONE of the following movements. If you finish in under ten minutes, rest three minutes and then complete 100 repetitions of one of the other movements. If you do not finish in under 10 minutes, perform 50 repetitions of two of the other movements.

```wod
100 Push Ups
100 Sit Ups
100 Kipping Pull Ups
100 Air Squats
```

### Traceur — Expert

For time, perform 100 repetitions of ONE of the following movements. If you finish in under ten minutes, rest three minutes and then complete 100 repetitions of one of the other movements. Attempt to do this for all 4 movements.

```wod
100 Push Ups
100 Sit Ups
100 Kipping Pull Ups
100 Air Squats
```
