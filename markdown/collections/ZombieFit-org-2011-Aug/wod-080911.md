## Warm Up

```wod
Run 1/4 mile | Bike 1/2 mile | Row 250m
(3)
  10 Air Squats
  10 Push Ups
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Air Squats
  10 Pull Ups
```

May sub pull-ups with jumping pull-ups if needed.

## WOD

On the minute, every minute, for 10 minutes perform the following routine.

If you don’t finish a routine within the minute, restart at the start of the next minute and add 1 additional burpee to your burpee set.

### Étudiant — Beginner

```wod
(10) :60 EMOM
  + 2 Burpees
  + 5 Push Ups
  + 7 Air Squats
```

### Avancée — Advanced

```wod
(10) :60 EMOM
  + 3 Burpees
  + 7 Push Ups
  + 9 Air Squats
```

### Traceur — Expert

```wod
(10) :60 EMOM
  + 5 Burpees
  + 7 Push Ups
  + 9 Air Squats
```

## Cool Down

```wod
50 Sit Ups
```

Enjoy!
