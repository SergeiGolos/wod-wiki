## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile or bike 1 mile or row 250m.

3 rounds of:
- 10 double unders
- 10 push-ups
- 10 sit-ups
- 10 dips

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

```wod
(5)
  Run 100m
  10 Five-Foot Precisions
  10 Push Ups
  10 Air Squats
```

### Avancée — Advanced

```wod
(5)
  Run 100m
  15 Five-Foot Precisions
  15 Push Ups
  15 Air Squats
```

### Traceur — Expert

```wod
(5)
  Run 200m
  15 Five-Foot Precisions
  10 Handstand Push Ups
  15 Air Squats
```
