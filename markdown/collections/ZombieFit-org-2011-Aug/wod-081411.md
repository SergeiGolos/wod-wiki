## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

Perform 100 repetitions of ONE of the following movements:

```wod
100 Push Ups
100 Sit Ups
100 Jumping Pull Ups
100 Air Squats
```

### Avancée — Advanced

Perform 100 repetitions of ONE of the following movements. If you finish in under 10 minutes, rest 3 minutes then complete 100 repetitions of one of the other movements. If you do not finish in under 10 minutes, perform 50 repetitions of two of the other movements.

```wod
100 Push Ups
100 Sit Ups
100 Kipping Pull Ups
100 Air Squats
```

### Traceur — Expert

Perform 100 repetitions of ONE of the following movements. If you finish in under 10 minutes, rest 3 minutes then complete 100 repetitions of one of the other movements. Attempt to do this for all 4 movements.

```wod
100 Push Ups
100 Sit Ups
100 Kipping Pull Ups
100 Air Squats
```
