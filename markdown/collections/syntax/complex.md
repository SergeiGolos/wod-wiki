# Syntax: Complex Examples

Combine loops and timers to create advanced training plans.

```wod

(3 Rounds)
  (Sun Salutation)
    5 Mountain Pose
    5 Plank
    5 Cobra
    5 Downward Dog
  10 Air Squats
  1:00 Rest

(AMRAP) 15:00
  (Strength)
    5 Pullups
    10 Pushups
  (Conditioning)
    Run 400m
```
