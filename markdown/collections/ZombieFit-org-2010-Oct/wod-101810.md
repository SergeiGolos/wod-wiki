## WOD

Precision Ladder

With a continuously running clock do one precision the first minute, two precisions the second minute, three precisions the third minute… continuing as long as you are able.

### Étudiant — Beginner

Stand in place and mark the spot where your feet are. Then find a spot approximately 4 feet away. That spot is where you’ll be precisioning to for the ladder.

```wod
:60 EMOM
  Precision — 1 the 1st minute, add 1 each minute until unable
```

### Avancée — Advanced

Pick a precision length of 65% of your max broad jump. If possible precision over a gap or up to a ledge.

```wod
:60 EMOM
  Precision (65% Max Broad Jump) — 1 the 1st minute, add 1 each minute until unable
```

### Traceur — Expert

Pick a precision length of 85% of your max broad jump. If possible precision over a gap or up to a ledge.

```wod
:60 EMOM
  Precision (85% Max Broad Jump) — 1 the 1st minute, add 1 each minute until unable
```
