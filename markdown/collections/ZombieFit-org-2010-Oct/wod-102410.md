## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile OR row 250m OR bike 1 mile.

```wod
(3)
  10 Air Squats
  10 Push Ups
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Pull Ups or Jumping Pull Ups
  10 I Suck At This
```

Perform a movement that you "suck at." If that movement is already in the warm up, perform 15 repetitions of that movement instead of 10. For instance, if you suck at sit ups and sit ups are in the warm up, do 3 rounds of 15 instead of 3 rounds of 10. If you suck at handstand push ups, perform 3 rounds of 10 handstand push ups (or an appropriate progression) and add that to the warm up.

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

```wod
(20) :60 EMOM
  + 2 Push Ups
  + 4 Air Squats
  + 6 Jumping Jacks
  2 Burpees (If you do not complete the repetitions in the minute, stop the clock, perform 2 burpees, then restart and continue for 20 minutes)
```

### Avancée — Advanced

```wod
(20) :60 EMOM
  + 5 Push Ups
  + 7 Air Squats
  + 9 Jumping Jacks
  2 Burpees (If you do not complete the repetitions in the minute, stop the clock, perform 2 burpees, then restart and continue for 20 minutes)
```

### Traceur — Expert

```wod
(20) :60 EMOM
  + 2 Burpees
  + 5 Push Ups
  + 7 Air Squats
  + 9 Jumping Jacks
  5 Burpees (If you do not complete the repetitions in the minute, stop the clock, perform 5 burpees, then restart and continue for 20 minutes)
```
