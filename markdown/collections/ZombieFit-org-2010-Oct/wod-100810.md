## Warm Up

Happy Friday!

Run 1/4 mile OR row 250m OR bike 2 miles.

```wod
(3)
  10 Air Squats
  5 Sun Salutations
  10 Sit Ups
```

## WOD

Then practice your parkour roll. If you have no training with a parkour roll, or need some help with your roll, check out our tutorial. Perform at least 50 rolls. The parkour roll is an important skill; practice it on a padded, soft surface unless you have significant experience. DO NOT PERFORM THE ROLL IN THE WOD UNLESS YOU ARE CONFIDENT YOU’RE DOING IT CORRECTLY.

### Étudiant — Beginner

```wod
10:00 AMRAP
  1 Parkour Roll
  3 Push Ups
  6 Air Squats
  9 Sit Ups
```

### Avancée — Advanced

```wod
15:00 AMRAP
  2 Parkour Roll
  3 Pull Ups
  6 Push Ups
  9 Air Squats
```

### Traceur — Expert

Perform rolls alternating sides.

```wod
20:00 AMRAP
  2 Parkour Roll (alternate sides)
  5 Pull Ups
  10 Push Ups
  15 Air Squats
```

Now, when performing this WOD, take your time when performing the parkour roll. Concentrate on your form and technique, then complete the rest of the round. Parkour rolls can be dangerous if not done correctly. Do not flip over your neck or over the center of your back. If any part of the roll hurts you, stop the timer and practice the roll until you can perform it pain free.
