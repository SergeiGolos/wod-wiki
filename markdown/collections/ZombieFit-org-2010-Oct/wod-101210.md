## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile | bike 1 mile | row 250m followed by:

3 rounds of:
- 25m quadrupedal movement
- 10 Sit Ups
- 10 Push Ups

## WOD

(3)

  25 Air Squats
  25 Jumping Pull Ups
```

```

### Traceur — Expert
