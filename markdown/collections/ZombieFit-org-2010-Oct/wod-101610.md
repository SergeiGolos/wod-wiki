## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile | bike 1 mile | row 250m, followed by:

```wod
(3)
  25m Quadrupedal Movement
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Push Ups
  10 Jumping Pull Ups
```

## Post

Post total number of repetitions performed to the comments.
