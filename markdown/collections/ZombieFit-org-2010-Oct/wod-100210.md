## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile OR row 250m OR bike 2 miles.

```wod
(3)
  25m Quadrupedal Movement
  10 Push Ups
  10 Pull Ups / Jumping Pull Ups
  10 I Suck At This
```

Perform a movement that you "suck at." If that movement is already in the warm up, perform 15 repetitions of that movement instead of 10. For instance, if you "suck" at sit ups and sit ups are in the warm up, do 3 rounds of 15 sit ups. If you "suck" at handstand push ups, perform 3 rounds of 10 HSPU (or an appropriate progression) and add that to the warm up.

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

```wod
(5)
  100m Sprint
  2:00 Rest
```

### Avancée — Advanced

You should attempt to complete the entire round (100m sprint + 25 air squats) without resting.

```wod
(5)
  100m Sprint
  25 Air Squats
  1:00 Rest
```

### Traceur — Expert

You should attempt to complete the entire round (100m sprint + 25 air squats) without resting.

```wod
(7)
  100m Sprint
  25 Air Squats
  1:00 Rest
```
