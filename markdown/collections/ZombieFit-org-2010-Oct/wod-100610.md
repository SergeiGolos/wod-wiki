## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile OR row 250m OR bike 2 miles.

```wod
(3)
  25m Quadrupedal Movement
  10 Push Ups
  10 Pull Ups / Jumping Pull Ups
  10 Movement You Suck At
```

Perform a movement that you suck at. If it is already in the warm up, perform 15 repetitions of that movement instead of 10. For instance, if you suck at sit ups and sit ups are in the warm up, do 3 rounds of 15 instead of 3 rounds of 10. If you suck at handstand push ups, perform 3 rounds of 10 handstand push ups (or a progression) and add that to the warm up.

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

In as few sets as possible, complete:

```wod
30 Push Ups
30 Air Squats
30 Jumping Pull Ups
30 Sit Ups
```

### Avancée — Advanced

In as few sets as possible, complete:

```wod
50 Push Ups
50 Air Squats
50 Jumping Pull Ups
50 Sit Ups
```

### Traceur — Expert

In the fewest sets possible, complete:

```wod
75 Push Ups
75 Air Squats
75 Jumping Pull Ups
75 Sit Ups
```

## Cool Down

```wod
(2)
  100m Sprint
```
