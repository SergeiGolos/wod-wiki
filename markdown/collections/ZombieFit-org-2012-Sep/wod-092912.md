## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile OR row 250m OR bike 1/2 miles.

3 rounds of:
- 25m quadrupedal movement
- 10 push-ups
- 10 pull-ups or jumping pull-ups
- 10 isuckatthis

isuckatthis: perform a movement that you suck at. If it is already in the warm up, perform 15 repetitions of that movement instead of 10. For instance, if you suck at sit-ups and there are sit-ups in the warm up, do 3 rounds of 15 instead of 3 rounds of 10. If you suck at handstand push-ups, perform 3 rounds of 10 handstand push-ups (or their progression) and add that to the warm up.
