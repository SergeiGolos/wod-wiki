## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile OR row 250m OR bike 1/2 mile.

```wod
3 rounds
  25m Quadrupedal Movement
  10 Push Ups
  10 Pull Ups or Jumping Pull Ups
  10 Movement You Suck At
```

If the movement you "suck at" is already in the warm up, perform 15 repetitions of that movement instead of 10. For example, if you suck at sit ups and sit ups are in the warm up, do 15 sit ups each round. If you suck at handstand push ups, perform 3 rounds of 10 HSPU (or a progression) and add that to the warm up.

## WOD

Skill work: Max effort broad jump. Start with precision jumps at 4ft and progress up until you reach your max precision distance. Once at max precision, perform 10 max-effort broad jumps.

```wod
5 Precision Jumps 4ft
5 Precision Jumps 5ft
5 Precision Jumps 6ft
5 Precision Jumps up to Max Distance
10 Max Effort Broad Jumps
```

Note: A max-effort precision requires a controlled landing. If you are tipping over or falling backward your technique is incorrect — focus on jumping up and landing cleanly.
