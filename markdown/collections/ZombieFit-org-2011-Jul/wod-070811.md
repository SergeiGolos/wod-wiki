## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile OR row 250m OR bike 2 miles.

3 rounds of:
- 10 air squats
- 5 sun salutations
- 10 sit-ups

## WOD

Practice your parkour roll. If you have no training with a parkour roll, or need help with your roll, seek instruction before attempting. Perform at least 50 rolls on a padded, soft surface unless you have significant experience. DO NOT perform the roll in the WOD unless you are confident you're doing it correctly.

### Étudiant — Beginner

```wod
10:00 AMRAP
  1 Parkour Roll
  3 Push Ups
  6 Air Squats
  9 Sit Ups
```

### Avancée — Advanced

```wod
15:00 AMRAP
  2 Parkour Roll
  3 Pull Ups
  6 Push Ups
  9 Air Squats
```

### Traceur — Expert

```wod
20:00 AMRAP
  2 Parkour Roll (alternate sides)
  5 Pull Ups
  10 Push Ups
  15 Air Squats
```

Now, when performing this WOD, take your time when performing the parkour roll. Concentrate on your form and technique, then complete the rest of the round. Parkour rolls can be dangerous if not done correctly — do not roll over your neck or the center of your back. If any part of the roll hurts, stop the timer and practice the roll until you can perform it pain free.
