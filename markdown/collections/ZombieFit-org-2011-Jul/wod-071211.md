## Warm Up

```wod
Run 1/4 mile
or Row 250m
or Bike 1/2 mile
(3)
  25m Quadrupedal Movement
  10 Squats
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Double Unders or 50 Single Unders
  10 Push Ups
```

## WOD

Choose one of the following: run, swim, bike or row (C2).

### Étudiant — Beginner

Hold maximal distance possible on each interval.
