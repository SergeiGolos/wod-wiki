## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile OR bike 1/2 mile OR row 250m.

```wod
(3)
  10 Parkour Squats or Air Squats
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Push Ups
  50ft Quadrupedal Movement
```

## WOD

If you have never done a back squat before, you’re an étudiant today. If you have limited experience with the back squat, you’re avancée, and if you have significant experience with the movement, you’re a traceur.

Bar placement: prefer low-bar placement for increased hip activation. Attempt low bar with LIGHT WEIGHT first (something you can comfortably perform at least 10 times).

### Étudiant — Beginner

Practice the squat movement with a 45lb bar. Focus on form, take breaks between repetitions, keep feet shoulder-width with toes slightly out, bend at the knees (not the back), track knees over toes, and keep chest and head up.

```wod
50 Back Squats 45lb
```

### Avancée — Advanced

Perform sets of 5,5,5,3,3,1,1. Work up to ~85% of your max effort lift. If you do not know your max, choose a weight that enforces good technique without being overly difficult.

```wod
(5,5,5,3,3,1,1)
  Back Squat
```

### Traceur — Expert

Perform 5,5,5,3,3,1,1 repetitions working toward a max effort lift.

```wod
(5,5,5,3,3,1,1)
  Back Squat
```

As always, if you are lifting heavy weight USE A SPOT. If you do not have a spot, ensure you are working in a squat rack set to prevent dropping the weight on yourself.
