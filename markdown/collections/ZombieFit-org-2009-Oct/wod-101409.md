## Warm Up

*   Run 1/4 mile
*   Bike 1/2 mile
*   Row 250 m
*   3 rounds of:
    *   10 parkour squats / air squats
    *   10 sit ups
    *   50 ft quadrupedal movement

## WOD

### Étudiant

```wod
(1) 100 Pushups
```
*Scaling:* If you cannot do a full push, do 200 push ups on your knees. Remember to keep your back flat and your butt down regardless of how it is scaled.

### Avancée

```wod
(1) 250 Pushups
```

### Traceur

```wod
(1) 400 Pushups
```
*Note:* Must be completed in under 12 minutes.

## Cool Down

Go home and watch some parkour videos on YouTube.
