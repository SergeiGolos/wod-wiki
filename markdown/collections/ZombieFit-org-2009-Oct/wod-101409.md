## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile | bike 1/2 mile | row 250m.

3 rounds of:
- 10 parkour squats | air squats
- 10 sit-ups
- 50 ft quadrupedal movement

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

If you cannot do a full push-up, perform 200 push-ups on your knees. Keep your back flat and your butt down regardless of scaling.

```wod
100 Push Ups
```

### Avancée — Advanced

```wod
250 Push Ups
```

### Traceur — Expert

Must be completed in under 12 minutes.

```wod
400 Push Ups
```

## Cool Down

Go home and watch some parkour videos on YouTube.
