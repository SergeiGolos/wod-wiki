## Warm Up

Precision Ladder Warm Up

```wod
Deadlift 5
Deadlift 3
Deadlift 3
Deadlift 1
Deadlift 1
```

Deadlift (for Avancée and Traceur levels): sets of 5, 3, 3, 1, 1. Make sure to go up in weight each set. The set of 5 should be easy. Your two sets of 1 repetition should be either a) 85% of your max effort deadlift; or b) something that is moderately difficult but achievable.

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

Take a broomstick or empty bar and practice the deadlift.

```wod
Broomstick or Empty Bar Deadlift Practice
```

### Avancée — Advanced

If you know your max effort deadlift, perform 5 sets of 10 repetitions at 55% of your max effort.

```wod
(5)
  Deadlift 10 55% max effort
```

If you do not know your max effort, use 40% of the one-rep weight achieved during the warm up.

### Traceur — Expert

Same as Avancée: 5 sets of 10 repetitions at 55% of your max effort.

```wod
(5)
  Deadlift 10 55% max effort
```

If you do not know your max effort, use 40% of the one-rep weight achieved during the warm up.

## Cool Down

Handstand against a wall until failure.
