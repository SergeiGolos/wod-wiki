## Warm Up

**Precision Ladder Warm Up**

*   **Deadlift (Avancée & Traceur):** Sets of 5, 3, 3, 1, 1. Increase weight for each subsequent set. The initial set of 5 should feel easy.
*   **Notes:** Your two sets of 1 repetition should be either:
    a) 85% of your max effort deadlift; or
    b) Something moderately difficult but achievable.

## WOD

### Étudiant

Take a broomstick/empty bar and practice the deadlift.

### Avancée & Traceur

If you know your max effort deadlift, perform:
```wod
(5) 10 Deadlifts @ 55% Max Effort Deadlift
```
If you do not know your max effort, use:
```wod
(5) 10 Deadlifts @ 40% Warm Up Weight
```

## Cool Down

Handstand against a wall until failure.
