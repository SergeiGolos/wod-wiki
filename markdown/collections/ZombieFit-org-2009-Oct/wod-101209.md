## Warm Up

Warm Up: run 1/4 mile, bike 1/2 mile, or row 250 m.

```wod
(3)
  10 Parkour Squats | Air Squats
  10 Push Ups
  10 Sit Ups
  50ft Quadrupedal Movement
```

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

If you cannot perform a stationary handstand against a wall, use supports for your feet or place something under your head to shorten range of motion.

```wod
:60 EMOM
  1 Handstand Push Up // increase by 1 each minute until unable to complete
```

### Avancée — Advanced

Perform the ladder against a wall with full range of motion: touch nose to the ground and press to full lockout each rep.

```wod
:60 EMOM
  1 Handstand Push Up // full range of motion; increase by 1 each minute until unable to complete
```

### Traceur — Expert

Freestanding handstand push-up ladder. Do not use the wall to steady yourself.

```wod
:60 EMOM
  1 Handstand Push Up // freestanding; increase by 1 each minute until unable to complete
```

## Cool Down

```wod
50 Push Ups
```

Scale as needed. Time yourself.
