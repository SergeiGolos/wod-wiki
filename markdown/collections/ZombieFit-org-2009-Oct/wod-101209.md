## WOD

### Étudiant

If you cannot perform a stationary handstand against a wall, use an object to keep your feet up in the air. The higher you can get your feet, the better. If you can perform a stationary handstand but lack full range of motion (top of head to floor), place an object under your head (books/weights/etc) to shorten the distance.

### Avancée

**Handstand Push Up Ladder:**
Perform a cumulative ladder effort against a wall, using full range of motion (touching nose to ground, followed by full press extension till arms are locked out and shoulders active).

*   Minute 1: 1 Handstand Push Up
*   Minute 2: 2 Handstand Push Ups
*   Minute 3: 3 Handstand Push Ups
*   ... continue increasing reps until failure.

**Goal:** Complete the ladder for time, or record total rounds/reps completed before failing to meet the minute's required rep count.

### Traceur

**Standalone Handstand Push Up Ladder:**
Perform a cumulative ladder effort without using any wall support.

*   Minute 1: 1 Handstand Push Up
*   Minute 2: 2 Handstand Push Ups
*   Minute 3: 3 Handstand Push Ups
*   ... continue increasing reps until failure.

**Goal:** Complete the ladder for time, or record total rounds/reps completed before failing to meet the minute's required rep count.
