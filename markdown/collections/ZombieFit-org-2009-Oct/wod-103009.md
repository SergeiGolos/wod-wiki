## WOD

### Étudiant
```wod
(5)
  100 Double Unders/Tuck Jumps
  80 Double Unders/Tuck Jumps
  60 Double Unders/Tuck Jumps
  40 Double Unders/Tuck Jumps
  20 Double Unders/Tuck Jumps
(5) Sit Ups
```
*Scaling:* If no jump rope is available, substitute double unders with tuck jumps. Use rounds of 30-20-10 for the jumping portion if unable to complete the full range.

### Avancée
Double Unders (or Tuck Jumps)/Sit Ups:
```wod
(5)
  50 Double Unders/Tuck Jumps
  40 Double Unders/Tuck Jumps
  30 Double Unders/Tuck Jumps
  20 Double Unders/Tuck Jumps
  10 Double Unders/Tuck Jumps
(5) Sit Ups
```

### Traceur
Same as Avancée, but the entire WOD must be completed in under 6 minutes.

## Cool Down
