## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile OR bike 2 miles OR row 500m.

```wod
(3)
  10 Pull Ups
  10 Push Ups
  10 Air Squats
  10 Sit Ups
```

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

Perform single-unders with rounds of 100-80-60-40-20.
If you do not have a jump rope, sub tuck jumps in rounds of 30-20-10.

```wod
(100-80-60-40-20)
  Single Unders
  Sit Ups
```

### Avancée — Advanced

WOD as RX'd. 50-40-30-20-10 double unders and sit ups.
If you do not have a jump rope, sub tuck jumps.

```wod
(50-40-30-20-10)
  Double Unders
  Sit Ups
```

### Traceur — Expert

Same as Avancée; complete the entire WOD in under 6:00.

```wod
(50-40-30-20-10)
  Double Unders
  Sit Ups
```

## Cool Down

```wod
100m Backwards Quadrupedal Movement
```
