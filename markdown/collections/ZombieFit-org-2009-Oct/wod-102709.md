## Warm Up

Run 1/2 mile, bike 1/2 mile, row 250m.

3 rounds of:
- 10 parkour squats/air squats
- 20 sit ups
- 10 push ups

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

```wod
(5)
  10 Jumping Pull Ups
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Twelve Inch Box Jumps
```

### Avancée — Advanced

```wod
(5)
  10 Pull Ups
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Twenty-Four Inch Box Jumps
```

### Traceur — Expert

```wod
(5)
  10 L-Sit Pull Ups
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Thirty-Six Inch Box Jumps
```

## Cool Down

200m quadrupedal movement.
