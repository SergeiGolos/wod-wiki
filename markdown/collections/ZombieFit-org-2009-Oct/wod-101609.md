## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile OR bike 1/2 mile OR row 250m.

```wod
(3)
  10 Parkour Squats / Air Squats
  10 Sit Ups
  50ft Quadrupedal Movement
```

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

Make sure your knee touches the ground on every lunge.

```wod
100m Lunge Walk
100m Reverse Lunge Walk
```

### Avancée — Advanced

Make sure your knee touches the ground on every lunge.

```wod
200m Lunge Walk
200m Reverse Lunge Walk
```

### Traceur — Expert

Make sure your knee touches the ground on every lunge. Must be completed in 8 – 10 minutes.

```wod
300m Lunge Walk
300m Reverse Lunge Walk
```

## Cool Down

```wod
100m Sprint
```
