## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile | row 250m | bike .5 miles.

3 rounds of:
- 25m quadrupedal movement
- 5 burpees
- 10 sit-ups

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

In 15 minutes, perform as many rounds as possible of:

```wod
15:00 AMRAP
  3 Push Ups
  5 Sit Ups
  7 Air Squats
```

### Avancée — Advanced

In 15 minutes, perform as many rounds as possible of:

```wod
15:00 AMRAP
  5 Jumping Pull Ups
  7 Sit Ups
  9 Push Ups
```

### Traceur — Expert

In 20 minutes, perform as many rounds as possible of:

```wod
20:00 AMRAP
  5 Pull Ups
  7 Sit Ups
  9 Push Ups
```
