## Warm Up

I hope everyone is enjoying their time off of work and their respective holidays!

Run 1/4 mile | row 250m | bike 1/2 mile.

3 rounds of:
- 10 Air Squats
- 10 Push Ups
- 10 Sit Ups
- 10 Pull Ups | Jumping Pull Ups
- 10 "isuckatthis"

Perform a movement that you suck at. If it is already in the warm up, perform 15 repetitions of that movement instead of 10. For instance, if you suck at sit ups and there are sit ups in the warm up, do 3 rounds of 15 instead of 3 rounds of 10. If you suck at handstand push ups, perform 3 rounds of 10 HSPU (or a progression) and add that to the warm up.
