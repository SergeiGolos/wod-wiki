## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile | row 250m | bike 2 miles.

3 rounds of:
- 25m quadrupedal movement
- 10 push-ups
- 10 pull-ups | jumping pull-ups
- 10 "isuckatthis"

Perform a movement that you suck at. If it is already in the warm up, perform 15 repetitions of that movement instead of 10. For instance, if you suck at sit-ups and there are sit-ups in the warm up, do 3 rounds of 15 instead of 3 rounds of 10. If you suck at handstand push-ups, perform 3 rounds of 10 HSPU (or an appropriate progression) and add that to the warm up.

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

```wod
(20) :60 EMOM
  + 5 Push Ups
  + 5 Sit Ups
  + 5 Air Squats
```

### Traceur — Expert

Same as Avancée, plus perform 2 burpees after each minute. If you cannot complete all movements within the minute time frame, stop the exercise, complete 2 burpees, and then move on to the next minute’s repetitions.

```wod
(5) :60 EMOM
  + 5 Push Ups
  + 5 Sit Ups
  + 5 Air Squats
  + 2 Burpees
(5) :60 EMOM
  + 10 Push Ups
  + 5 Sit Ups
  + 5 Air Squats
  + 2 Burpees
(5) :60 EMOM
  + 10 Push Ups
  + 10 Sit Ups
  + 5 Air Squats
  + 2 Burpees
(5) :60 EMOM
  + 10 Push Ups
  + 10 Sit Ups
  + 10 Air Squats
  + 2 Burpees
```
