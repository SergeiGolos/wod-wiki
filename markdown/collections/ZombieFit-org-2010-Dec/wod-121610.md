## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile OR row 250m OR bike 2 miles.

```wod
(3)
  10 Squats
  10 Push Ups
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Pull Ups // or Jumping Pull Ups
```

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

```wod
(15-9-7)
  12 inch Box Jumps
  Push Ups
  Sit Ups
```

### Avancée — Advanced

```wod
(21-15-9)
  24 inch Box Jumps
  Push Ups
  Sit Ups
```

### Traceur — Expert

```wod
(21-15-9)
  24 inch Box Jumps
  Push Ups
  Elbows to Knees
```

If needed, substitute parkour squats for box jumps.

## Cool Down

```wod
100m Quadrupedal Movement
```

Remember that when completing a WOD for time you must complete the exercises as quickly as possible with minimal/no rest between repetitions and sets. Attempt to go at a pace that is sustainable. If you do not have experience with metabolic conditioning style workouts, strongly consider performing several WODs at an Étudiant level prior to moving on to Avancée or Traceur.

When performing box jumps, ensure that you are landing with both feet firmly planted. Stand up to your full height and activate (push forward) your hips. Don’t forget to pick up your knees when performing the jumps.
