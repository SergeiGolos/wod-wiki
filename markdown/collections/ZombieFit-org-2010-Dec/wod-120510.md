## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile | bike 0.5 miles | row 250m followed by:

3 rounds of:
- 10 Air Squats
- 10 Sit Ups
- 10 Jumping Pull Ups (may substitute push-ups if you don’t have a pull-up bar)

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

3 rounds for time.

```wod
(3)
  50m Quadrupedal Movement
  5 Five-Foot Precision Jumps
  10 Dips
```

### Avancée — Advanced

5 rounds for time.

```wod
(5)
  50m Quadrupedal Movement
  5 Seven-Foot Precision Jumps
  10 Dips
```

### Traceur — Expert

5 rounds for time.

```wod
(5)
  50m Quadrupedal Movement
  5 Eight-Foot Precision Jumps
  20 Dips
```

## Cool Down

25 pull ups (may be dead hang, kipping, jumping | assisted).
