## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile | bike 1/2 mile | row 250m.

3 rounds of:
- 10 Push Ups
- 10 Air Squats
- 10 Sit Ups
- 10 Jumping Pull Ups

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

```wod
  5 Sit Ups
  5 Air Squats
```
