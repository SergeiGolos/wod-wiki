## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile OR row 250m OR bike 2 miles.

3 rounds of:
- 10 Air Squats
- 10 Push Ups
- 10 Sit Ups
- 10 Pull Ups or Jumping Pull Ups
- 10 of a movement you "suck at" (see note)

Note: Perform a movement that you suck at. If it is already in the warm up, perform 15 repetitions of that movement instead of 10. For instance, if you suck at sit ups and there are sit ups in the warm up, do 3 rounds of 15 instead of 3 rounds of 10. If you suck at handstand push ups, perform 3 rounds of 10 handstand push ups (or their progression) and add that to the warm up.

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

Perform, for time, the following movements:

```wod
25m Quadrupedal Movement
10 Push Ups
25m Quadrupedal Movement
10 Sit Ups
25m Quadrupedal Movement
10 Air Squats
25m Quadrupedal Movement
```

### Avancée — Advanced

20 Air Squats
