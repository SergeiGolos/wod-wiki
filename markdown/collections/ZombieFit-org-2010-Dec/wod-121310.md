## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile OR row 250m OR bike 2 miles.

3 rounds of:
- 10 Pull Ups
- 10 Air Squats
- 10 Push Ups
- 10 Sit Ups
- 10 Dips

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

Perform the following movements for time:

```wod
10 Burpees
2 Five-Foot Precisions
8 Burpees
4 Five-Foot Precisions
6 Burpees
6 Five-Foot Precisions
4 Burpees
8 Five-Foot Precisions
2 Burpees
10 Five-Foot Precisions
```

### Avancée — Advanced

Same as above, add an extra 12-rep set and perform Six-Foot Precisions:

```wod
12 Burpees
2 Six-Foot Precisions
10 Burpees
4 Six-Foot Precisions
8 Burpees
6 Six-Foot Precisions
6 Burpees
8 Six-Foot Precisions
4 Burpees
10 Six-Foot Precisions
2 Burpees
12 Six-Foot Precisions
```

### Traceur — Expert

Same as Avancée, perform Seven-Foot Precisions:

```wod
12 Burpees
2 Seven-Foot Precisions
10 Burpees
4 Seven-Foot Precisions
8 Burpees
6 Seven-Foot Precisions
6 Burpees
8 Seven-Foot Precisions
4 Burpees
10 Seven-Foot Precisions
2 Burpees
12 Seven-Foot Precisions
```

## Cool Down

Perform 50 push ups as quickly as possible.
