## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile or bike 0.5 miles or row 250m followed by:

3 rounds of:
- 10 Air Squats
- 10 Sit Ups
- 10 Push Ups
- 10 Pull Ups

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

On the minute, for 5 minutes:

```wod
(5) :60 EMOM
  + 100m Sprint
```

### Avancée — Advanced

On the minute, for 7 minutes:

```wod
(7) :60 EMOM
  + 100m Sprint
```

### Traceur — Expert

On the minute, for 10 minutes:

```wod
(10) :60 EMOM
  + 100m Sprint
```

## Cool Down

100 Air Squats
