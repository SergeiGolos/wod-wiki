## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile OR bike 1/2 miles OR row 250m.

```wod
(3)
  50m Quadrupedal Movement
  5 Five-Foot Precisions
  10 Push Ups
  10 Double Unders
```

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

```wod
(20) :60 EMOM
  + 5 Push Ups
  + 5 Sit Ups
  + 5 Air Squats
```

### Traceur — Expert

After each minute perform 2 burpees. If you cannot complete all movements within the minute timeframe, stop the exercise, complete 2 burpees, and then move on to the next minute’s repetitions.

```wod
(5) :60 EMOM
  + 5 Push Ups
  + 5 Sit Ups
  + 5 Air Squats
  + 2 Burpees
(5) :60 EMOM
  + 10 Push Ups
  + 5 Sit Ups
  + 5 Air Squats
  + 2 Burpees
(5) :60 EMOM
  + 10 Push Ups
  + 10 Sit Ups
  + 5 Air Squats
  + 2 Burpees
(5) :60 EMOM
  + 10 Push Ups
  + 10 Sit Ups
  + 10 Air Squats
  + 2 Burpees
```
