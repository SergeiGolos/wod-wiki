## Warm Up

```wod
Run 1/4 mile | Bike 1/2 mile | Row 250m
(3)
  10 Air Squats
  10 Push Ups
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Air Squats
  10 Pull Ups // may sub Jumping Pull Ups
```

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

Perform 100 repetitions of ONE of the following movements for time:

```wod
100 Push Ups | 100 Sit Ups | 100 Jumping Pull Ups | 100 Air Squats
```

### Avancée — Advanced

Perform 100 repetitions of ONE of the following movements for time.
If you finish in under 10:00, rest 3:00 then complete 100 reps of one of the other movements.
If you do not finish in under 10:00, perform 50 repetitions of two of the other movements.

```wod
100 Push Ups | 100 Sit Ups | 100 Kipping Pull Ups | 100 Air Squats
If finish <10:00
  Rest 3:00
  Then 100 of a different movement
Else
  Perform 50 of two of the other movements
```

### Traceur — Expert

Perform 100 repetitions of ONE of the following movements for time.
If you finish in under 10:00, rest 3:00 then complete 100 reps of one of the other movements.
Attempt to do this for all 4 movements.

```wod
100 Push Ups | 100 Sit Ups | 100 Kipping Pull Ups | 100 Air Squats
If finish <10:00
  Rest 3:00
  Then 100 of another movement
Attempt to repeat for all 4 movements
```

## Cool Down

(3)
