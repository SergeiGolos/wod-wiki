## Warm Up

I hope everyone has a happy and safe New Years celebration!

Run 1/4 mile OR row 250m OR bike 1 mile.

```wod
3 rounds
  10 Air Squats
  10 Push Ups
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Pull Ups | Jumping Pull Ups
  10 Movement You Suck At
```

Perform a movement that you suck at. If it is already in the warm up, perform 15 repetitions of that movement instead of 10. For instance, if you suck at sit ups and they are in the warm up, do 3 rounds of 15 instead of 3 rounds of 10. If you suck at handstand push ups, perform 3 rounds of 10 handstand push ups (or the appropriate progression) and add that to the warm up.

## WOD

Forward quadrupedal movement — please note that this is NOT for time. Concentrate on the movement. Keep your back flat and your hips square. If you’re feeling a little frisky, try to do the movement as if you were on a six-inch rail.

### Étudiant — Beginner

```wod
200m Quadrupedal Movement
```

### Avancée — Advanced

```wod
300m Quadrupedal Movement
```

### Traceur — Expert

```wod
400m Quadrupedal Movement
```
