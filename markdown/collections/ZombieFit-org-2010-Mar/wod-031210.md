## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile OR row 250m OR bike 2 miles.

3 rounds of:
- 25m quadrupedal movement
- 10 jumping jacks
- 10 sit-ups
- 10 pull-ups or jumping pull-ups
- 10 air squats

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

```wod
(3)
  50 Sit Ups
  50 Push Ups
  50 Air Squats
```

### Avancée — Advanced

```wod
(5)
  50 Sit Ups
  50 Push Ups
  50 Air Squats
```

### Traceur — Expert

```wod
(3)
  25 Elbows to Knees
  25 Handstand Push Ups
  25 Alternating Pistols
```
