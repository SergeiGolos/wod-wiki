## Warm Up

Choose one of the following options for warm up:
*   Run 1/4 mile
*   Row 250m
*   Bike 2 miles

Followed by 3 rounds of:
*   10 Push Ups
*   10 Sit Ups
*   10 Jumping Jacks

## WOD

### Étudiant

Complete for time. The movement sequence is repeated three times, with a 5 meter quick movement (QM) in between each set.

```wod
(3)
  25 Air Squats
  25 Sit Ups
  25 Pushups
```

### Avancée

Complete for time. The movement sequence is repeated three times, with a 10 meter quick movement (QM) in between each set.

```wod
(3)
  35 Tuck Jumps
  35 Sit Ups
  35 Pushups
```

### Traceur

Complete for time. This workout consists of four distinct movements performed sequentially.

```wod
(4)
  50 Pistols or One Legged Air Squats (25 per leg)
  50 Sit Ups (with 10 second plank after set)
  50 Varied Pushups
  50 Dips
```
