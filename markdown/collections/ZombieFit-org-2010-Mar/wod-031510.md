## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile OR row 250m OR bike 2 miles.

3 rounds of:
- 10 Push Ups
- 10 Sit Ups
- 10 Jumping Jacks

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

Perform the following movements for time:

```wod
25 Reverse Wall Dips
5m Quadrupedal Movement
25 Knees To Elbows
5m Quadrupedal Movement
25 Tuck Jumps to Silent Landings
```

### Avancée — Advanced

Perform the following movements for time:

```wod
35 Reverse Wall Dips
5m Quadrupedal Movement
35 Knees To Elbows
5m Quadrupedal Movement
35 Tuck Jumps to Silent Landings
```

### Traceur — Expert

Perform the following movements for time:

```wod
35 Reverse Wall Dips
10m Quadrupedal Movement
35 Knees To Elbows
10m Quadrupedal Movement
35 Tuck Jumps to Silent Landings
10m Quadrupedal Movement
35 Skull Crushers
```
