## Warm Up

**Cardio Option:**
*   Run ?mile (1/4 mile) OR Row 250m OR Bike ?mile (2 miles).

**Circuit:**
(3) Rounds for time of:
*   25m Quadrupedal Movement
*   10 Dips
*   10 Five-foot Precisions
*   10 Pushups

## WOD

### Étudiant

```wod
(3)

  50 Jumping Pullups

```

### Avancée

```wod
(5)

```

### Traceur

This WOD requires completing the main circuit for time, followed by a distance finisher at the end of each round.

```wod
(5)
  50 Air Squats
  50 Jumping Pullups
  50 Sit Ups
```
**Finisher:** At the end of every round, complete one of the following: Row 250m OR Bike ?mile (1 mile) OR Run 300m.
