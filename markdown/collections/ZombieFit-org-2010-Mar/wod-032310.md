## WOD

### Étudiant (Beginner)

On the minute, for 10 minutes:
```wod
(10) :60 EMOM / 5 Pushups / 7 Situps / 9 Jumping Jacks
```
*If unable to complete the round within the minute time frame, perform 2 burpees before starting the next round.*

### Avancée (Intermediate)

On the minute, for 15 minutes:
```wod
(15) :60 EMOM / 5 Pullups / 7 Pushups / 9 Air Squats
```
*Pullup substitution: Jumping Pullups. If unable to complete the round within the minute time frame, perform 2 burpees before starting the next round.*

### Traceur (Advanced)

On the minute, for 20 minutes:
```wod
(20) :60 EMOM / 5 L-sit Pullups / 9 Pushups / 11 Air Squats
```
*If unable to complete the round within the minute time frame, perform 2 burpees before starting the next round.*
