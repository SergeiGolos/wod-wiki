## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile OR row 250m OR bike 2 miles.

```wod
3 rounds
  25m Quadrupedal Movement
  10 Jumping Jacks
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Pull Ups | Jumping Pull Ups
  10 Air Squats
```

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

On the minute, for ten minutes:

```wod
(10) :60 EMOM
  + 5 Push Ups
  + 7 Sit Ups
  + 9 Jumping Jacks
  - 2 Burpees // if round not completed within the minute
```

### Avancée — Advanced

On the minute, for fifteen minutes:

```wod
(15) :60 EMOM
  + 5 Pull Ups | Jumping Pull Ups
  + 7 Push Ups
  + 9 Air Squats
  - 2 Burpees // if round not completed within the minute
```

### Traceur — Expert

On the minute, for twenty minutes:

```wod
(20) :60 EMOM
  + 5 L-Sit Pull Ups
  + 9 Push Ups
  + 11 Air Squats
  - 2 Burpees // if round not completed within the minute
```
