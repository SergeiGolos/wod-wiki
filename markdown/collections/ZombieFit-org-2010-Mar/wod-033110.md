## Warm Up

**Cardio Option:** 1/4 mile Run OR 250m Row OR 2 miles Bike.

**Circuit (3 Rounds):**
*   10 Air Squats
*   10 Push Ups
*   10 Sit Ups
*   10 Pull Ups / Jumping Pull Ups
*   10 isuckatthis (perform a movement that you suck at)

***Note on Warm Up:** If the movement you choose to "suck at" is already listed in the warm-up circuit, perform 15 repetitions of that specific movement instead of 10. For example, if you struggle with sit ups, complete 3 rounds of 15 sit ups. If you struggle with handstand push ups, add 3 rounds of 10 handstand push ups (or progression) to the circuit.*

## WOD

### Étudiant
For Time:
```wod
(15-7-9)
  18 inch box jumps (sub with tuck jumps if you don’t have something to jump to)
  jumping pull ups
  alternating leg lunges
```

### Avancée
For Time:
```wod
(21-15-9)
  24 inch box jumps (sub with tuck jumps if you don’t have something to jump to)
  pull ups
  alternating leg lunges
```

### Traceur
For Time:
```wod
(21-15-9)
  30 inch box jumps (sub with tuck jumps if you don’t have something to jump to)
  pull ups
  alternating leg pistols
```
