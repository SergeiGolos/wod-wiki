## Warm Up

```wod
Run 1/4 mile OR Row 250m OR Bike 2 miles
(3)
  10 Air Squats
  10 Push Ups
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Pull Ups | Jumping Pull Ups
  10 I Suck At This
```

Perform a movement that you "suck at." If that movement is already in the warm up, do 15 repetitions of that movement instead of 10. For example, if you suck at sit ups and sit ups are in the warm up, do 3 rounds of 15 instead of 10. If you suck at handstand push ups, perform 3 rounds of 10 handstand push ups (or the appropriate progression) and add that to the warm up.

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

Substitute tuck jumps if you don’t have something to jump to.

```wod
(15-7-9)
  18in Box Jumps
  Jumping Pull Ups
  Alternating Leg Lunges
```
