## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile or bike 2 miles or row 250m.

3 rounds of:
- 10 Double Unders
- 10 Air Squats
- 10 Pull Ups
- 10 Sit Ups

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

Complete as many rounds as possible in 10 minutes of:

```wod
10:00 AMRAP
  5 Push Ups
  10 Sit Ups
  15 Air Squats
```

### Avancée — Advanced

Complete as many rounds as possible in 10 minutes of:

```wod
10:00 AMRAP
  10 Push Ups
  15 Sit Ups
  20 Air Squats
```

### Traceur — Expert

Complete as many rounds as possible in 10 minutes of:

```wod
10:00 AMRAP
  5 Handstand Push Ups
  10 Push Ups
  15 Air Squats
```
