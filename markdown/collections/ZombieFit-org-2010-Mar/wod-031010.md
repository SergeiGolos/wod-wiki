## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile OR row 250m OR bike 2 miles.

3 rounds of 10:
- 25m quadrupedal movement
- 10 jumping jacks
- 10 sit ups
- 10 pull ups or jumping pull ups
- 10 air squats

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

15-9-7 repetitions for time.

```wod
(15-9-7)
  12 inch Box Jumps
  Push Ups
  Alternating Leg Lunges
```

### Avancée — Advanced

21-15-9 repetitions for time.

```wod
(21-15-9)
  24 inch Box Jumps
  Push Ups
  Lunges Each Leg
```

### Traceur — Expert

21-15-9 repetitions for time. Lunges each leg — pistols may be used if able.

```wod
(21-15-9)
  24 inch Box Jumps
  Handstand Push Ups
  Lunges Each Leg
```
