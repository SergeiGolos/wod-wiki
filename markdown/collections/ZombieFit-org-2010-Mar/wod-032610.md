## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile OR row 250m OR bike 2 miles.

3 rounds of:
- 10 Push Ups
- 10 Sit Ups
- 10 Pull Ups or Jumping Pull Ups
- 10 Air Squats

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

```wod
300 Air Squats
```

### Avancée — Advanced

```wod
500 Air Squats
```

### Traceur — Expert

```wod
600 Air Squats
```
