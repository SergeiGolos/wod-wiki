## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile | row 250m | bike 2 miles.

3 rounds of:
- 25m quadrupedal movement
- 10 push ups
- 10 sit ups
- 10 pull ups
- 10 air squats

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

```wod
(5)
  50 Jumping Jacks
  15 Jumping Pull Ups
  25m Quadrupedal Movement
```

### Avancée — Advanced

```wod
(5)
  15 Burpees
  15 Pull Ups
  50m Quadrupedal Movement
```

### Traceur — Expert

```wod
(5)
  15 Pull-Up Burpees
  15 Elbows to Knees
  50m Quadrupedal Movement
```
