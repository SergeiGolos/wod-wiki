## Warm Up

WOD: 114
All levels:
Perform 3 rounds of the following circuit:
*   10 Push Ups
*   10 Sit Ups
*   10 Pull Ups
*   10 Air Squats

Follow this with a run/row/bike effort as quickly as possible.
