## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile | row 250m | bike 2 miles.

```wod
(3)
  25m Quadrupedal Movement
  10 Jumping Jacks
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Pull Ups | Jumping Pull Ups
  10 Air Squats
```

## WOD

sets of 21-15-9 reps

```wod
(21-15-9)
  Push Ups
  Sit Ups
  Air Squats
  Jumping Jacks
```
