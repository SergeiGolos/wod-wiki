## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile OR bike 2 miles OR row 500m followed by:

3 rounds of:
- 10 Pull Ups
- 10 Push Ups
- 10 Parkour Squats
- 10 Sit Ups

## WOD

```wod
400m Forward Quadrupedal Movement
```

## Cool Down

10 Precision Jumps at 85% of your max broad jump.
