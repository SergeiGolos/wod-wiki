## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile OR bike 1/2 mile OR row 250m.

```wod
(3)
  10 Air Squats
  10 Push Ups
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Air Squats
  10 Pull Ups (sub Jumping Pull Ups if needed)
```

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner
Choose ONE movement and perform 100 repetitions for time.

```wod
100 Push Ups
100 Sit Ups
100 Jumping Pull Ups
100 Air Squats
```

### Avancée — Advanced
Choose ONE movement and perform 100 repetitions for time. If you finish in under 10:00, rest 3:00 and then complete 100 repetitions of one of the other movements. If you do not finish in under 10:00, perform 50 repetitions of two of the other movements.

```wod
100 Push Ups
100 Sit Ups
100 Kipping Pull Ups
100 Air Squats
```

### Traceur — Expert
Choose ONE movement and perform 100 repetitions for time. If you finish in under 10:00, rest 3:00 and then complete 100 repetitions of another movement. Attempt to do this for all four movements if possible.

```wod
100 Push Ups
100 Sit Ups
100 Kipping Pull Ups
100 Air Squats
```

## Cool Down

```wod
(3)
  100m Sprint
```
