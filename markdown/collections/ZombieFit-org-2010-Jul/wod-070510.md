## Warm Up

*   Run 1/4 mile, Bike 1/2 mile, Row 250m.
*   Complete 3 rounds of the following circuit:
    *   10 Push Ups
    *   10 Sit Ups
    *   10 Air Squats
    *   10 Pull Ups (may substitute with jumping pull ups)
    *   10 Dips

## WOD

### Étudiant

```wod
(5)
  25m Quadrupedal Movement
  25 Jumping Jacks
  5 Five-Foot Precisions
```

### Avancée

```wod
(5)
  50m Quadrupedal Movement
  5 Burpees
  10 Five-Foot Precisions
```

### Traceur

```wod
(5)
  50m Quadrupedal Movement
  10 Burpees
  10 Six-Foot Precisions
```

## Cool Down

Perform 100 repetitions of only one exercise (Push Ups, Air Squats, Sit Ups, or Pull Ups). Continue performing the chosen exercise until failure.
