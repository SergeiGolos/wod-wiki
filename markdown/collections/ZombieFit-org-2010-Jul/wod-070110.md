## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile | bike 1/2 mile | row 250m.

```wod
(3)
  10 Air Squats
  10 Push Ups
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Air Squats
  10 Pull Ups
```

May substitute pull-ups with jumping pull-ups if needed.
