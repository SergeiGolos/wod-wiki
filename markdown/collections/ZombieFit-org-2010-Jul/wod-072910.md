## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile OR row 250m OR bike 2 miles.

3 rounds of:
- 25m quadrupedal movement
- 15 jumping jacks
- 15 push-ups
- 15 air squats

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

```wod
(3)
  200m Sprint
  50 Alternating Leg Lunges (25 each leg)
```

### Avancée — Advanced

```wod
(5)
  200m Sprint
  50 Alternating Leg Lunges (25 each leg)
```

### Traceur — Expert

```wod
(5)
  300m Sprint
  60 Alternating Leg Lunges (30 each leg)
```
