## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile OR bike 2 miles OR row 500m followed by:

```wod
(3)
  25m Quadrupedal Movement
  10 Push Ups
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Air Squats
  25 Jumping Jacks
```
