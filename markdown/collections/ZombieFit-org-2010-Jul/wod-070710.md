## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile | bike 2 miles | row 500m, followed by:

```wod
(3)
  10 Push Ups
  10 Air Squats
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Jumping Pull Ups
```

## WOD

Precision Ladder

With a continuously running clock do one precision the first minute, two precisions the second minute, three precisions the third minute… continuing as long as you are able. Use as many sets each minute as needed.

### Étudiant — Beginner

Stand in place and mark the spot where your feet are. Then find a spot approximately 4 feet away to precision to for the ladder.

```wod
:60 Intervals
  1 Precision
  2 Precisions
  3 Precisions
  4 Precisions
  5 Precisions
  Continue adding 1 Precision each minute until unable to continue
```

### Avancée — Advanced

Pick a precision length of 85% of your max broad jump. If possible precision over a gap or up to a ledge.

```wod
:60 Intervals
  1 Precision (85% max broad jump)
  2 Precisions (85% max broad jump)
  3 Precisions (85% max broad jump)
  Continue adding 1 Precision each minute until unable to continue
```

### Traceur — Expert

Max effort every precision. If possible, precision over a gap or to a ledge.

```wod
:60 Intervals
  1 Max-Effort Precision
  2 Max-Effort Precisions
  3 Max-Effort Precisions
  Continue adding 1 Max-Effort Precision each minute until failure
```

## Cool Down

In the smallest number of sets complete 50 pull ups followed by 50 push ups.

```wod
50 Pull Ups
50 Push Ups
```

(May sub with chin-ups, jumping pull-ups, | L-sit pull-ups depending on your ability level.)
