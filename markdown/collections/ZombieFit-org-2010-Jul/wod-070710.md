## Warm Up

Warm Up options (Choose one):
*   Run 1/4 mile
*   Bike 2 miles
*   Row 500m

Followed by 3 rounds of:
*   10 Push Ups
*   10 Air Squats
*   10 Sit Ups
*   10 Jumping Pull Ups

## WOD

### Étudiant

Precision Ladder (Continuous effort):
The goal is to complete a precision jump every minute, increasing the number of jumps each minute until failure.

```wod
(N) 1 min :? Precision Jumps ?m/gap
  - 1 jump
  - 2 jumps
  - 3 jumps
  ... (Continue escalating per minute)
```

*Instructions: Stand in place and mark the spot where your feet are. Then find a spot approximately 4 feet away. This is the target for precision.*

### Avancée

Precision Ladder (Continuous effort):
The goal is to complete a precision jump every minute, increasing the number of jumps each minute until failure. Use a length of 85% of max broad jump.

```wod
(N) 1 min :? Precision Jumps ?m/gap
  - 1 jump
  - 2 jumps
  - 3 jumps
  ... (Continue escalating per minute)
```

*Instructions: If possible, precision over a gap or up to a ledge.*

### Traceur

Precision Ladder (Continuous effort):
Max effort every precision. The goal is to complete a precision jump every minute, increasing the number of jumps each minute until failure.

```wod
(N) 1 min :? Precision Jumps ?m/gap
  - 1 jump
  - 2 jumps
  - 3 jumps
  ... (Continue escalating per minute)
```

*Instructions: If possible, precision over a gap or to a ledge.*

## Cool Down

Complete the following in the smallest number of sets possible:
*   50 Pull Ups (Sub with chin ups, jumping pull ups, or l-sit pull ups)
*   50 Push Ups
