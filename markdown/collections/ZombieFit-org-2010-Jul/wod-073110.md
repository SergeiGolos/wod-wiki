## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile | row 250m | bike 2 miles.

3 rounds of:
- 10 Air Squats
- 10 Push Ups
- 10 Sit Ups
- 10 Pull Ups | Jumping Pull Ups

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

```wod
(5)
  25m Quadrupedal Movement
  25 Jumping Jacks
  5 Five-Foot Precisions
```

### Avancée — Advanced

```wod
(5)
  50m Quadrupedal Movement
  5 Burpees
  10 Five-Foot Precisions
```

### Traceur — Expert

```wod
(5)
  50m Quadrupedal Movement
  10 Burpees
  10 Six-Foot Precisions
```

## Cool Down

Perform 100 repetitions of a single exercise (push-ups, air squats, sit-ups, or pull-ups). Only pick one movement and go to failure on every set.
