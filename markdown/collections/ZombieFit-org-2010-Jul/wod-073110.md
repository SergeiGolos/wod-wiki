## Warm Up

**Distance Options:**
*   Run 1/4 mile
*   Row 250m
*   Bike 2 miles

**Circuit (3 Rounds):**
Perform 3 rounds of the following circuit:
*   10 Air Squats
*   10 Push Ups
*   10 Sit Ups
*   10 Pull Ups (Jumping pull ups may be substituted for regular pull ups)

## WOD

### Étudiant

Perform 5 rounds for time of:
```wod
(5)
  25m Quadrupedal Movement
  25 Jumping Jacks
  5 Five-Foot Precisions
```

### Avancée

Perform 5 rounds for time of:
```wod
(5)
  50m Quadrupedal Movement
  5 Burpees
  10 Five-Foot Precisions
```

### Traceur

Perform 5 rounds for time of:
```wod
(5)
  50m Quadrupedal Movement
  10 Burpees
  10 Six-Foot Precisions
```

## Cool Down

Perform a total of 100 repetitions, selecting only one exercise from the following list. Go to failure on every set:
*   Push Ups
*   Air Squats
*   Sit Ups
*   Pull Ups
