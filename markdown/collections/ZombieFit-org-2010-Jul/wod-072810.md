## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile OR row 250m OR bike 2 miles.

3 rounds of:
- 10 push ups
- 10 sit ups
- 10 air squats
- 10 pull ups (may sub with jumping)

## WOD

You are to perform these exercises on the minute for at least 15 minutes but no longer than 20 minutes. If you fail to complete the repetitions within the minute before the 15 minute mark, stop the clock, perform 2 burpees, then restart the clock and continue. You must attempt the movements for 15 minutes. If you fail to complete the movements in the 1 minute time frame after 15 minutes, that is your last round.

### Étudiant — Beginner

```wod
(15-20) :60 EMOM
  3 Push Ups
  5 Air Squats
  7 Jumping Jacks
```

### Avancée — Advanced

```wod
(15-20) :60 EMOM
  3 Pull Ups (or 7 Jumping Pull Ups)
  5 Push Ups
  7 Air Squats
```

### Traceur — Expert

```wod
(15-20) :60 EMOM
  5 Pull Ups
  7 Push Ups
  9 Air Squats
```
