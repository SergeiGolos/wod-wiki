## Warm Up

Warm up options:
*   Run 1/4 mile OR row 250m OR bike 2 miles.
*   3 rounds of:
    *   10 push ups
    *   10 sit ups
    *   10 air squats
    *   10 pull ups (may substitute with jumping)

## WOD

WOD 190 is an EMOM performed for a minimum of 15 minutes and no more than 20 minutes.

**Rules:**
The athlete must attempt the movements for at least 15 minutes. If the required repetitions are missed in any minute round before the 15-minute mark, the clock stops. The athlete then performs 2 burpees, restarts the clock, and continues the movements until the next minute is completed.

### Étudiant
```wod
(15-20) EMOM / 3 pushups / 5 airsquats / 7 jumpingjacks
```

### Avancée
*Note: If performing jumping pull ups, substitute with 7 repetitions.*
```wod
(15-20) EMOM / 3 pullups / 5 pushups / 7 airsquats
```

### Traceur
```wod
(15-20) EMOM / 5 pullups / 7 pushups / 9 airsquats
```
