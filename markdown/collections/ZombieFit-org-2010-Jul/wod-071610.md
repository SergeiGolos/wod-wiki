## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile | bike 2 miles | row 500m followed by:

```wod
(3)
  10 Pull Ups
  10 Push Ups
  10 Parkour Squats
  10 Sit Ups
```

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

Perform the following for time; 50m quadrupedal movement between sets.

```wod
(21-15-9)
  Alternating Leg Lunges
  Push Ups
  50m Quadrupedal Movement
```

### Avancée — Advanced

Lunges performed for both legs (42 total reps on the first set) and push ups. 100m quadrupedal movement between sets.

```wod
(21-15-9)
  Lunges (Left Leg)
  Lunges (Right Leg)
  Push Ups
  100m Quadrupedal Movement
```

### Traceur — Expert

Pistols (one-legged squats): complete reps on one leg then the other (no alternating). Push ups as prescribed. 100m quadrupedal movement between sets.

```wod
(21-15-9)
  Pistols (Left)
  Pistols (Right)
  Push Ups
  100m Quadrupedal Movement
```

## Cool Down

In as few sets as possible, complete 75 air squats.

```wod
75 Air Squats
```
