## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile | bike 1/2 mile | row 250m.

3 rounds of:

```wod
(3)
  10 Air Squats
  10 Push Ups
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Air Squats
  10 Pull Ups
```

May substitute pull-ups with jumping pull-ups if needed.

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

On the minute, every minute, for 10 minutes. If you fail to complete all sets within a single minute, restart the round at the beginning of the next minute and add 1 additional burpee for each round you fail to complete.

```wod
(10) :60 EMOM
  + 2 Burpees
  + 5 Push Ups
  + 7 Air Squats
```

### Avancée — Advanced

On the minute, every minute, for 10 minutes. If you fail to complete all sets within a single minute, restart the round at the beginning of the next minute and add 1 additional burpee for each round you fail to complete.

```wod
(10) :60 EMOM
  + 5 Burpees
  + 7 Push Ups
  + 9 Air Squats
```

### Traceur — Expert

On the minute, every minute, for 10 minutes. If you fail to complete all sets within a single minute, restart the round at the beginning of the next minute and add 1 additional burpee for each round you fail to complete.

```wod
(10) :60 EMOM
  + 5 Burpees
  + 9 Push Ups
  + 10 Air Squats
```

## Cool Down

```wod
50 Sit Ups
```
