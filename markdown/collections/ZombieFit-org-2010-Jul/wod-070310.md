## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile, bike 1/2 mile, or row 250m.

3 rounds of:
- 10 alternating leg lunges
- 10 sit ups
- 10 push ups
- 10 air squats

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

Complete as many rounds as possible in 10 minutes of:

```wod
10:00 AMRAP
  5 Five-Foot Precisions
  50m Quadrupedal Movement
  10 Push Ups
```

### Avancée — Advanced

Complete as many rounds as possible in 15 minutes of:

```wod
15:00 AMRAP
  7 Seven-Foot Precisions
  50m Quadrupedal Movement
  10 Push Ups
```

### Traceur — Expert

Complete as many rounds as possible in 20 minutes of:

```wod
20:00 AMRAP
  7 Seven-Foot Precisions
  50m Quadrupedal Movement
  10 Push Ups
```

## Cool Down

25 pull ups (kipping, dead hang or jumping)
