## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile OR row 250m OR bike 2 miles.

3 rounds of:
- 25m quadrupedal movement
- 10 push ups
- 10 sit ups
- 10 pull ups or jumping pull ups
- 10 "perform a movement you suck at"

If the movement you suck at is already in the warm up, perform 15 repetitions of that movement instead of 10. For example, if you suck at sit ups and they are in the warm up, do 3 rounds of 15 sit ups. If you suck at handstand push ups, perform 3 rounds of 10 handstand push ups (or an appropriate progression) and add that to the warm up.

## WOD

For time, complete 21-15-9 repetitions of:

### Étudiant — Beginner

Perform the following movements for time:

```wod
(21-15-9)
  18 inch Box Jumps
  Jumping Pull Ups
```

### Avancée — Advanced

Perform the following movements for time:

```wod
(21-15-9)
  24 inch Box Jumps
  Pull Ups
```

### Traceur — Expert

Perform the following movements for time:

```wod
(21-15-9)
  24 inch Box Jumps
  Pull Ups
  Double Unders
```

If you do not have a box available, perform parkour squats (tuck jumps) instead.

## Cool Down

Hold a plank position to failure twice.
