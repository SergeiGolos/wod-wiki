## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile OR bike 1/2 mile OR row 250m.

```wod
(3)
  10 Parkour Squats / Air Squats
  10 Sit Ups
  50ft Quadrupedal Movement
```

## WOD

For time, complete 5 rounds of:

### Étudiant — Beginner

```wod
(5)
  5 Burpees
  5 Push Ups
  5 Sit Ups
  5 Jumping Jacks
```

### Avancée — Advanced

```wod
(5)
  5 Burpees
  10 Push Ups
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Jumping Jacks
```

### Traceur — Expert

```wod
(5)
  10 Burpees
  10 Push Ups
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Jumping Jacks
```

## Cool Down

```wod
50m Forward Quadrupedal Movement
50m Backward Quadrupedal Movement
```
