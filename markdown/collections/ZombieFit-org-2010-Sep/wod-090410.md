## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile OR bike 1/2 mile OR row 250m.

```wod
(3)
  10 Air Squats
  10 Push Ups
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Air Squats
  10 Pull Ups
```

May substitute pull-ups with jumping pull-ups if needed.

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

Complete as many rounds as possible in 10 minutes of:

```wod
10:00 AMRAP
  4 Push Ups
  6 Sit Ups
  6 Air Squats
```

### Avancée — Advanced

Complete as many rounds as possible in 15 minutes of:

```wod
15:00 AMRAP
  5 Push Ups
  7 Sit Ups
  9 Air Squats
```

### Traceur — Expert

Complete as many rounds as possible in 20 minutes of:

```wod
20:00 AMRAP
  5 Push Ups
  7 Sit Ups
  9 Air Squats
```
