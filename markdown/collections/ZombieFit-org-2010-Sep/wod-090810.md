## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile | bike 1/2 mile | row 250m.

3 rounds of:
- 10 Parkour squats / Air squats
- 10 Sit Ups
- 50ft Quadrupedal movement

Skill work — Precision practice: Perform 25 (Étudiant) or 50 (Avancée and Traceur) precisions at ~65% max effort. Concentrate on form: do NOT bounce out of your landing. Stick the landing, pause, then stand up and count to 2. This is not a conditioning exercise; take as much time as needed to perform these movements.

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

For time, complete 15-9-7 repetitions of: 5-foot precisions, dips and sit ups. After each round complete a 100m sprint OR bike 1/4 mile OR row 125m.

```wod
(15-9-7)
  5-Foot Precisions
  Dips
  Sit Ups
  100m Sprint | Bike 1/4 mile | Row 125m
```

### Avancée — Advanced

For time, complete 21-15-9 repetitions of: 6-foot precisions, dips and sit ups. After each round complete a 100m sprint OR bike 1/4 mile OR row 125m.

```wod
(21-15-9)
  6-Foot Precisions
  Dips
  Sit Ups
  100m Sprint | Bike 1/4 mile | Row 125m
```

### Traceur — Expert

For time, complete 21-15-9 repetitions of: 6-foot precisions, dips and elbows to knees. After each round complete a 100m sprint OR bike 1/4 mile OR row 125m.

```wod
(21-15-9)
  6-Foot Precisions
  Dips
  Elbows to Knees
  100m Sprint | Bike 1/4 mile | Row 125m
```

## Cool Down

Perform a plank hold to failure, times 3.
