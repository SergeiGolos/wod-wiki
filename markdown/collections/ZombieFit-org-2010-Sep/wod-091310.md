## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile or bike 2 miles or row 250m followed by:

```wod
(3)
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Push Ups
  10 Air Squats
  10 Pull Ups | Handstand Push Ups
```

## WOD

On the minute, for 10 minutes, complete the following:

### Étudiant — Beginner

```wod
(10) :60 EMOM
  100m Sprint
```

### Avancée — Advanced

```wod
(10) :60 EMOM
  + 100m Sprint
  + 10 Air Squats
```

### Traceur — Expert

```wod
(10) :60 EMOM
  + 100m Sprint
  + 10 Air Squats
  + 5 Push Ups
```

*This means that you complete the prescribed movements at the start of each minute. Once completed, you rest for the remaining time in the minute and then start over again at the beginning of the next minute.*
