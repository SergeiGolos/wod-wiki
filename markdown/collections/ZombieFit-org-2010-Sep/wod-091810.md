## Warm Up

Warm Up options:
*   Run 1/4 mile
*   Row 250m
*   Bike 2 miles

Circuit (3 rounds for time):
```wod
(3)
  25m Quadrupedal Movement
  10 Dips
  10 Five-foot Precisions
  10 Pushups
```

## WOD

WOD 225: CrossFit’s “Cindy” AMRAP (20 minutes total)

### Étudiant

Complete as many rounds as possible in 20 minutes of:
```wod
20:00 AMRAP / 5 Jumping Pullups / 10 Pushups / 15 Air Squats
```

### Avancée

Complete as many rounds as possible in 20 minutes of:
```wod
20:00 AMRAP / 5 Pullups / 10 Pushups / 15 Air Squats
```

### Traceur

Complete as many rounds as possible in 20 minutes of:
```wod
20:00 AMRAP / 5 L-sit Pullups / 10 Pushups / 15 Air Squats
```

## Cool Down

Perform 10 handstand push ups. Concentrate on your form. If possible, do them free-standing.
