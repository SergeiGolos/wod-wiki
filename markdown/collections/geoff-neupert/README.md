# Geoff Neupert Kettlebell Workouts

**Category**: Strength & Conditioning / Kettlebell Specialist  
**Founder**: Geoff Neupert  
**Philosophy**: "Build a better body through intelligent kettlebell training." - Structured progression with focus on results.

---

## Overview

Geoff Neupert is a Master StrongFirst Certified Instructor (SFG II) and former RKC Team Leader who has developed numerous kettlebell programs focused on building strength, muscle, and conditioning efficiently. His programs are known for their structured approach, clear progressions, and time-efficient formats that respect the busy lives of his clients. Geoff's programming philosophy centers on "minimum effective dose" - using the least amount of training necessary to achieve the desired result.

{{workouts}}

Geoff Neupert's contributions to kettlebell training include programs like "Kettlebell Muscle," "Kettlebell Burn," "Easy Muscle," and numerous complex-based programs. His approach often uses complexes - sequences of movements performed without setting the weight down - to maximize training efficiency. He emphasizes that most people don't need more complicated programs; they need consistently executed, well-designed programs that fit into their lives.
