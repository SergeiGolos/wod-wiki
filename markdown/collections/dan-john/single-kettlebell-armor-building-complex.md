## Workout 3: Single Kettlebell Armor Building Complex

**Category**: Dan John Complex  
**Type**: Strength Complex  
**Difficulty**: Intermediate

### Description

This single-kettlebell version of the Armor Building Complex provides similar benefits to the double version while requiring only one bell. The complex alternates between left and right sides, building unilateral strength and addressing imbalances. This version is more accessible for those without matching pairs of kettlebells.

The single-kettlebell complex involves a clean and press on one side, a swing to switch hands, then a clean and press on the other side, followed by squats or additional movements. While the total load is reduced compared to doubles, the unilateral nature challenges stability and core strength in different ways.

```wod
(5)
  + 1 Clean & Press Left 24kg
  + 1 Swing
  + 1 Clean & Press Right 24kg
  + 2 Goblet Squat 24kg
  :45 Rest
```

### Breakdown

- **Rounds/Duration**: 5 rounds
- **Exercises**: Clean & Press, Swing, Goblet Squat
- **Goal**: Complete all rounds with good form

### Collectible Metrics

- Rounds completed
- Weight used
- Quality of movement
