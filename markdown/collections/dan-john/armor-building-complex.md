## Workout 2: Armor Building Complex (ABC)

**Category**: Dan John Complex  
**Type**: Strength Complex  
**Difficulty**: Intermediate to Advanced

### Description

The Armor Building Complex is Dan John's signature double kettlebell complex, originally designed as a "graduation workout" for the RKC kettlebell certification. The complex consists of 2 cleans, 1 press, and 3 front squats, performed as a continuous sequence without setting the bells down. This deceptively simple combination builds total-body strength, power, and conditioning.

The term "armor building" reflects the program's ability to develop a resilient, muscular physique that can withstand impact - valuable for contact sports and life in general. The complex targets the entire body: cleans develop explosive hip power, presses build overhead strength and shoulder stability, and front squats develop leg strength and core stability. When performed for multiple rounds with appropriate weight, the ABC creates significant metabolic demand.

```wod
(5)
  + 2 Double Clean 24kg
  + 1 Double Press 24kg
  + 3 Double Front Squat 24kg
  1:00 Rest
```

### Breakdown

- **Rounds/Duration**: 5 rounds
- **Exercises**: Double Clean, Double Press, Double Front Squat
- **Goal**: Complete all rounds with good form
