## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile OR row 250m OR bike .2 miles.

```wod
(3)
  25m Quadrupedal Movement
  10 Push Ups
  10 Pull Ups or Jumping Pull Ups
  10 Isuckatthis
```

Perform a movement that you "suck at." If that movement is already in the warm up, perform 15 repetitions of that movement instead of 10. For instance, if you suck at sit ups and sit ups are in the warm up, do 3 rounds of 15 sit ups. If you suck at handstand push ups, perform 3 rounds of 10 handstand push ups (or a scaled progression) and add that to the warm up.

## WOD

WOD 550 — "Annie" (50-40-30-20-10) double unders and sit ups. If you don’t have a jump rope, substitute double unders with tuck jumps.

### Étudiant — Beginner

Perform single-unders with rounds of 100-80-60-40-20.

If you do not have a jump rope, sub with tuck jumps in rounds of 30-20-10.

```wod
(100-80-60-40-20)
  Single Unders
  Sit Ups
```

### Avancée — Advanced

WOD as prescribed (Rx): 50-40-30-20-10 double unders and sit ups. If no rope, sub double unders with tuck jumps.

```wod
(50-40-30-20-10)
  Double Unders
  Sit Ups
```
