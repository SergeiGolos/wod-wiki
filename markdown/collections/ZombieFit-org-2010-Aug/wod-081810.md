## Warm Up

Warm Up options: Run 1/4 mile OR Row 250m OR Bike 2 miles.

Perform 3 rounds of:
*   25m quadrupedal movement
*   10 push ups
*   10 sit ups
*   10 pull ups/jumping pull ups
*   10 isuckatthis (perform a movement that you suck at).

**Scaling Note:** If the "sucked at" movement is already in the warm up, perform 15 repetitions instead of 10. If it's a new movement (e.g., handstand push ups), add 3 rounds of 10 reps for that movement to the warm up.
