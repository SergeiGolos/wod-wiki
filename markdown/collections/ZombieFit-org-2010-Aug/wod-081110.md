## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile OR row 250m OR bike 2 miles.

3 rounds of:
- 25m quadrupedal movement
- 10 push-ups
- 10 sit-ups
- 10 pull-ups or jumping pull-ups

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

On the minute for 20 minutes perform 5 push-ups, 5 sit-ups and 5 air squats.

```wod
(20) :60 EMOM
  + 5 Push Ups
  + 5 Sit Ups
  + 5 Air Squats
```

### Avancée — Advanced

Progressive EMOM: increase reps every 5 minutes.

```wod
(5) :60 EMOM
  + 5 Push Ups
  + 5 Sit Ups
  + 5 Air Squats

(5) :60 EMOM
  + 10 Push Ups
  + 5 Sit Ups
  + 5 Air Squats

(5) :60 EMOM
  + 10 Push Ups
  + 10 Sit Ups
  + 5 Air Squats

(5) :60 EMOM
  + 10 Push Ups
  + 10 Sit Ups
  + 10 Air Squats
```

### Traceur — Expert

Same as Advanced, but perform 2 burpees after each minute's work. If you cannot complete the movements within the minute, stop, complete 2 burpees, then move on to the next minute.

```wod
(5) :60 EMOM
  + 5 Push Ups
  + 5 Sit Ups
  + 5 Air Squats
  + 2 Burpees

(5) :60 EMOM
  + 10 Push Ups
  + 5 Sit Ups
  + 5 Air Squats
  + 2 Burpees

(5) :60 EMOM
  + 10 Push Ups
  + 10 Sit Ups
  + 5 Air Squats
  + 2 Burpees

(5) :60 EMOM
  + 10 Push Ups
  + 10 Sit Ups
  + 10 Air Squats
  + 2 Burpees
```
