## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile OR bike 2 miles OR row 250m followed by:

3 rounds of:
- 10 Push Ups
- 10 Sit Ups
- 10 Pull Ups (may sub with jumping pull-ups)
- 10 Air Squats

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

```wod
(3)
  100m Sprint
  15 Tuck Jumps
  15 Sit Ups
```

### Avancée — Advanced

```wod
(5)
  100m Sprint
  15 Tuck Jumps
  15 Sit Ups
```

### Traceur — Expert

```wod
(5)
  100m Sprint
  25 Tuck Jumps
  25 Sit Ups
```

If you do not have an area to run in, perform 1 minute of bicycle kicks (lay on your back and act like you're pedaling a bicycle).
