## Warm Up

Warm up options include running 1/4 mile, biking 2 miles, or rowing 250m. This should be followed by 3 rounds of:
*   10 Push Ups
*   Sit Ups
*   Pull Ups (may substitute with jumping pull ups)
*   Air Squats

## WOD

### Étudiant
```wod
(3)
  100m Run
  15 Tuck Jumps
  15 Sit Ups
```

### Avancée
```wod
(5)
  100m Run
  15 Tuck Jumps
  15 Sit Ups
```

### Traceur
```wod
(5)
  100m Run
  25 Tuck Jumps
  25 Sit Ups
```
*If you do not have an area to run in, perform 1 minute of bicycle kicks (lay on your back and act like you’re peddling a bicycle).*
