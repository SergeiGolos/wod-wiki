## WOD

### Étudiant
```wod
For Time:
  200m Lunge Walk (Forward)
  100m Lunge Walk (Backward)
```
*Instructions: Make sure your knee touches the ground on every lunge.*

### Avancée
```wod
For Time:
  300m Lunge Walk (Forward)
  200m Lunge Walk (Backward)
```
*Instructions: Make sure your knee touches the ground on every lunge.*

### Traceur
```wod
For Time:
  400m Lunge Walk (Forward)
  300m Lunge Walk (Backward)
```
*Instructions: Make sure your knee touches the ground on every lunge.*
