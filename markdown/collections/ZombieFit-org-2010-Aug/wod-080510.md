## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile OR row 250m OR bike 2 miles.

3 rounds of:
- 10 Air Squats
- 10 Push Ups
- 10 Sit Ups
- 10 Pull Ups (you may sub jumping pull ups for regular pull ups)

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

Make sure your knee touches the ground on every lunge.

```wod
200m Lunge Walk
100m Lunge Walk Backwards
```

### Avancée — Advanced

Make sure your knee touches the ground on every lunge.

```wod
300m Lunge Walk
200m Lunge Walk Backwards
```

### Traceur — Expert

Make sure your knee touches the ground on every lunge.

```wod
400m Lunge Walk
300m Lunge Walk Backwards
```

## Cool Down

2 x 100m sprint
