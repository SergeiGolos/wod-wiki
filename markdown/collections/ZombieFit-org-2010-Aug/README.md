## ZombieFit.org August 2010 Collection

Welcome to the August collection! This month focuses on building endurance and explosive power through a mix of running intervals, bodyweight circuits, and dynamic movements inspired by urban parkour training. Expect high intensity across all levels.

### Workouts

#### WOD 193 (Aug 1)
**Warm Up:** Run 1/4 mile OR row 250m OR bike 2 miles. Complete 3 rounds of: 10 air squats, 10 push ups, 10 sit ups, 10 pull ups.

**WOD:** For time:
```wod
(1)
  100m backwards sprint
```

#### WOD 194 (Aug 2)
**Warm Up:** Run 1/4 mile OR row 250m OR bike 2 miles. Complete 3 rounds of: 10 air squats, 10 push ups, 10 sit ups, 10 pull ups.

**WOD:** For time:
```wod
(15-7-9)
  12 inch box jumps
  push ups
```

#### WOD 196 (Aug 5)
**Warm Up:** Run 1/4 mile OR row 250m OR bike 2 miles. Complete 3 rounds of: 10 air squats, 10 push ups, 10 sit ups, 10 pull ups.

**WOD:** For time:
```wod
(1)
  Lunge walk 200m
  Lunge walk backwards 100m
```

#### WOD 197 (Aug 9)
**Warm Up:** Run 1/4 mile OR row 250m OR bike 2 miles. Complete 3 rounds of: 10 air squats, 10 push ups, 10 sit ups, 10 pull ups.

**WOD:** For time:
```wod
(5)
  10 burpees
  10 push ups
```
*(Advanced variation provided)*

#### WOD 198 (Aug 10)
**Warm Up:** Run 1/4 mile OR row 250m OR bike 2 miles. Complete 3 rounds of: 25m quadrupedal movement, 10 push ups, 10 sit ups, 10 pull ups/jumping pull ups.

**WOD:** For time:
```wod
(5)
  Run 100m
  10 five foot precisions
  10 push ups
  10 air squats
```
*(Advanced variation provided)*

### Week Summary

*   **Week of Aug 1:** Focus on short sprints and foundational bodyweight strength.
*   **Week of Aug 2:** Plyometrics and classic rep schemes (15-7-9).
*   **Week of Aug 5:** Lunge endurance and controlled movement patterns.
*   **Week of Aug 9:** High-volume circuit training using burpees and push ups.
*   **Week of Aug 10:** Combining running intervals with complex bodyweight movements (precisions, squats).
