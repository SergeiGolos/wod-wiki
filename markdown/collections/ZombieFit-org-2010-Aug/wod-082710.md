## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile OR bike 1/2 mile OR row 250m.

3 rounds of:
- 10 Air Squats
- 10 Push Ups
- 10 Sit Ups
- 10 Air Squats
- 10 Pull Ups or Jumping Pull Ups

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

For time, complete 15-9-7 repetitions of: 5-foot precisions, dips and sit ups. After each round complete a 100m sprint or bike 1/4 mile or row 125m.

```wod
(15-9-7)
  Five-Foot Precisions
  Dips
  Sit Ups
  100m Sprint or Bike 1/4 mile or Row 125m
```

### Avancée — Advanced

For time, complete 21-15-9 repetitions of: 6-foot precisions, dips and sit ups. After each round complete a 100m sprint or bike 1/4 mile or row 125m.

```wod
(21-15-9)
  Six-Foot Precisions
  Dips
  Sit Ups
  100m Sprint or Bike 1/4 mile or Row 125m
```

### Traceur — Expert

For time, complete 21-15-9 repetitions of: 6-foot precisions, dips and elbows to knees. After each round complete a 100m sprint or bike 1/4 mile or row 125m.

```wod
(21-15-9)
  Six-Foot Precisions
  Dips
  Elbows to Knees
  100m Sprint or Bike 1/4 mile or Row 125m
```

## Cool Down

Perform a plank hold to failure, 3 times.
