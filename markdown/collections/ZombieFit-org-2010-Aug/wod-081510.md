## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile or bike 2 miles or row 250m followed by:

```wod
(3)
  10 Push Ups
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Pull Ups or 10 Jumping Pull Ups
  10 Air Squats
```

## WOD

Compare to your rep count for WOD 31.

Pull-ups may be substituted with jumping pull-ups. Each repetition is 1 point. No rest in-between sets.

### Étudiant — Beginner

Tabata squats then Tabata push ups.

```wod
(8) :20 Tabata
  Air Squats
(8) :20 Tabata
  Push Ups
```

### Avancée — Advanced

Tabata squats, Tabata push ups, Tabata sit ups, then Tabata pull ups.

```wod
(8) :20 Tabata
  Air Squats
(8) :20 Tabata
  Push Ups
(8) :20 Tabata
  Sit Ups
(8) :20 Tabata
  Pull Ups
```

### Traceur — Expert

Tabata squats, Tabata push ups, Tabata sit ups, Tabata pull ups, and Tabata five-foot precisions.

```wod
(8) :20 Tabata
  Air Squats
(8) :20 Tabata
  Push Ups
(8) :20 Tabata
  Sit Ups
(8) :20 Tabata
  Pull Ups
(8) :20 Tabata
  Five-Foot Precisions
```
