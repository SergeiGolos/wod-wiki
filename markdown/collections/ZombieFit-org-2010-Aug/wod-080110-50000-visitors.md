## Warm Up

Choose one option for the run/row/bike portion, then complete 3 rounds of the circuit.

**Option:**
*   Run: ?mile (1/4 mile)
*   Row: ?m (250m)
*   Bike: ?mile (2 miles)

**Circuit (3 Rounds):**
*   10 Air Squats
*   10 Push Ups
*   10 Sit Ups
*   10 Pull Ups (Jumping pull ups may be substituted for regular pull ups)

## Cool Down

Perform a single exercise for 100 repetitions (Push Ups, Air Squats, Sit Ups, or Pull Ups). Select only one movement. Go to failure on every set. Choose an exercise different from yesterday’s WOD.
