## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile OR row 250m OR bike 2 miles.

```wod
(3)
  10 Air Squats
  10 Push Ups
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Pull Ups or Jumping Pull Ups
```

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

Perform the following movements for time:

```wod
100m Backwards Sprint
1:00 Front-Lean and Rest
100m Backwards Sprint
10 Burpees
100m Backwards Sprint
10 Ground Kongs
100m Sprint
10 Burpees
100m Sprint
1:00 Front-Lean and Rest
100m Sprint
```

### Avancée — Advanced

Perform the following movements for time:

```wod
100m Backwards Sprint
1:30 Front-Lean and Rest
100m Backwards Sprint
20 Burpees
100m Backwards Sprint
20 Ground Kongs
100m Sprint
20 Burpees
100m Sprint
1:30 Front-Lean and Rest
100m Sprint
```

### Traceur — Expert

Perform the following movements for time:

```wod
100m Backwards Sprint
2:00 Front-Lean and Rest
100m Backwards Sprint
30 Burpees
100m Backwards Sprint
30 Ground Kongs
100m Sprint
30 Burpees
100m Sprint
2:00 Front-Lean and Rest
100m Sprint
```

*This is the push-up position fully extended. Your back, butt and stomach are all flat. Keep your arms underneath your shoulders. Push through the floor.*

## Cool Down

Perform 100 repetitions of one of the following movements. Only pick one exercise. When performing the exercises, go to failure on every set. Pick a different movement from yesterday’s WOD.

```wod
100 Push Ups
100 Air Squats
100 Sit Ups
100 Pull Ups
```
