## Warm Up

```wod
Run 1/4 mile | Bike 1/2 mile | Row 250m

(3)
  10 Air Squats
  10 Push Ups
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Air Squats
  10 Pull Ups // may sub with Jumping Pull Ups
```

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

In as few sets as possible, complete:

```wod
30 Push Ups
30 Air Squats
30 Jumping Pull Ups
30 Sit Ups
```

### Avancée — Advanced

In as few sets as possible, complete:

```wod
50 Push Ups
50 Air Squats
50 Jumping Pull Ups
50 Sit Ups
```

### Traceur — Expert

In the fewest sets possible, complete:

```wod
75 Push Ups
75 Air Squats
75 Jumping Pull Ups
75 Sit Ups
```

## Cool Down

```wod
2x 100m Sprint
```
