## Warm Up

Warm up options include:
*   Run 1/4 mile OR Row 250m OR Bike 2 miles.

Perform 3 rounds of:
```wod
(3)
  25m Quadrupedal Movement
  10 Pushups
  10 Situps
  10 Pullups / Jumping Pullups
```
