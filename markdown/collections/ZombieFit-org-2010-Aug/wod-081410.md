## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile or bike 2 miles or row 250m followed by:

```wod
(3)
  10 Push Ups
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Pull Ups (may sub w/ Jumping Pull Ups)
  10 Air Squats
```

## WOD

WOD 201

Compare to your time for WOD 81.

### Étudiant — Beginner

```wod
(21-15-9)
  5-Foot Precisions
  Jumping Pull Ups
```

### Avancée — Advanced

```wod
(21-15-9)
  6-Foot Precisions
  Pull Ups (Kipping or Dead Hang)
```

### Traceur — Expert

```wod
(21-15-9)
  6-Foot Precisions
  Double Unders
  Pull Ups (Kipping or Dead Hang)
```

## Post

Post time to comments.

For these precisions, make sure that you are concentrating on form. This is a metabolic conditioning workout, but don’t let your precision form and technique go to crap. Get full extension on your jump, pick up your knees, spot your landing and land quietly on the balls of your feet. ABSORB the impact.
