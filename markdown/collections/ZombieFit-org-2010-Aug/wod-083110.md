## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile OR bike 1/2 mile OR row 250m.

```wod
(3)
  10 Air Squats
  10 Push Ups
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Air Squats
  10 Pull Ups
```

May substitute pull-ups with jumping pull-ups if needed.

## WOD

Make sure your knee touches the ground on every lunge.

### Étudiant — Beginner

```wod
200m Lunge Walk
100m Lunge Walk Backwards
```

### Avancée — Advanced

```wod
300m Lunge Walk
200m Lunge Walk Backwards
```

### Traceur — Expert

```wod
400m Lunge Walk
300m Lunge Walk Backwards
```
