## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile | row 250m | bike 2 miles.

```wod
(3)
  25m Quadrupedal Movement
  10 Push Ups
  10 Pull Ups | Jumping Pull Ups
  10 Movement You Suck At
```

Perform a movement that you "suck at." If that movement is already in the warm up, perform 15 repetitions of that movement instead of 10. For example, if you suck at sit ups and sit ups are in the warm up, do 3 rounds of 15 sit ups. If you suck at handstand push ups, perform 3 rounds of 10 handstand push ups (or an appropriate progression) and add that to the warm up.

## WOD

Skill work: Max effort broad jump. Start with precision jumps at 4 feet distance — concentrate on form and landings. Perform five repetitions at each distance and increase distance until you reach your max precision jump. Once you reach a max precision, perform 10 max-effort broad jumps.

```wod
5 Precision Jumps 4ft
5 Precision Jumps 5ft
5 Precision Jumps 6ft
Repeat 5 Precision Jumps, increasing distance until you reach your max precision
Max Precision Jump ?ft
10 Max Effort Broad Jumps
```
