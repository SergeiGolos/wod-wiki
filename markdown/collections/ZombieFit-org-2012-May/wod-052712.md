## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile OR row 250m OR bike 2 miles.

3 rounds of:
- 10 Air Squats
- 10 Push Ups
- 10 Sit Ups
- 10 Pull Ups or Jumping Pull Ups

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

```wod
(5)
  10 Burpees
  10 Push Ups
```

### Avancée — Advanced

```wod
(5)
  10 Burpees
  15 Push Ups
```

### Traceur — Expert

```wod
(10)
  5 Burpees
  5 Handstand Push Ups
```
