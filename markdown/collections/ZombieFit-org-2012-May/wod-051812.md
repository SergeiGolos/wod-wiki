## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile OR bike 1/2 mile OR row 250m.

```wod
(3)
  10 Air Squats
  10 Push Ups
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Air Squats
  10 Pull Ups
```

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

For time, perform 100 repetitions of ONE of the following movements:

```wod
100 Push Ups
100 Sit Ups
100 Jumping Pull Ups
100 Air Squats
```

### Avancée — Advanced

For time, perform 100 repetitions of ONE of the following movements:

```wod
100 Push Ups
100 Sit Ups
100 Kipping Pull Ups
100 Air Squats
```

If you finish in under ten minutes, rest three minutes and then complete 100 repetitions of one of the other movements. If you do not finish in under 10 minutes, perform 50 repetitions of two of the other movements.

### Traceur — Expert

For time, perform 100 repetitions of ONE of the following movements:

```wod
100 Push Ups
100 Sit Ups
100 Kipping Pull Ups
100 Air Squats
```

If you finish in under ten minutes, rest three minutes and then complete 100 repetitions of one of the other movements. Attempt to do this for all 4 movements.

## Cool Down

```wod
(3)
  100m Sprint
```
