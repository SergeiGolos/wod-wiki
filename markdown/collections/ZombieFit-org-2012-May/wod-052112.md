## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile, bike 1/2 mile, or row 250 m.

```wod
(3)
  10 Air Squats
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Push Ups
  10 Jumping Pull Ups
```

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

Make sure your knee touches the ground on every lunge.

```wod
100m Lunge Walk
100m Lunge Walk Backwards
```

### Avancée — Advanced

Same as above for 200m each direction. Make sure your knee touches the ground on every lunge.

```wod
200m Lunge Walk
200m Lunge Walk Backwards
```

### Traceur — Expert

Same as above for 300m each direction. Make sure your knee touches the ground on every lunge.

```wod
300m Lunge Walk
300m Lunge Walk Backwards
```

## Cool Down

```wod
(3)
  50m Quadrupedal Movement
```

You may rest in between sets.
