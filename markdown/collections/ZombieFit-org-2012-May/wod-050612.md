## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile OR row 250m OR bike 2 miles.

```wod
(3)
  25m Quadrupedal Movement
  10 Push Ups
  10 Pull Ups or Jumping Pull Ups
  10 I Suck At This
```

Perform a movement that you "suck at." If that movement is already in the warm up, perform 15 repetitions of that movement instead of 10. For instance, if you suck at sit ups and sit ups are in the warm up, do 3 rounds of 15 instead of 10. If you suck at handstand push ups, perform 3 rounds of 10 handstand push ups (or an appropriate progression) and add that to the warm up.

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

```wod
50 Burpees
```

### Avancée — Advanced

```wod
100 Burpees
```

### Traceur — Expert

```wod
100 Pull-Up or Precision Burpees
```

Instead of jumping up and clapping your hands over your head at the end of the burpee, perform either a pull-up or a five- to six-foot precision jump. After that movement drop down into your press.
