## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile OR bike 1/2 mile OR row 250m followed by:

```wod
(3)
  10 Push Ups
  10 Air Squats
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Jumping Pull Ups
```

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

For time, complete 5 rounds of:

```wod
(5)
  100m Sprint
  5 Push Ups
  5 Sit Ups
  5 Air Squats
```

### Avancée — Advanced

For time, complete 7 rounds of:

```wod
(7)
  100m Sprint
  5 Push Ups
  5 Sit Ups
  5 Air Squats
```

### Traceur — Expert

For time, complete 10 rounds of:

```wod
(10)
  100m Sprint
  5 Push Ups
  5 Sit Ups
  5 Air Squats
```

## Cool Down

```wod
Handstand Hold Against Wall to Failure x3
```
