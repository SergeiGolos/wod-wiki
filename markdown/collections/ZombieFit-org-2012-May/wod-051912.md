## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile OR row 250m OR bike 1 mile.

```wod
(3)
  10 Squats
  10 Push Ups
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Pull Ups | Jumping Pull Ups
```

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

Perform the following movements for time:

```wod
(15-9-7)
  12 inch Box Jumps
  Push Ups
  Sit Ups
```

### Avancée — Advanced

Perform the following movements for time:

```wod
(21-15-9)
  24 inch Box Jumps
  Push Ups
  Sit Ups
```

### Traceur — Expert

If needed, substitute parkour squats for box jumps.

Perform the following movements for time:

```wod
(21-15-9)
  24 inch Box Jumps
  Push Ups
  Elbows to Knees
```

## Cool Down

```wod
100m Quadrupedal Movement
```

Remember that when completing a WOD for time, you should complete the exercises as quickly as possible with minimal or no rest between repetitions and sets. Attempt to go at a pace that is sustainable. If you do not have experience with metabolic conditioning workouts, it is strongly suggested that you perform several WODs at an Étudiant level before moving on to Avancée or Traceur.

When performing box jumps, ensure that you land with both feet firmly planted. Stand up to your full height and activate (push forward) your hips. Don’t forget to pick up your knees when performing the jumps.
