## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile | bike 1 mile | row 250m.

3 rounds of:
- 10 Air Squats
- 10 Sit Ups
- 10 Pull Ups (may sub with Push Ups)

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

```wod
(5)
  15 Lunges
  5 Burpees
```

### Avancée — Advanced

```wod
(5)
  15 Lunges
  5 Burpee Pull Ups
```

### Traceur — Expert

```wod
(5)
  15 Lunges
  10 Burpee Pull Ups
```

## Cool Down

Three 100m sprints with 2 minutes rest in between each run.
