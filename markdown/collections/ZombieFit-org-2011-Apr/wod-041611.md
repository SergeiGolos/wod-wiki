## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile OR row 250m OR bike 2 miles.

```wod
(3)
  10 Air Squats
  10 Push Ups
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Pull Ups or Jumping Pull Ups
  10 Movement You Suck At
```

Perform a movement that you "suck at." If that movement is already in the warm up, perform 15 repetitions of that movement instead of 10. For example, if you suck at sit ups and sit ups are in the warm up, do 3 rounds of 15. If you suck at handstand push ups, perform 3 rounds of 10 HSPU (or an appropriate progression) and add that to the warm up.

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

```wod
(3)
  50ft Quadrupedal Movement
  15 Jumping Pull Ups
  25 Push Ups
```

### Avancée — Advanced

```wod
(5)
  50ft Quadrupedal Movement
  15 Pull Ups (Kipping)
  25 Push Ups
```

### Traceur — Expert

Keep legs straight, tighten your abs, and fully extend your arms on L-sit pull ups — no cheating.

```wod
(5)
  100ft Quadrupedal Movement
  15 L-Sit Pull Ups
  50 Push Ups
```
