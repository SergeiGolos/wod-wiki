## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile OR bike 1/2 mile OR row 250m.

```wod
(3)
  10 Push Ups
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Air Squats
  10 Pull Ups or Jumping Pull Ups
  10 Dips
```

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

```wod
(5)
  25m Quadrupedal Movement
  25 Jumping Jacks
  5 Five-Foot Precisions
```

### Avancée — Advanced

```wod
(5)
  50m Quadrupedal Movement
  5 Burpees
  10 Five-Foot Precisions
```

### Traceur — Expert

```wod
(5)
  50m Quadrupedal Movement
  10 Burpees
  10 Six-Foot Precisions
```

## Cool Down

```wod
100 Repetitions // choose one
  Push Ups
  Air Squats
  Sit Ups
  Pull Ups
```

Only pick one exercise. When performing the sets, go to failure on every set.
