## WOD

### Étudiant
Complete 21-15-9 repetitions for time of:
```wod
(21-15-9)
  12 inch Box Jumps
  Push Ups
  Sit Ups
```

### Avancée
Complete 21-15-9 repetitions for time of:
```wod
(21-15-9)
  24 inch Box Jumps
  Push Ups
  Knees to Elbows
```

### Traceur
Complete 21-15-9 repetitions for time of:
```wod
(21-15-9)
  36 inch Box Jumps
  Push Ups
  Knees to Elbows
```

**Notes:**
*   If needed, substitute parkour squats for box jumps.
*   This workout is completed as fast as possible with minimal rest.
*   **Breakdown (21-15-9):** 21 reps of movement A, 21 reps of movement B, 21 reps of movement C, then 15 reps of each, and finally 9 reps of each.
