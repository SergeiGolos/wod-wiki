## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile OR row 250m OR bike 2 miles.

Perform 3 rounds of the following. For the "movement you suck at" choose a movement you need to practice — if it is already in the warm up, perform 15 reps instead of 10; otherwise perform 10 reps (or a scaled progression).

```wod
(3)
  10 Air Squats
  10 Push Ups
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Pull Ups | Jumping Pull Ups
  10 Movement You Suck At
```
