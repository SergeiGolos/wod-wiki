## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile | bike 1/2 mile | row 250m.

3 rounds of:
- 10 Air Squats
- 10 Push-Ups
- 10 Sit-Ups
- 10 Air Squats
- 10 Pull-Ups | jumping pull-ups

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

```wod
(3)
  25 Air Squats
  25 Jumping Pull Ups
  25 Sit Ups
```

### Avancée — Advanced

```wod
(5)
  25 Air Squats
  25 Jumping Pull Ups
  25 Sit Ups
```

### Traceur — Expert

```wod
(5)
  50 Air Squats
  50 Jumping Pull Ups
  50 Sit Ups
```

## Cool Down

Tabata push-ups.
