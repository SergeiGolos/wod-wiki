# ZombieFit.org Apr 2011

Welcome back to the concrete jungle! April programming focused heavily on building endurance and integrating parkour movements into classic CrossFit conditioning. Expect a mix of high-intensity circuit training, long-distance cardio challenges, and explosive plyometrics designed to test your adaptability in urban environments. Remember to respect the warmups—they are crucial for preparing your joints for these demanding movements!

***

### 🗓️ Weekly Training Overview

**Week 1 (April 11th):** Focus on foundational strength endurance with a classic "21-15-9" format, incorporating plyometrics and core work.
**Week 2 (April 13th):** A cardio challenge week featuring long-distance runs/rows/bikes to build aerobic capacity, paired with high-rep bodyweight circuits.
**Week 3 (April 29th):** Emphasis on raw movement efficiency. The WOD requires mastering the forward quadrupedal crawl over a set distance, demanding core stability and power.
**Week 4 (April 30th):** A brutal finisher month! This session tests pure grit with varying burpee formats, scaling difficulty from basic reps to complex pull-up variations.

***

### 💪 Featured Workouts

#### WOD 368 (Apr 11)
A challenging metabolic circuit testing your ability to maintain form under fatigue. The workout follows the classic 21-15-9 structure:
*   **Movement:** Box Jumps / Push Ups / Sit Ups
*   **Focus:** Plyometrics and Core Strength

#### WOD 369 (Apr 13)
A pure endurance test designed to push your cardiovascular limits. Choose your preferred cardio modality for the distance requirement.
*   **Cardio Options:** Run 1 mile OR Row 2000m OR Bike 3 miles
*   **Focus:** Aerobic Capacity

#### WOD 381 (Apr 29)
This session demands functional, ground-based movement. The core challenge is maintaining speed and efficiency while moving in a quadrupedal fashion over the required distance.
*   **Movement:** Forward Quadrupedal Crawl
*   **Focus:** Functional Strength & Core Stability

#### WOD 382 (Apr 30)
The ultimate test of grit! This workout scales burpees to match your current fitness level, ensuring a brutal but manageable challenge for all athletes.
*   **Movement:** Burpees
*   **Levels:** Étudiant (50 reps), Avancée (100 reps), Traceur (Burpee Pull-ups)
*   **Focus:** Muscular Endurance & Mental Toughness
