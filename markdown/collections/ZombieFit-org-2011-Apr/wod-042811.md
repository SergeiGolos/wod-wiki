## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile OR bike 1/2 mile OR row 250m.

```wod
(3)
  10 Air Squats
  10 Push Ups
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Air Squats
  10 Pull Ups
```

May substitute pull-ups with jumping pull-ups if needed.

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

Choose ONE movement and perform 100 repetitions for time: push ups, sit ups, jumping pull ups, or air squats.

```wod
100 Push Ups
100 Sit Ups
100 Jumping Pull Ups
100 Air Squats
```

### Avancée — Advanced

Choose ONE movement and perform 100 repetitions for time: push ups, sit ups, kipping pull ups, or air squats.
If you finish in under 10:00, rest 3:00 then complete 100 repetitions of one of the other movements. If you do not finish in under 10:00, perform 50 repetitions of two of the other movements.

```wod
100 Push Ups
100 Sit Ups
100 Kipping Pull Ups
100 Air Squats
```

### Traceur — Expert

Choose ONE movement and perform 100 repetitions for time: push ups, sit ups, kipping pull ups, or air squats.
If you finish in under 10:00, rest 3:00 then complete 100 repetitions of one of the other movements. Attempt to complete 100 reps of all four movements if possible.

```wod
100 Push Ups
100 Sit Ups
100 Kipping Pull Ups
100 Air Squats
```

## Cool Down

```wod
(3)
  100m Sprint
```
