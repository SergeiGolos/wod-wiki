## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile | bike 1/2 miles | row 500m.

```wod
(3)
  10 Pull Ups
  10 Push Ups
  10 Parkour Squats
  10 Sit Ups
```

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

```wod
50 Burpees
```

### Avancée — Advanced

```wod
100 Burpees
```

### Traceur — Expert

```wod
100 Burpee Pull Ups
```

If you do not have a pull up bar available, perform a "precision burpee" instead. A precision burpee adds a short broad jump to the end of each burpee — instead of jumping up to grab the pull-up bar, jump forward 5 to 6 feet.

## Cool Down

```wod
(3)
  50m Reverse Quadrupedal Movement
```
