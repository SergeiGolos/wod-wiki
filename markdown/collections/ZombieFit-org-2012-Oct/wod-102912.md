## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile | row 250m | bike 1/2 miles.

3 rounds of:
- 25m quadrupedal movement
- 10 Push Ups
- 10 Pull Ups | Jumping Pull Ups
- 10 Movement you suck at (see note)

Perform a movement that you suck at. If it is already in the warm up, perform 15 repetitions of that movement instead of 10. For instance, if you suck at sit ups and there are sit ups in the warm up, do 3 rounds of 15 instead of 10. If you suck at handstand push ups, perform 3 rounds of 10 handstand push ups (or an appropriate progression) and add that to the warm up.
