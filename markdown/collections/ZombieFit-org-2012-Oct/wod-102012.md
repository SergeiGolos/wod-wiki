## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile OR row 250m OR bike 1/2 mile.

```wod
(3)
  10 Air Squats
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Pull Ups
```

You may substitute jumping pull-ups for regular pull-ups.

## WOD

For each movement perform as many repetitions as possible in the given time. Rest 1 minute between each movement.

### Étudiant — Beginner

```wod
1:00 Push Ups
:60 Rest
1:00 Air Squats
:60 Rest
1:00 Sit Ups
:60 Rest
1:00 Jumping Jacks
:60 Rest
1:00 Tuck Jumps
```

### Avancée — Advanced
