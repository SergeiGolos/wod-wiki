## Warm Up

Jog 1/4 mile OR row 250m OR bike 1/2 mile.

```wod
(3)
  25m Quadrupedal Movement
  10 Jumping Jacks
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Pull Ups / Jumping Pull Ups
```
