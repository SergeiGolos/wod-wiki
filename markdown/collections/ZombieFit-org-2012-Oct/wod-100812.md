## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile OR bike 1/2 miles OR row 250m followed by:

```wod
(3)
  50m Quadrupedal Movement
  10 Air Squats
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Jumping Pull Ups // sub Push Ups if no pull-up bar
```

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner
Stand in place and mark the spot where your feet are. Then find a spot (either visually or by marking it) approximately 4 feet away. That spot is where you’ll be precisioning to for the above WOD.

```wod
Precision Ladder — With a continuously running clock do 1 Precision the 1st minute, 2 Precisions the 2nd minute, 3 Precisions the 3rd minute, and continue increasing each minute as long as you are able. Use as many sets each minute as needed.
```

### Avancée — Advanced
Pick a precision length of 85% of your max broad jump. If possible precision over a gap or up to a ledge.

```wod
Precision Ladder — With a continuously running clock do 1 Precision the 1st minute, 2 Precisions the 2nd minute, 3 Precisions the 3rd minute, and continue increasing each minute as long as you are able. Use as many sets each minute as needed.
```

### Traceur — Expert
Max effort every precision. If possible, precision over a gap or to a ledge.

```wod
Precision Ladder — With a continuously running clock do 1 Precision the 1st minute, 2 Precisions the 2nd minute, 3 Precisions the 3rd minute, and continue increasing each minute as long as you are able. Use as many sets each minute as needed.
```
