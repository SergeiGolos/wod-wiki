## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile OR row 250m OR bike 1/2 miles.

```wod
(3)
  25m Quadrupedal Movement
  10 Push Ups
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Pull Ups / Jumping Pull Ups
  10 Movement You Suck At
```

Perform a movement that you "suck at." If that movement is already in the warm up, perform 15 repetitions of that movement instead of 10. For example, if you suck at sit ups and sit ups are in the warm up, do 3 rounds of 15 instead of 3 rounds of 10. If you suck at handstand push ups, perform 3 rounds of 10 handstand push ups (or an appropriate progression) and add that to the warm up.

## WOD

For time, complete 21-15-9 repetitions of:

### Étudiant — Beginner

Perform the following movements for time:

```wod
(21-15-9)
  18 inch Box Jumps
  Jumping Pull Ups
```

### Avancée — Advanced

Perform the following movements for time:

```wod
(21-15-9)
  24 inch Box Jumps
  Pull Ups
```

### Traceur — Expert

Perform the following movements for time:

```wod
(21-15-9)
  24 inch Box Jumps
  Pull Ups
  Double Unders
```

If you do not have a box available, perform parkour squats (tuck jumps) instead.

## Cool Down

Hold a plank position to failure twice.
