## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile OR row 250m OR bike 1/2 mile.

```wod
(3)
  10 Pull Ups
  10 Air Squats
  10 Push Ups
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Dips
```

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

Perform the following for time:

```wod
10 Burpees
2 Five-Foot Precisions
8 Burpees
4 Five-Foot Precisions
6 Burpees
6 Five-Foot Precisions
4 Burpees
8 Five-Foot Precisions
2 Burpees
10 Five-Foot Precisions
```

### Avancée — Advanced

Same sequence with an added 12-set and use 6-foot precisions:

```wod
12 Burpees
2 Six-Foot Precisions
10 Burpees
4 Six-Foot Precisions
8 Burpees
6 Six-Foot Precisions
6 Burpees
8 Six-Foot Precisions
4 Burpees
10 Six-Foot Precisions
2 Burpees
12 Six-Foot Precisions
```

### Traceur — Expert

Same as Avancée, using 7-foot precisions:

```wod
12 Burpees
2 Seven-Foot Precisions
10 Burpees
4 Seven-Foot Precisions
8 Burpees
6 Seven-Foot Precisions
6 Burpees
8 Seven-Foot Precisions
4 Burpees
10 Seven-Foot Precisions
2 Burpees
12 Seven-Foot Precisions
```

## Cool Down

```wod
50 Push Ups
```
