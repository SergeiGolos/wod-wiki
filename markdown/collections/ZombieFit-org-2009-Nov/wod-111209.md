## Warm Up

Great class tonight!! Thanks to everyone who came out!

Run 1/4 mile or bike 2 miles or row 500m followed by:

```wod
(3)
  50m Quadrupedal Movement
  10 Air Squats
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Pull Ups
```

(You may sub Pull Ups with Push Ups if you don’t have a pull-up bar.)

## WOD
