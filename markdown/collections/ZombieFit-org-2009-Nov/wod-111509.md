## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile or bike 2 miles or row 500m followed by:

3 rounds of:
- 10 air squats
- 10 sit ups
- 10 pull ups

May substitute pull ups with push ups if you don’t have a pull up bar.

```wod
(3)
  10 Air Squats
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Pull Ups
```

## WOD

## Cool Down

Concentrate on your form. If possible do them free-standing.

```wod
10 Handstand Push Ups
```
