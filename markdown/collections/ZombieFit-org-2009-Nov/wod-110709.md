## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile | bike 2 miles | row 500m followed by:

```wod
(3)
  50m Quadrupedal Movement
  10 Air Squats
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Jumping Pull Ups
```

(May sub jumping pull ups with push ups if you don’t have a pull up bar.)

## WOD

```wod

```
