## Warm Up

```wod
Run 1/4mile | Bike 2mile | Row 500m
(3)
  50m Quadrupedal Movement
  10 Air Squats
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Jumping Pull Ups
```

## WOD

Must be completed in 11 minutes.
