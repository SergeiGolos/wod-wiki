## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile | bike 2 miles | row 500m.

```wod
(3)
  50m Quadrupedal Movement
  10 Air Squats
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Jumping Pull Ups
```

You may substitute jumping pull-ups with push-ups if you don't have a pull-up bar.

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

```wod
300 Air Squats
```

### Avancée — Advanced

```wod
500 Air Squats
```

### Traceur — Expert

```wod
600 Air Squats
```
