# ZombieFit.org Nov 2009

Welcome to the November collection! This month's programming focuses heavily on functional movement and endurance, blending classic CrossFit movements with advanced parkour skills. Expect a mix of timed efforts, ladder challenges, and foundational strength work designed to keep you moving like the undead.

## Monthly Overview

This month provides a progressive challenge, starting with structured circuit training (WOD 13) and progressing through distance-based endurance tests (WOD 14), classic rep schemes (WOD 15), specialized skill ladders (WOD 16), and targeted strength circuits (WOD 17).

### Week Summary

*   **Week of Nov 2:** Focus on foundational circuit work, incorporating ground kongs and jumping pull-ups.
*   **Week of Nov 3:** Dedicated to pure endurance and movement efficiency with the 400m quadrupedal challenge.
*   **Week of Nov 4:** Classic strength conditioning using a challenging 21-15-9 rep scheme (Push ups, Air squats, Sit ups).
*   **Week of Nov 6:** Skill development week featuring the Precision Ladder for continuous effort.
*   **Week of Nov 7:** Targeted circuit training emphasizing core and upper body endurance.

***

## Featured Workouts

### WOD 13 (Nov 2)
A three-round circuit focusing on ground movement, squats, and pulling strength. Levels provided for differentiation.

```wod
(3)
  50m ground kongs; 25 air squats; 25 jumping pull ups
```

### WOD 14 (Nov 3)
An all-levels endurance test requiring sustained forward quadrupedal movement.

```wod
400m Forward Quadrupedal Movement For Time
```

### WOD 15 (Nov 4)
A classic rep scheme challenge testing overall muscular endurance. Levels provided for differentiation.

```wod
(21-15-9) Push ups / Air squats / Sit ups
```

### WOD 16 (Nov 6)
The Precision Ladder requires continuous effort over a set time, demanding consistent pacing and skill execution.

```wod
Precision Ladder For Time
```

### WOD 17 (Nov 7)
A targeted circuit for upper body and core strength, presented in timed sets. Levels provided for differentiation.

```wod
[Sets] Push ups / [Other Movements] For Time
```
