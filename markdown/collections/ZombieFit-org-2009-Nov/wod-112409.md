## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile or bike 2 miles or row 500m followed by:

```wod
(3)
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Push Ups
  10 Air Squats
  10 Pull Ups / Handstand Push Ups
```

## WOD

Warm up the back squat.

### Étudiant — Beginner

4 sets of 5 repetitions of back squats. Concentrate on your form!

```wod
(4)
  5 Back Squats
```

### Avancée — Advanced

5, 5, 3, 3, 1, 1, 1. Work towards a max effort lift.

```wod
5 Back Squats
5 Back Squats
3 Back Squats
3 Back Squats
1 Back Squat
1 Back Squat
1 Back Squat ?lb
```

### Traceur — Expert

Same progression; work to a max single.

```wod
5 Back Squats
5 Back Squats
3 Back Squats
3 Back Squats
1 Back Squat
1 Back Squat
1 Back Squat ?lb
```
