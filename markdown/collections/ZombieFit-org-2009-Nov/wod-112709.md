## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile or bike 2 miles or row 500m followed by:

```wod
(3)
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Push Ups
  10 Air Squats
  10 Pull Ups or Handstand Push Ups
```

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

Tabata protocol: 8 rounds of 20s work, 10s rest for each movement.

```wod
(8) :20 Tabata
  Air Squats
(8) :20 Tabata
  Push Ups
```

### Avancée — Advanced

Tabata protocol: 8 rounds of 20s work, 10s rest for each movement.

```wod
(8) :20 Tabata
  Air Squats
(8) :20 Tabata
  Push Ups
(8) :20 Tabata
  Sit Ups
(8) :20 Tabata
  Pull Ups
```
