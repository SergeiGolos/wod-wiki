## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile | bike 1/2 mile | row 250m followed by:

```wod
(3)
  50m Quadrupedal Movement
  10 Air Squats
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Jumping Pull Ups
```

May substitute jumping pull-ups with push-ups if you don't have a pull-up bar.

## WOD

Today we are performing burpee pull ups. This exercise is exactly what it sounds like: a burpee with a pull up in it. To complete a burpee you finish with a jump while clapping your hands overhead — in this exercise replace the clap with a pull up. After the pull up, drop down into the push-up position and start the next burpee.

### Étudiant — Beginner

```wod
(30)
  1 Burpee
  1 Jumping Pull Up
```

### Avancée — Advanced

```wod
30 Burpee Pull Ups
```

### Traceur — Expert

```wod
50 Burpee Pull Ups
```

## Cool Down

```wod
50 Sit Ups
```
