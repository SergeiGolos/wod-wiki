## WOD

### Étudiant

*This is NOT a conditioning WOD. Focus on form.*

**Press Progression:**
Perform 50 repetitions of the press using a broomstick or pvc pipe.

**Push Press Progression:**
Perform 50 repetitions of the push press using a broomstick or pvc pipe. (Ensure dip depth is about 1/5 squat depth.)

**Push Jerk Progression:**
Perform 50 repetitions of the push jerk using a broomstick or pvc pipe. (Focus on the second dip under the bar.)

*Instructions: Pause after each rep and correct form.*

### Avancée

*Familiarity with all three movements is required. Keep weight very light if spotting is needed.*

**Shoulder Press Sets:**
5, 5, 3, 3, 1, 1, 1 sets

**Push Press Sets:**
5, 5, 3, 3, 1, 1, 1 sets

**Push Jerk Sets:**
5, 5, 3, 3, 1, 1, 1 sets

### Traceur

*Work towards your one rep max.*

**Shoulder Press Sets:**
5, 5, 3, 3, 1, 1, 1 sets

**Push Press Sets:**
5, 5, 3, 3, 1, 1, 1 sets

**Push Jerk Sets:**
5, 5, 3, 3, 1, 1, 1 sets
