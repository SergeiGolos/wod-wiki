## Warm Up

Warm up options:
*   Run 1/4 mile
*   Bike 2 miles
*   Row 500m

Followed by:
(3) rounds of:
*   10 Air Squats
*   10 Sit Ups
*   10 Pullups (May substitute with Pushups if no pull up bar is available)

## WOD

### Étudiant

```wod
(5)
  15 Lunges
  5 Burpees
```

### Avancée

```wod
(5)
  15 Lunges
  5 Burpee Pullups
```

### Traceur

```wod
(5)
  15 Lunges
  10 Burpee Pullups
```

## Cool Down

Three 100m sprints with 2 minutes rest in between each run.
