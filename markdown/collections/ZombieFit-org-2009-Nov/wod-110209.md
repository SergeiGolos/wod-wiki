## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile | bike 2 miles | row 500m followed by:

```wod
(3)
  10 Pull Ups
  10 Push Ups
  10 Air Squats
  10 Sit Ups
```

## WOD

Three rounds for time. Must be completed in 9 minutes.

### Étudiant — Beginner

```wod
(3) 9:00
  50m Ground Kongs
  25 Air Squats
  25 Jumping Pull Ups
```

### Avancée — Advanced

```wod
(3) 9:00
  50m Ground Kongs
  50 Air Squats
  25 Pull Ups
```

### Traceur — Expert

```wod
(3) 9:00
  50m Ground Kongs
  50 Air Squats
  25 Pull Ups
```

## Cool Down

```wod
(3)
  100m Sprint
  2:00 Rest
```
