## Warm Up

Warm up options include running 1/4 mile, biking 2 miles, or rowing 500m. This is followed by a circuit of 3 rounds:

```wod
(3)
  10 Pullups
  10 Pushups
  10 Air Squats
  10 Sit Ups
```

## WOD

WOD 13 is structured as three rounds for time, with a goal completion time of 9 minutes.

### Étudiant

```wod
(3)
  50m Ground Kongs
  25 Air Squats
  25 Jumping Pullups
```

### Avancée

```wod
(3)
  50m Ground Kongs
  50 Air Squats
  25 Pullups
```

### Traceur

```wod
(3)
  50m Ground Kongs
  50 Air Squats
  25 Pullups
```

## Cool Down

Sprint 100m three times, resting two minutes between each sprint.
