## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile | bike 2 miles | row 500m.

3 rounds of:
- 10 Sit Ups
- 10 Push Ups
- 10 Air Squats
- 10 Pull Ups | Handstand Push Ups

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

Perform the following movements for time:

```wod
(10-8-6-4-2-4-6-8-10)
  Burpees
  Sit Ups
```

### Avancée — Advanced

Perform the following movements for time:

```wod
(10-8-6-4-2-4-6-8-10)
  Burpees
  Sit Ups
  Double Unders
```

### Traceur — Expert

Perform the following movements for time:

```wod
(10-8-6-4-2-4-6-8-10)
  Burpee Pull Ups
  Sit Ups
  Double Unders
```

## Cool Down

100 m walking lunges.
