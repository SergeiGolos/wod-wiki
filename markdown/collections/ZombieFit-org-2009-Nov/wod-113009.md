## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile or bike 2 miles or row 500m followed by:

3 rounds of:
- 10 Sit Ups
- 10 Push Ups
- 10 Air Squats
- 10 Pull Ups (or Handstand Push Ups)

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

```wod
(21-15-9)
  12 inch Box Jumps
  Push Ups
  Sit Ups
```

### Avancée — Advanced

```wod
(21-15-9)
  24 inch Box Jumps
  Push Ups
  Elbows to Knees
```

### Traceur — Expert

```wod
(21-15-9)
  36 inch Box Jumps
  Push Ups
  Elbows to Knees
```

If needed, substitute parkour squats for box jumps.

## Cool Down

100m quadrupedal movement.
