## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile OR bike 2 miles OR row 500m followed by:

```wod
(3)
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Push Ups
  10 Air Squats
  10 Pull Ups or Handstand Push Ups
```

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

21-15-9 for time of lunges and push ups with 50m quadrupedal movement between rounds.

```wod
(21-15-9)
  Lunges
  Push Ups
  50m Quadrupedal Movement
```

### Avancée — Advanced

21-15-9 for time of pistols (alternating each leg) and push ups with 100m quadrupedal movement between rounds.

```wod
(21-15-9)
  Pistols (alternating each leg)
  Push Ups
  100m Quadrupedal Movement
```

### Traceur — Expert

21-15-9 for time of pistols (no alternating — complete reps on one leg per set) and push ups with 100m quadrupedal movement between rounds.

```wod
(21-15-9)
  Pistols // no alternating
  Push Ups
  100m Quadrupedal Movement
```
