## WOD

### Étudiant

```wod
(21-15-9)
  Lunge & Push Up ?
  50m Quadrupedal Movement ?
```

*Note: The movement is performed in sets of 21 reps, followed by 50m quadrupedal movement. Then 15 reps + 50m qm. Finally 9 reps + 50m qm.*

### Avancée

```wod
(21-15-9)
  Pistol Squats ?
  100m Quadrupedal Movement ?
```

*Note: Pistol squats are alternating each leg. The movement is performed in sets of 21 reps, followed by 100m quadrupedal movement. Then 15 reps + 100m qm. Finally 9 reps + 100m qm.*

### Traceur

```wod
(21-15-9)
  Pistol Squats ?
  100m Quadrupedal Movement ?
```

*Note: Pistol squats are performed non-alternating (i.e., 21 reps with the left leg, then 21 reps with the right leg). The movement is performed in sets of 21 reps, followed by 100m quadrupedal movement. Then 15 reps + 100m qm. Finally 9 reps + 100m qm.*
