## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile | bike 2 miles | row 500m followed by:

```wod
(3)
  50m Quadrupedal Movement
  10 Jumping Jacks
  5 Burpees
```

## WOD

Warm up the deadlift movement. Start with the bar/broomstick/pvc pipe and concentrate on form. Remember to use your legs on the pull. Push through the ground while activating your hips.

### Étudiant — Beginner

Continue to use only the bar/broomstick/pvc pipe. Concentrate on form. If you feel comfortable, use very light weight to practice. Do not perform any repetitions at a weight you cannot do at least 5 times.

```wod
(10)
  5 Deadlift // bar / broomstick / PVC
```

### Avancée — Advanced

You should be familiar with this movement. After warming up, perform 5 sets of 10 deadlifts. Try to increase the weight each set.

```wod
(5)
  10 Deadlift
```

### Traceur — Expert

You should be familiar with this movement. After warming up, perform 5 sets of 10 deadlifts. Try to increase the weight each set.

```wod
(5)
  10 Deadlift
```

## Cool Down

```wod
(2)
  Handstand Push Ups to Failure
```
