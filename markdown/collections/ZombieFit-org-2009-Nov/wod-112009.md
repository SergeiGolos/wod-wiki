## Warm Up

Warm up options: 1/4 mile Run, 2 miles Bike, or 500m Row.

Followed by:
```wod
(3)
  50m Quadrupedal Movement
  10 Jumping Jacks
  5 Burpees
```

## WOD

WOD 27 - Deadlift Focus

*Note: Concentrate on form, using the bar/broomstick/pvc pipe. Remember to use your legs and push through the ground while activating your hips.*

### Étudiant

Perform 10 sets of 5 reps, concentrating on form. Use very light weight to practice. Do not perform any repetitions at a weight you cannot do at least 5 times.

### Avancée

Perform 5 sets of 10 deadlifts. Try to increase the weight each set.

```wod
(5)
  10 Deadlift ?lb
```

### Traceur

*Instructions for Traceur were combined with Avancée.*

## Cool Down

2 sets of Handstand Push Ups to failure.
