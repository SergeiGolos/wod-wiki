## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile | bike 2 miles | row 500m followed by:

```wod
(3)
  50m Quadrupedal Movement
  10 Jumping Jacks
  5 Burpees
```

## WOD

Tabata This: 20 seconds work / 10 seconds rest, 8 sets per exercise. No rest between exercises. Keep track of all repetitions; total reps across all sets/exercises is your score.

### Étudiant — Beginner

Complete a half Tabata (4 sets per exercise).

```wod
(4) :20
  Air Squats
(4) :20
  Push Ups
(4) :20
  Sit Ups
(4) :20
  Pull Ups
Total Reps ?
```

### Avancée — Advanced

Complete the full Tabata (8 sets per exercise). Substitutions allowed (e.g., jumping pull-ups, knee push-ups).

```wod
(8) :20
  Air Squats
(8) :20
  Push Ups
(8) :20
  Sit Ups
(8) :20
  Pull Ups
Total Reps ?
```

### Traceur — Expert

Complete the full Tabata (8 sets per exercise) using strict form and full range of motion for every movement.

```wod
(8) :20
  Air Squats
(8) :20
  Push Ups
(8) :20
  Sit Ups
(8) :20
  Pull Ups
Total Reps ?
```
