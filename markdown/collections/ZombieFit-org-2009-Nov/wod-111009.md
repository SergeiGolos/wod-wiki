## WOD

WOD 19 is a drill circuit based on American Parkour drills. Athletes must choose the required number of exercises and perform 1 set of 10 reps for each chosen movement.

### Étudiant
Choose 5 exercises from the provided list. Perform 1 set of 10 reps for each chosen exercise.

### Avancée
Choose 7 exercises from the provided list. Perform 1 set of 10 reps for each chosen exercise.

### Traceur
Choose 10 exercises from the provided list. Perform 1 set of 10 reps for each chosen exercise.

*Note: The full list of drills can be reviewed at http://www.americanparkour.com/content/category/5/25/386/*
