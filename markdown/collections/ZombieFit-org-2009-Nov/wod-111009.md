## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile or bike 2 miles or row 500m followed by:

```wod
(3)
  50m Quadrupedal Movement
  10 Air Squats
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Jumping Pull Ups or 10 Push Ups
```

## WOD

We are going to use a great list of drills for this WOD from APK (American Parkour). Review the list for exercises and ideas: http://www.americanparkour.com/content/category/5/25/386/

### Étudiant — Beginner

Choose 5 of the APK exercises and do 1 set of 10 reps for each exercise.

```wod
(5)
  10 Selected APK Parkour Drill
```

### Avancée — Advanced

Choose 7 of the APK exercises and do 1 set of 10 reps for each exercise.

```wod
(7)
  10 Selected APK Parkour Drill
```

### Traceur — Expert

Choose 10 of the APK exercises and do 1 set of 10 reps for each exercise.

```wod
(10)
  10 Selected APK Parkour Drill
```
