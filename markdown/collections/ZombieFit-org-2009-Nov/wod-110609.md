## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile or bike 2 miles or row 500m followed by:

```wod
(3)
  50m Quadrupedal Movement
  10 Air Squats
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Jumping Pull Ups (may sub with Push Ups)
```

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner
Stand in place and mark the spot where your feet are. Then find a spot approximately 4 feet away. That spot is where you’ll be precisioning to for the below ladder.

```wod
:60 EMOM
  + Precision Ladder — 1 precision the 1st minute, 2 precisions the 2nd minute, 3 precisions the 3rd minute… continue as long as you are able. Use as many sets each minute as needed.
```

### Avancée — Advanced
Pick a precision length of ~85% of your max broad jump. If possible, precision over a gap or up to a ledge.

```wod
:60 EMOM
  + Precision Ladder — 1 precision the 1st minute, 2 precisions the 2nd minute, 3 precisions the 3rd minute… continue as long as you are able. Use precision distance = 85% max broad jump; use gap or ledge if available.
```

### Traceur — Expert
Max effort every precision. If possible, precision over a gap or to a ledge.

```wod
:60 EMOM
  + Precision Ladder — 1 precision the 1st minute, 2 precisions the 2nd minute, 3 precisions the 3rd minute… continue as long as you are able. Max effort every precision; use gap or ledge if possible.
```
