## WOD

WOD 16 - Precision Ladder
Perform a precision ladder continuously. Complete 1 precision in the first minute, 2 precisions in the second minute, 3 precisions in the third minute, and so on, continuing until failure.

### Étudiant

Stand in place and mark the spot where your feet are. Find a target spot approximately 4 feet away (visually or by marking). Precision to this marked spot for the duration of the WOD.

### Avancée

Pick a precision length of 85% of your max broad jump. If possible, precision over a gap or up to a ledge.

### Traceur

Max effort every precision. If possible, precision over a gap or to a ledge.

```wod
(Ladder) Continuous until failure:
  1 Precision (Minute 1)
  2 Precisions (Minute 2)
  3 Precisions (Minute 3)
  4 Precisions (Minute 4)
  5 Precisions (Minute 5)
  ... continue increasing reps per minute ...
```
