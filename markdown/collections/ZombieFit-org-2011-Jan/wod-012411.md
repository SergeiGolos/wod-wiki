## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile | row 250m | bike 1/2 mile.

```wod
(3)
  25m Quadrupedal Movement
  10 Push Ups
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Pull Ups | Jumping Pull Ups
```

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

```wod
(20) :60 EMOM
  + 5 Push Ups
  + 5 Sit Ups
  + 5 Air Squats
```

### Avancée — Advanced

```wod
(5) :60 EMOM
  + 5 Push Ups
  + 5 Sit Ups
  + 5 Air Squats
(5) :60 EMOM
  + 10 Push Ups
  + 5 Sit Ups
  + 5 Air Squats
(5) :60 EMOM
  + 10 Push Ups
  + 10 Sit Ups
  + 5 Air Squats
(5) :60 EMOM
  + 10 Push Ups
  + 10 Sit Ups
  + 10 Air Squats
```

### Traceur — Expert

Same as Avancée, with 2 burpees after each minute's work.

```wod
(5) :60 EMOM
  + 5 Push Ups
  + 5 Sit Ups
  + 5 Air Squats
  + 2 Burpees
(5) :60 EMOM
  + 10 Push Ups
  + 5 Sit Ups
  + 5 Air Squats
  + 2 Burpees
(5) :60 EMOM
  + 10 Push Ups
  + 10 Sit Ups
  + 5 Air Squats
  + 2 Burpees
(5) :60 EMOM
  + 10 Push Ups
  + 10 Sit Ups
  + 10 Air Squats
  + 2 Burpees
```

If you cannot complete all movements within the minute timeframe, stop the exercise, complete 2 burpees, and move on to the next minute’s repetitions.
