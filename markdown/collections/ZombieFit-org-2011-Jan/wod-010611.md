## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile OR row 250m OR bike 1/2 mile.

```wod
(3)
  25m Quadrupedal Movement
  5 Burpees
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Jumping Pull Ups
```

## WOD

ZombieFit Benchmark WOD — All Levels

Perform as many consecutive repetitions as possible on each movement; go until failure on each movement before immediately moving to the next.

Start with push-ups and do as many consecutive push-ups as possible. If your knees touch the ground, or you pause for more than 2 breaths, you’re done with push-ups and must immediately move to sit-ups. During sit-ups, if consecutive movement stops then you have reached failure and must move on to pull-ups. If you’re performing dead-hang or kipping pull-ups you are done when you come off the bar. If you choose jumping pull-ups you are finished when you stop consecutive movement.

When doing the five-foot precisions, mark off a five-foot area and jump from one end, turn around, and jump to the other. Switch turning directions periodically. You are done with this movement when you stop with consecutive repetitions.

```wod
Push Ups
Sit Ups
Pull Ups (may sub with Jumping Pull Ups)
Five-Foot Precisions
Total Reps ?
```
