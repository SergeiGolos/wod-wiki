## Warm Up

```wod
(3)
  10 Air Squats
  10 Push Ups
  10 Sit Ups
  50ft Quadrupedal Movement
```

## WOD

For time, complete 5 rounds of the following:

### Étudiant — Beginner

```wod
(5)
  10 Burpees
  10 Five-Foot Precisions
  10 Jumping Pull Ups
```

### Avancée — Advanced

```wod
(5)
  10 Burpees
  10 Six-Foot Precisions
  10 Pull Ups
```

### Traceur — Expert

```wod
(5)
  10 Pull-Up Burpees
  10 Six-Foot Precisions
  10 Handstand Push Ups
```
