## Warm Up

I hope everyone has a happy and safe New Years celebration!

Run 1/4 mile | row 250m | bike 1 mile.

3 rounds of:
- 10 Air Squats
- 10 Push Ups
- 10 Sit Ups
- 10 Pull Ups | Jumping Pull Ups
- 10 "isuckatthis" (perform a movement that you suck at)

If the movement you suck at is already in the warm up, perform 15 repetitions of that movement instead of 10. For instance, if you suck at sit ups and sit ups are in the warm up, do 3 rounds of 15 instead of 3 rounds of 10. If you suck at handstand push ups, perform 3 rounds of 10 handstand push ups (or an appropriate progression) and add that to the warm up.

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

Complete for time.

```wod
25 Air Squats
5m Quadrupedal Movement
25 Sit Ups
5m Quadrupedal Movement
25 Push Ups
```

### Avancée — Advanced

Complete for time.

```wod
35 Tuck Jumps
10m Quadrupedal Movement
35 Sit Ups
10m Quadrupedal Movement
35 Push Ups
```

### Traceur — Expert

Complete for time.

```wod
50 Pistols (25 each leg)
50 Sit Ups
  10s Plank after each rep
50 Push Ups
50 Dips
```
