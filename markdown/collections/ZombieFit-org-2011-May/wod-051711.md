## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile | row 250m | bike 1/2 miles.

```wod
(3)
  10 Air Squats
  10 Push Ups
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Pull Ups
```

You may substitute jumping pull-ups for regular pull-ups.
