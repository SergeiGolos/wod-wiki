## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile | row 250m | bike 1/2 miles.

3 rounds of:
- 10 air squats
- 10 push ups
- 10 sit ups
- 10 pull ups (you may sub jumping pull ups for regular pull ups)

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

```wod
(5)
  10 Burpees
  10 Push Ups
```

### Avancée — Advanced

```wod
(5)
  10 Burpees
  15 Push Ups
```

### Traceur — Expert

```wod
(10)
  5 Burpees
  5 Handstand Push Ups
```
