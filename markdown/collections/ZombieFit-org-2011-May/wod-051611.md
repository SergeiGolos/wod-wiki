## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile OR row 250m OR bike 1/2 mile.

```wod
(3)
  10 Air Squats
  10 Push Ups
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Pull Ups / Jumping Pull Ups
```

## WOD

For time, perform the following movements:

### Étudiant — Beginner

```wod
100m Backwards Sprint
1:00 Front-Lean and Rest
100m Backwards Sprint
10 Burpees
100m Backwards Sprint
10 Ground Kongs
100m Sprint
10 Burpees
100m Sprint
1:00 Front-Lean and Rest
100m Sprint
```

### Avancée — Advanced

```wod
100m Backwards Sprint
1:30 Front-Lean and Rest
100m Backwards Sprint
20 Burpees
100m Backwards Sprint
20 Ground Kongs
100m Sprint
20 Burpees
100m Sprint
1:30 Front-Lean and Rest
100m Sprint
```

### Traceur — Expert

```wod
100m Backwards Sprint
2:00 Front-Lean and Rest
100m Backwards Sprint
30 Burpees
100m Backwards Sprint
30 Ground Kongs
100m Sprint
30 Burpees
100m Sprint
2:00 Front-Lean and Rest
100m Sprint
```

*Front-lean: This is the push-up position fully extended. Your back, butt and stomach are all flat. Keep your arms underneath your shoulders. Push through the floor.*
