## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile OR row 250m OR bike 1/2 miles.

```wod
(3)
  10 Push Ups
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Air Squats
  10 Pull Ups or Jumping Pull Ups
```

## WOD

On the minute, for at least 15 minutes and no longer than 20 minutes, complete the prescribed movements each minute. If you fail to complete the repetitions in a minute before the 15:00 mark, stop the clock, perform 2 burpees, then restart the clock and continue the movements until you have attempted at least 15 minutes. If you fail to complete the movements in the 1-minute window after 15:00, that is your last round.

### Étudiant — Beginner

```wod
(20) :60 EMOM
  + 3 Push Ups
  + 5 Air Squats
  + 7 Jumping Jacks
```

### Avancée — Advanced

```wod
(20) :60 EMOM
  + 3 Pull Ups or 7 Jumping Pull Ups
  + 5 Push Ups
  + 7 Air Squats
```

### Traceur — Expert

```wod
(20) :60 EMOM
  + 5 Pull Ups
  + 7 Push Ups
  + 9 Air Squats
```
