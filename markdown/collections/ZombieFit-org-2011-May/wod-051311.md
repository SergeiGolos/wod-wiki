## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile OR row 250m OR bike 1/2 mile, then:

```wod
(3)
  25m Quadrupedal Movement
  15 Jumping Jacks
  15 Push Ups
  15 Air Squats
```

## WOD

For time, complete the following:

### Étudiant — Beginner

```wod
(3)
  200m Sprint
  50 Alternating Leg Lunges (25 each leg)
```

### Avancée — Advanced

```wod
(5)
  200m Sprint
  50 Alternating Leg Lunges (25 each leg)
```

### Traceur — Expert

```wod
(5)
  300m Sprint
  60 Alternating Leg Lunges (30 each leg)
```
