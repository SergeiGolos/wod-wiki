## Warm Up

```wod
(3)
  50 Jumping Jacks
  100m Jog
  50m Sprint
```

## WOD

Then practice your parkour roll. If you have no training with a PK roll, or need some help with your roll, check out our tutorial. Perform at least 50 rolls. The PK roll is a critical safety and movement skill — spend time learning it properly before taking your training outside.

### Étudiant — Beginner

```wod
10:00 AMRAP
  1 Parkour Roll
  3 Push Ups
  6 Air Squats
  9 Sit Ups
```

### Avancée — Advanced

```wod
15:00 AMRAP
  2 Parkour Rolls
  3 Pull Ups
  6 Push Ups
  9 Air Squats
```

### Traceur — Expert

```wod
20:00 AMRAP
  2 Parkour Rolls // alternate sides
  5 Pull Ups
  10 Push Ups
  15 Air Squats
```

Now, when performing this WOD, TAKE YOUR TIME WHEN PERFORMING THE PK ROLL. Concentrate on your form and technique, then bust through the rest of the round. Parkour rolls can be dangerous if not done correctly. Do not flip over your neck or over the center of your back. If ANY part of the roll hurts you you are doing it wrong. Stop the timer, and practice the roll until you can perform it pain free.

## Cool Down

If you have experience with this movement, go outside and pick a spot to do these repetitions. If not, just pick a point on a flat surface to jump to. These jumps should be at 65% of your max broad jump.

```wod
50 Precision Jumps
```
