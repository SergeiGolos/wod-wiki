## Warm Up

3 rounds of:
- 25m quadrupedal movement
- 10 push-ups
- 10 sit-ups
- 10 air squats
- 25 jumping jacks

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

```wod
(21-15-9)
  Five-Foot Precisions
  Jumping Pull Ups
```

### Avancée — Advanced

```wod
(21-15-9)
  Six-Foot Precisions
  Pull Ups (kipping or dead hang)
```

### Traceur — Expert

```wod
(21-15-9)
  Six-Foot Precisions
  Double Unders
  Pull Ups (kipping or dead hang)
```

## Post

Post your time to the comments.

For these precisions, make sure that you are concentrating on form. This is a metabolic conditioning workout, but don’t let your precision form and technique go to crap. Get full extension on your jump, pick up your knees, spot your landing and land quietly on the balls of your feet. ABSORB the impact.
