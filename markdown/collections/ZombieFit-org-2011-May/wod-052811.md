## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile OR row 250m OR bike 1/2 miles.

```wod
(3)
  10 Air Squats
  10 Push Ups
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Pull Ups | Jumping Pull Ups
  10 Movement You Suck At
```

**Perform a movement that you suck at.** If it is already in the warm up, then perform 15 repetitions of that movement instead of 10. For instance, if you suck at sit ups and there are sit ups in the warm up, do 3 rounds of 15 instead of 3 rounds of 10. If you suck at handstand push ups, perform 3 rounds of 10 handstand push ups (or an appropriate progression) and add that to the warm up.

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

For time: alternating burpees and five-foot precisions with the rep ladder 10-2-8-4-6-6-4-8-2-10.

```wod
(10-2-8-4-6-6-4-8-2-10)
  Burpees
  Five-Foot Precisions
```

### Avancée — Advanced

Same as above with an added 12-rep set and using six-foot precisions.

```wod
(12-2-10-8-4-6-6-4-8-2-10-12)
  Burpees
  Six-Foot Precisions
```

### Traceur — Expert

Same as Avancée, but perform pull-up burpees instead of regular burpees.

```wod
(12-2-10-8-4-6-6-4-8-2-10-12)
  Pull-Up Burpees
  Six-Foot Precisions
```

## Cool Down

```wod
50 Push Ups
```

Perform in as few sets as possible.
