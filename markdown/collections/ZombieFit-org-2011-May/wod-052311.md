## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile | row 250m | bike 1/2 mile.

3 rounds of:
- 25m quadrupedal movement
- 10 push ups
- 10 sit ups
- 10 pull ups | jumping pull ups

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

```wod
20 Push Ups
40 Jumping Jacks
20 Sit Ups
40 Jumping Jacks
20 Air Squats
40 Jumping Jacks
20 Jumping Pull Ups
1:00 Rest
400m Sprint
```

### Avancée — Advanced

```wod
25 Push Ups
25 Jumping Pull Ups
25 Sit Ups
25 Jumping Pull Ups
25 Air Squats
25 Jumping Pull Ups
25 Five-Foot Precisions
25 Jumping Pull Ups
1:00 Rest
400m Sprint
```

### Traceur — Expert

```wod
25 Push Ups
25 Pistols Left Leg

25 Pistols Right Leg
25 Pull Ups
25 Knees to Elbows

```
