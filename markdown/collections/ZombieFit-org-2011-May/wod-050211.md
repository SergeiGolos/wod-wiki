## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile OR bike 1/2 miles OR row 500m followed by:

```wod
(3)
  10 Air Squats
  10 Push Ups
  10 Jumping Jacks
  100m Sprint
```

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

On the minute for 20 minutes. If you do not complete the repetitions in the minute, stop the clock and perform 2 Burpees, then restart the clock and continue.

```wod
(20) :60 EMOM
  + 2 Push Ups
  + 4 Air Squats
  + 6 Jumping Jacks
  If you do not complete the repetitions in the minute, stop the clock and perform 2 Burpees, then restart the clock and continue.
```

### Avancée — Advanced

On the minute for 20 minutes. If you do not complete the repetitions in the minute, stop the clock and perform 2 Burpees, then restart the clock and continue.

```wod
(20) :60 EMOM
  + 5 Push Ups
  + 7 Air Squats
  + 9 Jumping Jacks
  If you do not complete the repetitions in the minute, stop the clock and perform 2 Burpees, then restart the clock and continue.
```

### Traceur — Expert

On the minute for 20 minutes. If you do not complete the repetitions in the minute, stop the clock and perform 5 Burpees, then restart the clock and continue.

```wod
(20) :60 EMOM
  + 2 Burpees
  + 5 Push Ups
  + 7 Air Squats
  + 9 Jumping Jacks
  If you do not complete the repetitions in the minute, stop the clock and perform 5 Burpees, then restart the clock and continue.
```
