## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile OR row 250m OR bike 1/2 mile.

```wod
(3)
  10 Air Squats
  10 Push Ups
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Pull Ups (or Jumping Pull Ups)
```

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

```wod
(5)
  25m Quadrupedal Movement
  25 Jumping Jacks
  5 Five-Foot Precisions
```

### Avancée — Advanced

```wod
(5)
  50m Quadrupedal Movement
  5 Burpees
  10 Five-Foot Precisions
```

### Traceur — Expert

```wod
(5)
  50m Quadrupedal Movement
  10 Burpees
  10 Six-Foot Precisions
```

## Cool Down

```wod
100 Repetitions (choose one: Push Ups OR Air Squats OR Sit Ups OR Pull Ups) — go to failure on every set
```
