## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile | bike 2 miles | row 250m followed by:

```wod
3 rounds
  10 Push Ups
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Pull Ups
  10 Air Squats
```

May substitute pull-ups with jumping pull-ups.

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

```wod
(8) :20 Tabata
  Air Squats
(8) :20 Tabata
  Push Ups
```

### Avancée — Advanced

```wod
(8) :20 Tabata
  Air Squats
(8) :20 Tabata
  Push Ups
(8) :20 Tabata
  Sit Ups
(8) :20 Tabata
  Pull Ups
```

### Traceur — Expert

There is no rest in-between sets.

```wod
(8) :20 Tabata
  Air Squats
(8) :20 Tabata
  Push Ups
(8) :20 Tabata
  Sit Ups
(8) :20 Tabata
  Pull Ups
(8) :20 Tabata
  Five-Foot Precisions
```
