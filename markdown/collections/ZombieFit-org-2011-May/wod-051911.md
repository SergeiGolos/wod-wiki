## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile OR row 250m OR bike 1/2 mile.

```wod
(3)
  10 Air Squats
  10 Push Ups
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Pull Ups
```

You may substitute jumping pull ups for regular pull ups.

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

Make sure your knee touches the ground on every lunge.

```wod
Lunge Walk 200m
Lunge Walk Backwards 100m
```

### Avancée — Advanced

Make sure your knee touches the ground on every lunge.

```wod
Lunge Walk 300m
Lunge Walk Backwards 200m
```

### Traceur — Expert

Make sure your knee touches the ground on every lunge.

```wod
Lunge Walk 400m
Lunge Walk Backwards 300m
```

## Cool Down

```wod
(2)
  100m Sprint
```
