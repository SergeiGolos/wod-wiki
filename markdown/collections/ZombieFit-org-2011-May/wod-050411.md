## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile | bike 1/2 mile | row 500m.

```wod
(3)
  10 Air Squats
  10 Push Ups
  10 Jumping Jacks
  100m Sprint
```

## WOD

21-15-9 repetitions for time.

### Étudiant — Beginner

Push ups, sit ups, and air squats. At the end of the entire workout do static holds for 15 seconds.

```wod
(21-15-9)
  Push Ups
  Sit Ups
  Air Squats
15s Plank
15s Squat Hold
15s V Hold
```

### Avancée — Advanced

Push ups, sit ups, burpees, and squats. Static hold at the end of the entire workout for 30 seconds.

```wod
(21-15-9)
  Push Ups
  Sit Ups
  Burpees
  Squats
30s Plank
30s Squat Hold
30s V Hold
```

### Traceur — Expert

Push ups, sit ups, burpees, and pull ups. Static hold at the end of each set for 45 seconds: plank, squat sit, V hold for sit ups, and hold arms at 90 degree angle on the bar for pull ups.

```wod
(21-15-9)
  Push Ups
  Sit Ups
  Burpees
  Pull Ups
  45s Plank
  45s Squat Hold
  45s V Hold
  45s Hang // arms at 90 deg on bar
```
