## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile or bike 1/2 miles or row 500m followed by:

3 rounds for time of:
- 10 Air Squats
- 10 Push Ups
- 10 Jumping Jacks
- 100m Sprint

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

```wod
(20) :60 EMOM
  + 2 Push Ups
  + 4 Air Squats
  + 6 Jumping Jacks
  - 2 Burpees // if reps not completed in the minute
```

### Avancée — Advanced

```wod
(20) :60 EMOM
  + 5 Push Ups
  + 7 Air Squats
  + 9 Jumping Jacks
  - 2 Burpees // if reps not completed in the minute
```

### Traceur — Expert

```wod
(20) :60 EMOM
  + 2 Burpees
  + 5 Push Ups
  + 7 Air Squats
  + 9 Jumping Jacks
  - 5 Burpees // if reps not completed in the minute
```
