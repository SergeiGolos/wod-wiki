## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile OR row 250m OR bike 1/2 mile.

```wod
(3)
  25m Quadrupedal Movement
  10 Push Ups
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Pull Ups or Jumping Pull Ups
  10 Isuckatthis // movement you suck at
```

Perform a movement that you suck at. If it is already in the warm up, perform 15 repetitions of that movement instead of 10. For instance, if you suck at sit ups and they are in the warm up, do 3 rounds of 15 instead of 3 rounds of 10. If you suck at handstand push ups, perform 3 rounds of 10 handstand push ups (or their progression) and add that to the warm up.
