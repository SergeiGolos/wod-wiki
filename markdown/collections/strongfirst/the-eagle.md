## Workout 4: The Eagle

**Category**: StrongFirst Strength Endurance  
**Type**: For Time  
**Difficulty**: Advanced

### Description

The Eagle is a challenging benchmark workout that tests both strength endurance and mental fortitude. This workout combines pressing strength with pulling power, creating a balanced upper-body challenge that reveals weaknesses in pressing endurance, pulling capacity, or grip strength.

The workout consists of a series of clean and press complexes paired with pull-ups, performed for time. The volume is significant, requiring practitioners to maintain technique and efficiency throughout. Success in The Eagle requires not only raw strength but also the ability to pace oneself and maintain composure under fatigue.

```wod
(5)
  5 Clean & Press 24kg
  5 Pullups

(5)
  4 Clean & Press 24kg
  4 Pullups

(5)
  3 Clean & Press 24kg
  3 Pullups

(5)
  2 Clean & Press 24kg
  2 Pullups

(5)
  1 Clean & Press 24kg
  1 Pullup
```

### Breakdown

- **Rounds/Duration**: 5 rounds at each rep scheme
- **Exercises**: Clean & Press, Pull-ups
- **Goal**: Complete all reps as fast as possible with good form

### Collectible Metrics

- Total time to completion
- Quality maintained throughout
