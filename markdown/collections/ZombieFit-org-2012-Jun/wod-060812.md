## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

Max effort for 30 seconds per exercise. No rest between exercises. 2:00 rest between rounds. Count total reps for all exercises and rounds.

```wod
(3)
  :30 Push Ups
  :30 Sit Ups
  :30 Air Squats
  :30 Jumping Pull Ups
  2:00 Rest
Total Reps ?
```
