## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile | row 250m | bike 0.5 miles.

3 rounds of:
- 25m quadrupedal movement
- 10 push-ups
- 10 pull-ups | jumping pull-ups
- 10 "a movement you suck at"

Perform a movement that you suck at. If it is already in the warm up, perform 15 repetitions of that movement instead of 10. For instance, if you suck at sit-ups and sit-ups are in the warm up, do 3 rounds of 15 instead of 3 rounds of 10. If you suck at handstand push-ups, perform 3 rounds of 10 handstand push-ups (or a scaled progression) and add that to the warm up.

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

```wod
(3)
  10 Burpees
  10 Push Ups
  10 Sit Ups
  25m Quadrupedal Movement
```

### Avancée — Advanced

```

### Traceur — Expert

  15 Push Ups
  15 Sit Ups
  50m Quadrupedal Movement
```
