## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile OR bike 1/2 mile OR row 500m.

3 rounds of:
- 10 Sit Ups
- 10 Jumping Pull Ups
- 10 Dips

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

Three rounds for time:

```wod
(3)
  100m Sprint
  15 Air Squats
  5 Burpees
```

### Avancée — Advanced

Three rounds for time:

```wod
(3)
  100m Sprint
  25 Air Squats
  10 Burpees
```

### Traceur — Expert

Five rounds for time:

```wod
(5)
  100m Sprint
  25 Air Squats
  10 Burpees
```

*May sub the sprints for 200m row.*
