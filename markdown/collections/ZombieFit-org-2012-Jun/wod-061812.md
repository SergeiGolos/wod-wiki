## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile OR row 250m OR bike 2 miles.

```wod
(3)
  25m Quadrupedal Movement
  10 Push Ups
  10 Pull Ups / Jumping Pull Ups
  10 Perform a movement you suck at
```

Perform a movement that you "suck at." If that movement is already in the warm up, do 15 repetitions of it instead of 10. For example, if you suck at sit ups and there are sit ups in the warm up, do 3 rounds of 15 instead of 3 rounds of 10. If you suck at handstand push ups, perform 3 rounds of 10 handstand push ups (or an appropriate progression) and add that to the warm up.

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

Perform the following movements for time:

```wod
(21-15-9)
  Burpees
  Push Ups
```

### Avancée — Advanced

Hold a handstand against a wall for 20 seconds between each round (after the sets of 21, 15 and 9).

```wod
(21-15-9)
  Burpees
  Push Ups
  :20 Handstand Against Wall
```

### Traceur — Expert

```wod
(21-15-9)
  Burpees
  Handstand Push Ups
```

This WOD requires maintaining proper form on the movements. Do not collapse in your burpees, maintain full range of motion on your push ups, and touch your forehead to the ground on the handstand push ups.
