## Warm Up

Run 1/4 mile | row 250m | bike 1/2 mile.

May substitute pull-ups with jumping pull-ups.

```wod
(3)
  10 Push Ups
  10 Sit Ups
  10 Air Squats
  10 Pull Ups
```

## WOD

### Étudiant — Beginner

```wod
(15) :60 EMOM
  + 3 Push Ups
  + 5 Air Squats
  + 7 Jumping Jacks
```

### Avancée — Advanced

If performing jumping pull-ups, do 7 jumping pull-ups instead of 3 pull-ups.

```wod
(15) :60 EMOM
  + 3 Pull Ups
  + 5 Push Ups
  + 7 Air Squats
```

### Traceur — Expert

```wod
(15) :60 EMOM
  + 5 Pull Ups
  + 7 Push Ups
  + 9 Air Squats
```
