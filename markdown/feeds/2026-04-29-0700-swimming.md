# Speed & Sprint — 2200m Track Set

**Source:** SwimSwam Masters Series  
**Feed:** swimming  
**Type:** Speed / Anaerobic  
**Distance:** 2200m  
**Duration:** ~45–55 minutes

## Workout

**Warm-Up — 600m**
- 200m easy freestyle
- 4 × 50m on 1:00 (descend pace: easy → moderate)
- 200m IM order (fly/back/breast/free 50m each)

**Main Set — 1200m**
- 12 × 100m on 1:45
  - 1–4: aerobic pace (comfortable, sustainable)
  - 5–8: threshold pace (push, slightly uncomfortable)
  - 9–12: sprint effort (all-out on odd, recovery pace on even)

**Kick Set — 200m**
- 4 × 50m kick on 1:30
  - Alternate flutter kick / breaststroke kick

**Cool-Down — 200m**
- 200m easy backstroke

## WOD Syntax

```wod
(Warmup)
  200m Swim
  (4) 1:00
    50m Swim
  200m IM

(12) 1:45
  100m Swim

(4) 1:30
  50m Kick

(Cooldown)
  200m Backstroke
```

## Notes

The "race-pace surge" reps (9, 11) should be at your 100m race pace or better. Full rest on the even reps is intentional — quality over quantity. Track your best splits for benchmark comparison.
