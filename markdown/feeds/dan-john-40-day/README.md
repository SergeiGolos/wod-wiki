# Dan John 40-Day Program

**Author**: Dan John  
**Type**: Barbell Strength — 40-Day Program  
**Level**: All levels  
**Reference**: *Can You Go?* — Dan John (2014)

---

## Overview

The 40-Day Program is one of the simplest and most effective barbell programs Dan John has written. Train 5 days per week for 40 sessions. Pick one lift per day and do five sets of five. Add assistance work if you like, but the heavy lifting is done.

**The five lifts:**
- Front Squat or Goblet Squat
- Press (strict)
- Deadlift or Hip Hinge
- Bench Press or Floor Press
- Easy Strength movement (your choice)

Rotate through the five lifts across your training days. Keep weight the same for all five sets. When all five sets feel easy, add weight next session.

---

## Rules

1. Never miss a lift.  
2. Leave something in the tank — this is not a max-effort program.  
3. If you miss a day, resume where you left off. Don't try to catch up.

---

## Schedule

Workouts are published every two to three days. Follow them in order, regardless of the calendar date. The dates shown are suggested training days — adjust to fit your schedule.
