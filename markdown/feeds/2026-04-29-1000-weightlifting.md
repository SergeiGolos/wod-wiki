# 5/3/1 Boring But Big — Press Day (Week 1)

**Source:** Jim Wendler / 5/3/1 Program  
**Feed:** weightlifting  
**Type:** Strength + Hypertrophy  
**Duration:** 60–75 minutes

## Workout

**Warm-Up**
- Band pull-aparts: 3 × 20
- Face pulls: 3 × 15
- Light barbell overhead press: 2 × 10

**Main Sets — Overhead Press (5s Week)**

*Example Training Max: 165 lb*

| Set | % TM | Weight | Reps |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | 65% | 107 lb | 5 |
| 2 | 75% | 124 lb | 5 |
| 3 | 85% | 140 lb | **5+** (AMRAP) |

**BBB Assistance — Overhead Press**
- 5 × 10 @ 50% TM (~82 lb)
- Rest 90 sec between sets

**Accessory**
- Pull-Ups / Chin-Ups: 5 × 10
- Dips: 5 × 10

**Cool-Down**
- Pec stretch, shoulder capsule stretch, tricep stretch

## WOD Syntax

```wod
(Warmup)
  (3)
    20 Band Pull Apart
  (3)
    15 Face Pull

5 Overhead Press 107lb
5 Overhead Press 124lb
5 Overhead Press 140lb

(5)
  10 Overhead Press 82lb

(5)
  10 Pull-Up

(5)
  10 Dip
```

## Notes

The "+" on set 3 means go until form breaks — this AMRAP set drives your next cycle's Training Max. Log your rep count. Dips and chins are non-negotiable — they're what turn a pressing day into a complete upper-body session. The BBB sets feel light. That's intentional — they accumulate volume that builds the muscle underneath the strength.
