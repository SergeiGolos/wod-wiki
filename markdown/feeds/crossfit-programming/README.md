# CrossFit Programming

**Type**: Weekly CrossFit Programming  
**Style**: Conjugate / Varied  
**Level**: Intermediate–Advanced

---

## Overview

A weekly CrossFit program with daily workouts covering strength, conditioning, and skill work. Each week follows a push/pull/hinge/squat rotation with at least one benchmark or chipper per week.

Each day's session takes 60–75 minutes including warm-up.

---

## How to Use

Browse by date below. Click **Add to Today** to drop a workout into your journal for today's training, or **Plan for date** to schedule it for a future session.

---

## Schedule

- **Monday** — Lower strength + short conditioning
- **Wednesday** — Upper strength + midline
- **Friday** — Benchmark or long chipper
