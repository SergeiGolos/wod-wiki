# CrossFit WOD — "Fight Gone Bad"

**Source:** CrossFit.com Official Benchmark  
**Feed:** crossfit  
**Type:** Station Rotation / Max Reps  
**Time Domain:** 17 minutes (3 rounds × 5 min work + 1 min rest)

## Workout

**Warm-Up (8 min)**
- Row 500m easy
- 2 rounds:
  - 10 wall-ball shots (light)
  - 10 sumo deadlift high pulls (empty bar)
  - 10 box step-ups
  - 10 push presses (empty bar)

**Main Set**
3 rounds of 5-station rotation (1 min each, no rest between stations):

1. **Wall-Ball Shots** — ♀ 14 lb to 9 ft / ♂ 20 lb to 10 ft
2. **Sumo Deadlift High Pull** — ♀ 55 lb / ♂ 75 lb
3. **Box Jumps** — 20-inch box
4. **Push Press** — ♀ 55 lb / ♂ 75 lb
5. **Row** (calories)
6. **REST 1 minute**

**Score:** Total reps across all 3 rounds (calories count as reps on rower)

**Cool-Down**
- 5 min easy row or walk
- Hip flexor stretch, shoulder/lat stretch

## Scaling

| Level | Wall Ball | Barbell | Box |
|---|---|---|---|
| Rx | ♀ 14 lb / ♂ 20 lb | ♀ 55 lb / ♂ 75 lb | 20 in |
| Beginner | ♀ 6 lb / ♂ 10 lb | ♀ 35 lb / ♂ 45 lb | 12 in |

## WOD Syntax

```wod
(3)
  1:00 Wall Ball Shots 20lb
  1:00 Sumo Deadlift High Pull 75lb
  1:00 Box Jumps
  1:00 Push Press 75lb
  1:00 ? Row
  1:00 Rest
```

## Notes

Go hard round 1. Protect your strongest movements — capitalize on high-rep stations. SDHP is the most technique-dependent; prioritize form over speed to avoid shoulder impingement.
