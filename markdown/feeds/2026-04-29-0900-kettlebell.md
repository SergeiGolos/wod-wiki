# RKC Program Minimum — Monday Session

**Source:** Pavel Tsatsouline / Enter the Kettlebell  
**Feed:** kettlebell  
**Type:** Foundation Program  
**Duration:** 20–30 minutes

## Workout

**A. Two-Hand Kettlebell Swing**
- 3 sets × 15 reps
- Rest: 2 min between sets
- Focus: hinge from hips, not squat; protect the lower back with a neutral spine

**B. Turkish Get-Up (TGU)**
- 2 × 3 per side (6 total)
- Rest: as needed between reps
- Full stop at each stage — elbow, hand, tall sit, lunge, stand — then reverse

## Weights

| Level | Swing | TGU |
|---|---|---|
| ♀ Start | 8–12 kg | 8 kg |
| ♀ Goal | 16 kg | 16 kg |
| ♂ Start | 16 kg | 12–16 kg |
| ♂ Goal | 24 kg | 24 kg |

## 3-Month Progression

1. **Month 1:** Two-hand swing, partial TGU (to tall sit only)
2. **Month 2:** One-hand swing, full TGU with light weight
3. **Month 3:** One-hand swing heavier, full TGU to goal weight

## WOD Syntax

```wod
(3)
  15 KB Swing 24kg
  2:00 Rest

(2)
  3 Turkish Get-Up Left 16kg
  3 Turkish Get-Up Right 16kg
```

## Notes

This is the "bare minimum" — just two movements, 3 days per week. Do not add exercises. Pavel's reasoning: mastery of the swing and TGU builds a complete foundation of hip power, shoulder stability, and movement quality that makes everything else better. Add complexity only after mastering these.
