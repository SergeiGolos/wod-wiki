# Starting Strength — Workout A, Week 2

**Source:** Mark Rippetoe / Starting Strength  
**Feed:** weightlifting  
**Type:** Novice Linear Progression  
**Duration:** 45–60 minutes

## Workout

**Warm-Up**
- 5 min easy walk/row
- 2 rounds: 10 air squats, 10 hip hinges, arm circles

**Main Sets**

| Exercise | Sets × Reps | Example Weight |
|---|---|---|
| **Back Squat** | 3 × 5 | 155 lb |
| **Bench Press** | 3 × 5 | 115 lb |
| **Deadlift** | 1 × 5 | 185 lb |

Add weight warmup sets before each working weight (bar → 50% → 70% → working weight).

**Cool-Down**
- Hip flexor stretch, hamstring stretch, pec stretch

## Progression

- **Squat:** +5 lb every session
- **Bench/Press:** +5 lb every session
- **Deadlift:** +10–15 lb every session (novice recovery is fast)

## WOD Syntax

```wod
(Warmup)
  5:00 Walk
  (2)
    10 Air Squat
    10 Hip Hinge

(3)
  5 Back Squat 155lb

(3)
  5 Bench Press 115lb

5 Deadlift 185lb
```

## Notes

This is Workout A. Alternate with Workout B (Squat / Press / Power Clean) every other session. The deadlift is one working set — one is enough when you're adding 10+ lb per session. If form breaks down before the weight stalls, the weight is already too heavy.

The squat is king. Every session. No exceptions.
