# Simple & Sinister — Daily Practice

**Source:** Pavel Tsatsouline / StrongFirst  
**Feed:** kettlebell  
**Type:** Skill + Strength  
**Duration:** 30–40 minutes

## Workout

**Warm-Up**
- Prying Goblet Squat: 3 × 5 (light KB, pause at bottom, pry knees out)
- Hip Hinge / Deadlift Pattern: 2 × 5 (same light KB)

**Main Set A — One-Arm Swings**
- 10 sets × 10 reps
- Alternate arms each set (L/R/L/R...)
- Rest: ~1 min between sets (or by "talk test" — can you hold a conversation?)

**Main Set B — Turkish Get-Ups**
- 10 total reps (5 right / 5 left)
- One rep at a time — set the bell down between each
- Rest: ~1 min between reps
- Treat every rep as a max-effort movement, no rushing

## Weights

| Goal Level | Swings | TGU |
|---|---|---|
| Starting (♀) | 12 kg | 12 kg |
| Starting (♂) | 16 kg | 16 kg |
| "Simple" Standard (♀) | 16 kg | 16 kg |
| "Simple" Standard (♂) | 32 kg | 32 kg |

## Time Standards (Simple)

- 10 × 10 Swings: ≤ 5 minutes
- 5+5 TGUs: ≤ 10 minutes (each rep ≤ 1 min)

## WOD Syntax

```wod
(Warmup)
  (3)
    5 Goblet Squat 16kg
  (2)
    5 Hip Hinge

(10)
  5 KB Swing Left 32kg
  5 KB Swing Right 32kg
  1:00 Rest

(10)
  1 Turkish Get-Up ?lb
  1:00 Rest
```

## Notes

The hardstyle swing: sharp exhale at the top ("hiss"), full hip extension at lockout, power from hip snap — not arm pull. Move up in weight only when all sets are crisp AND within time standard.
