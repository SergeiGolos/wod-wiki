# Morning Energizing Flow

**Source:** Yoga Journal  
**Feed:** yoga  
**Type:** Vinyasa Flow — Energizing  
**Duration:** 15 minutes  
**Level:** All Levels

## Sequence

**Wake-Up (2 min)**
- Supine Belly Breath: on back, one hand on chest / one on belly, 5 breaths
- Knees-to-Chest — *Apanasana*: 30 sec, gentle rock side to side
- Happy Baby — *Ananda Balasana*: 1 min, grip outer feet, breathe into hips

**Transition to Hands & Knees (2 min)**
- Cat-Cow Flow: 1 min
- Thread-the-Needle (both sides): 30 sec each

**Building Heat (5 min)**
- Downward Dog → Plank → Cobra: 3 rounds (breath-linked)
- Low Lunge with Twist: 45 sec each side (open top arm to sky)
- Three-Legged Dog → step through → Warrior I: 2× each side

**Standing (3 min)**
- Chair Pose — *Utkatasana*: 30 sec hold × 2 (sit back, arms up)
- Standing Forward Fold → Half-Lift → Fold: 3 rounds (breath-linked)

**Cool Down (3 min)**
- Seated Spinal Twist: 1 min each side
- Savasana: 1 min (yes, even just 1 min matters)

## Notes

The goal of a morning flow is activation, not depth. Don't push range of motion — the body is cold. Focus on breath-movement synchronization: inhale to expand, exhale to fold or twist. The sequence builds from horizontal → hands & knees → standing to gradually load the spine and joints.
