# Rite of Passage — Press Ladder Day

**Source:** Pavel Tsatsouline / StrongFirst (Enter the Kettlebell)  
**Feed:** kettlebell  
**Type:** Strength — Ladders  
**Duration:** 25–45 minutes

## Workout

**Warm-Up**
- Arm bar: 2 min each side (shoulder prep)
- Halos: 2 × 10 each direction (light KB)
- 3 × 5 two-hand swings (working weight)

**Main Set — Clean + Press Ladders**

5 ladders of (1, 2, 3 reps):
```
Clean once → press 1 rep → set down
Clean once → press 2 reps → set down
Clean once → press 3 reps → set down
= 1 ladder
Rest 1–2 min, then repeat × 5
```

**Superset:** 1 pull-up (or ring row) per rung — matches press volume exactly.

**Swing Finisher**
- 5 × 10 one-hand swings (alternating, 5L/5R per set)
- On the minute

## Weights

| Level | Press KB | Pull-Up |
|---|---|---|
| ♀ Start | 8–12 kg | Banded or ring row |
| ♀ Goal | 16 kg | Strict bodyweight |
| ♂ Start | 12–16 kg | Banded |
| ♂ Goal | 32 kg ("Beast") | Weighted |

## Progression Logic

Add one rung per session when form is crisp. Progress: 1-2-3 ladders → 1-2-3-4 → 1-2-3-4-5. When 5-rung ladders feel easy, step up one bell size and restart at 1-2-3.

## WOD Syntax

```wod
(Warmup)
  (2)
    10 Halo 16kg
  (3)
    5 KB Swing 24kg

(5)
  1 Clean and Press Left 24kg
  1 Pull-Up
  1:00 Rest
  2 Clean and Press Left 24kg
  2 Pull-Ups
  1:00 Rest
  3 Clean and Press Left 24kg
  3 Pull-Ups
  2:00 Rest

(Finisher)
  (5) :60
    5 KB Swing Left 24kg
    5 KB Swing Right 24kg
```

## Notes

The clean is not a deadlift with a flip. Initiate from the hips, let the elbow guide the bell into the rack. Rack position: bell resting on forearm, elbow on hip shelf. Press is strict — no leg drive. The ladder structure means you never grind — always leave reps in reserve.
