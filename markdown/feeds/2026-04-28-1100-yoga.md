# Yoga for Complete Beginners — Foundation Flow

**Source:** Yoga with Adriene (Adriene Mishler)  
**Feed:** yoga  
**Type:** Hatha — Foundational  
**Duration:** 20 minutes  
**Level:** Beginner

## Sequence

**Centering (2 min)**
- Easy Pose — *Sukhasana*: seated, close eyes, 3 deep breaths to arrive

**Warm-Up (4 min)**
- Cat-Cow — *Marjaryasana/Bitilasana*: 1 min, inhale cow / exhale cat
- Child's Pose — *Balasana*: 1 min, arms extended, forehead to mat
- Downward-Facing Dog — *Adho Mukha Svanasana*: 1 min, pedal heels, breathe

**Standing (6 min)**
- Mountain Pose — *Tadasana*: 30 sec, feel your feet
- Forward Fold — *Uttanasana*: 1 min, soft knees, let head hang
- Low Lunge — *Anjaneyasana*: 45 sec each side
- Warrior I — *Virabhadrasana I*: 45 sec each side
- Warrior II — *Virabhadrasana II*: 45 sec each side

**Floor (5 min)**
- Seated Forward Fold — *Paschimottanasana*: 1 min, don't force
- Supine Twist — *Supta Matsyendrasana*: 1 min each side

**Rest (3 min)**
- Corpse Pose — *Savasana*: 3 min, complete stillness, breathe naturally

```wod
1:00 Supine Twist Right

```

## Notes

"Find What Feels Good" — Adriene's signature phrase applies here. There is no perfect pose, only the one you can breathe in. Use blocks under hands in forward fold if hamstrings are tight. Never pull yourself into a stretch — create space with the exhale.
