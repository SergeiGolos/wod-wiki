# CrossFit WOD — "Cindy"

**Source:** CrossFit.com Official Benchmark  
**Feed:** crossfit  
**Type:** AMRAP Bodyweight  
**Time Domain:** 20 minutes

## Workout

**Warm-Up (5 min)**
- 3 rounds:
  - 10 PVC pass-throughs
  - 5 inchworms
  - 5 strict pull-ups (or ring rows)
  - 10 push-ups

**Main Set**
- AMRAP 20 min:
  - 5 Pull-Ups
  - 10 Push-Ups
  - 15 Air Squats

**Cool-Down**
- 3 min easy walk
- Lat stretch, pec stretch, hip flexor

## Scaling

| Level | Pull-Ups | Push-Ups |
|---|---|---|
| Rx | Kipping pull-ups | Full push-ups |
| Intermediate | Banded pull-ups | Full push-ups |
| Beginner | Ring rows | Knee push-ups |

## WOD Syntax

```wod
20:00 AMRAP
  5 Pull-Ups
  10 Push-Ups
  15 Air Squats
```

## Notes

Classic benchmark — goal is to maintain a consistent pace. Break sets early (3+2 on pull-ups, 5+5 on push-ups) rather than failing reps. Push-ups are typically the limiting factor. Track your score to compare across cycles.

**Score:** Total rounds + reps
