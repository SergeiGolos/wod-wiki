# CrossFit WOD — "Helen"

**Source:** CrossFit.com Official Benchmark  
**Feed:** crossfit  
**Type:** 3 Rounds for Time  
**Time Domain:** Elite ~7 min | General 10–15 min

## Workout

**Warm-Up (6 min)**
- 400m easy jog
- 2 rounds:
  - 10 Russian KB swings (light)
  - 5 pull-ups or ring rows
  - 10 air squats

**Main Set**
3 rounds for time:
- **400m Run**
- **21 American KB Swings** — ♀ 35 lb (16 kg) / ♂ 53 lb (24 kg)
- **12 Pull-Ups**

**Cool-Down**
- 5 min easy walk
- Hamstring stretch, lat stretch, hip opener

## Scaling

| Level | KB | Pull-Ups | Run |
|---|---|---|---|
| Rx | ♀ 35 lb / ♂ 53 lb | Kipping | 400m |
| Intermediate | ♀ 26 lb / ♂ 35 lb | Russian swings + banded pull-ups | 400m |
| Beginner | ♀ 18 lb / ♂ 26 lb | Russian swings + ring rows | 200m |

## WOD Syntax

```wod
(3)
  400m Run
  21 KB Swings 53lb
  12 Pull-Ups
```

## Notes

Set a sustainable pace on the run — you need your grip for the KB swings. Swing lockout at the top matters: full hip extension, arms overhead, active shoulders. Kip on pull-ups to save grip and upper back for later rounds.
