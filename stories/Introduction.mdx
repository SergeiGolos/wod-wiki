  <h2 className="text-3xl font-bold mt-8 mb-4">Timer Language Features</h2>

  <ul className="list-disc list-inside space-y-2 mb-6">
    <li><strong>Headings</strong>: Use `#`, `##`, or `###` for different heading levels</li>
    <li><strong>Rounds</strong>: Use (n) where n is the number of rounds</li>
    <li><strong>Durations</strong>: Use standard time format `mm:ss` or `hh:mm:ss`</li>
    <li><strong>Weights</strong>: Use `@` followed by weight and optional unit (kg/lb)</li>
    <li><strong>Effort</strong>: Simply describe what you are doing in text (e.g., "Push ups", "Deadlifts", "Sun Saluations", etc.)</li>
  </ul>

  <h3 className="text-2xl font-semibold mt-6 mb-3">Tips for Writing Workouts</h3>

  <ol className="list-decimal list-inside space-y-2 mb-6">
    <li>Start with a clear heading describing the workout type</li>
    <li>Specify rounds or time domains upfront</li>
    <li>List movements with specific rep schemes</li>
    <li>Include weight/loading information where relevant</li>
    <li>Add notes about movement standards or scaling options</li>
  </ol>
